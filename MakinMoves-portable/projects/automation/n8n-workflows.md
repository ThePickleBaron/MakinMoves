# n8n Workflow Ideas for Passive Income

n8n is running at http://localhost:5678 (admin / changeme123)
It's a visual automation builder - think Zapier but free and self-hosted.

## Workflow Ideas

### Content Automation
1. **RSS to Social Media** - Auto-post blog articles to Twitter/LinkedIn
2. **Content Repurposer** - Take a blog post, generate social snippets
3. **Email Newsletter** - Weekly roundup of your content, auto-sent
4. **SEO Monitor** - Check rankings weekly, alert on changes

### Lead Generation
1. **Lead Magnet Delivery** - Form submission -> email PDF automatically
2. **Drip Email Sequence** - Welcome series for new subscribers
3. **Abandoned Cart Recovery** - If selling digital products

### Market Research
1. **Price Monitor** - Track competitor prices, alert on changes
2. **Trend Watcher** - Monitor Reddit/HN for niche keywords
3. **Review Aggregator** - Collect and analyze competitor reviews

### Trading
1. **Price Alerts** - Monitor crypto/stock prices, alert via Telegram/email
2. **Daily Market Summary** - Aggregate market data, send morning briefing
3. **Portfolio Tracker** - Daily P&L updates

### Revenue Tracking
1. **Sales Aggregator** - Pull sales from Gumroad/Etsy/Stripe into database
2. **Monthly Report** - Auto-generate revenue reports
3. **Goal Tracker** - Recovery progress notifications

## Getting Started
1. Open http://localhost:5678
2. Click "New Workflow"
3. Start with a simple one: Schedule Trigger -> HTTP Request -> Email
4. Build from there!
