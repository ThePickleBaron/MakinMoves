## COMMON QUESTIONS

**Q: Can I change the daily task order?**
A: No. Days 1–4 are sequential (niche → account → create → upload). Days 5–6 can overlap. Day 7 is final.

**Q: What if I finish a day early?**
A: Get ahead on next day's tasks. Overproduction is OK (extra templates, designs, or articles). Just don't compromise quality.

**Q: What if I'm behind schedule?**
A: Focus on P1 first (highest priority), then P2, then P3. You don't need 15 designs to launch; 5 is enough. 2 articles to launch; 1 is enough.

**Q: Can I use different platforms (not Gumroad/Redbubble)?**
A: Day 1 only. After niches locked, no platform changes. Consistency is important for Week 1.

**Q: What if a product gets negative feedback?**
A: Note feedback. Don't change until Week 2. Week 1 is launch validation, not optimization.

**Q: Do I need to respond to customer inquiries during Week 1?**
A: Yes. First customer = highest priority. Respond within 2 hours.

**Q: Can I ask CEO for help with content creation?**
A: No. That's not CEO's role. Ask CTMO for technical issues only (file format, tool problems).
