## FAQ FOR LISTING (Expandable Sections)

**Q: Is this a real Notion template or just instructions?**
A: This is a real, fully-functional Notion template. You get the complete template with all 5 databases, 18+ views, formulas, and sample data. Duplicate it to your Notion workspace in 2 minutes and start using it immediately.

**Q: Can I customize it?**
A: Yes, completely. Add fields, remove unused views, change colors, integrate with your tools—Notion is yours to modify.

**Q: Do I need Notion Pro?**
A: Notion Free is sufficient for this template. Notion Pro ($10/month) unlocks more views and unlimited database records if you scale to 500+ invoices.

**Q: Will this replace my accountant / bookkeeper?**
A: No. This tracks invoices and payments. You'll still need an accountant for taxes, expense categorization, and filings. But it makes their job easier.

**Q: How do I get the template after purchase?**
A: After checkout, you'll receive a download link to a `.notion` file. Open it in Notion, click "Duplicate," and it's yours.

**Q: Can I use this for my agency and share with my team?**
A: Yes. Duplicate the template into a shared Notion workspace. Invite team members with appropriate permissions.

**Q: What if I already have invoices in another system?**
A: You can import historical invoices via CSV into the Invoices database. The setup guide includes instructions.
