## Product Overview

### Problem
Freelancers lose track of invoices, clients, and who owes them money. They juggle spreadsheets, email, and memory, leading to lost revenue and chaos.

### Solution
One unified Notion template replacing Freshbooks ($20–30/month), Wave, spreadsheets, and email chaos. Costs $39 (one-time) instead of $240–360/year.

### Key Databases
1. **Clients** — Contact info, payment terms, rates, lifetime value (6 rollups)
2. **Projects** — Scope, timeline, budget, deliverables, hours tracked
3. **Invoices** — Create, send, track, with automated status and payment tracking
4. **Payments** — Log payments, reconcile, track cleared vs. pending
5. **Reminders** — Schedule payment reminders, client follow-ups, auto-trigger

### Key Features
- **Smart Formulas:** Days outstanding, budget remaining, amount due
- **Rollups:** Auto-calculate client lifetime value, project profit, invoice totals
- **Relations:** Connect clients → projects → invoices → payments (one unified system)
- **18+ Views:** By client, by status, timeline, overdue, dashboard, kanban, etc.
- **Dashboard:** Real-time revenue tracking (total, paid, outstanding, overdue)
