## THUMBNAIL/COVER IMAGE TEXT SUGGESTIONS
*(Creating a cover image for the product)*

### Cover Image Specs (CRITICAL)
**Dimensions:** 1280 × 720 px (16:9 widescreen)
**Thumbnail:** Auto-cropped square from center — **place ALL key text/graphics in center zone only**
**Conversion Lift:** Products with video/image covers convert 2× better than text-only

### Design Rules for Cover
- Large, bold title (visible in square crop — will be your thumbnail)
- NO vital text on left/right edges — will be cut off in square thumbnail
- Center all key messaging and graphics
- Products WITHOUT covers convert at baseline; adding covers = immediate 2× lift
- **Video covers perform even better than static images** (test for prompt pack first)
