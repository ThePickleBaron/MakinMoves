## Product Positioning

**Market:** AI-savvy freelancers (designers, developers, writers, consultants, coaches, strategists)

**Price Point:** $19-29 (recommended: $24 with "pay what you want" minimum $19)

**Value Proposition:** Land better clients, close bigger deals, spend less time on admin—using proven AI prompts instead of figuring it out yourself.

**Core Benefit:** 10-50 hours saved per month on business operations (prospecting, proposals, scope docs, communication, pricing) = more time on client work OR more capacity for new clients.

**Key Differentiators:**
- Battle-tested (used in real freelance businesses)
- Copy-paste ready (no learning curve)
- Organized by actual freelancer pain points
- Includes philosophy and customization guidance
- Not AI-generated fluff; each prompt has specific purpose and use case
