## Product #2: Freelancer Invoice + Client Tracker Notion Template

### Quick Facts
- **Price:** $39 (one-time)
- **Target:** Freelancers, solopreneurs, small agencies
- **Problem Solved:** Lost invoices, missed payments, revenue tracking chaos
- **Positioning:** "Freshbooks replacement without the monthly fee"
- **6-Month Revenue:** $13,650 (conservative estimate)

### What's Included
1. **5 interconnected Notion databases**
   - Clients (contact, payment terms, lifetime value)
   - Projects (scope, budget, deliverables, hours)
   - Invoices (create, send, track, status)
   - Payments (log payments, reconcile)
   - Reminders (payment reminders, follow-ups)

2. **18+ pre-built views** (by client, by status, overdue, timeline, etc.)
3. **Smart formulas & rollups** (days outstanding, budget remaining, lifetime value)
4. **Revenue dashboard** (total invoiced, paid, outstanding, overdue)
5. **Sample data** (realistic examples to learn from)

## Key Differentiation

### vs. Freshbooks ($20–30/mo)
- This: $39 one-time → 2-year break-even
- Freshbooks: $240–360/year (recurring)
- Advantage: One-time cost, fully customizable, you own the data

### vs. Wave (free)
- Wave is free but has limited features
- This template has project tracking, reminders, customization
- Advantage: Complete system in one place, better UX

### vs. Spreadsheets/Email
- This: Organized, searchable, integrated, professional
- Spreadsheets: Chaotic, fragile, no notifications
- Advantage: Looks professional, saves 5–8 hours/month on admin
