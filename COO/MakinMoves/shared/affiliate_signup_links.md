# Affiliate Program Signup Guide

Comprehensive guide to joining affiliate programs that complement the Active Trader Portfolio Tracker Notion template. Both programs pay recurring commissions, making them ideal passive income streams.

---

## 1. TradingView Affiliate Program

### Overview
TradingView is a popular charting and analysis platform for traders and investors. Their affiliate program offers recurring commissions on referrals who purchase paid subscriptions.

### Commission Structure
- **Commission Rate**: 30% recurring commission on every subscription purchase
- **Duration**: Earn as long as your referral continues to subscribe (recurring)
- **Referral Discount**: People you refer get a $15 discount coupon toward their new TradingView plan
- **Attribution Window**: 90 days to sign up + 90 days to subscribe
- **Payment Terms**: Paid within 30 days after the end of the month
- **Minimum Payout**: Check terms during signup

### Step-by-Step Signup (< 5 minutes)

1. Visit **https://www.tradingview.com/partner-program/**
2. Click "Become a Partner" or scroll to the signup section
3. Fill in required information:
   - Name and email
   - Website/platform where you'll promote (blog, Reddit, YouTube, etc.)
   - Referral strategy (briefly explain how you'll promote)
4. Agree to Partner Program Rules (https://www.tradingview.com/partner-rules/)
5. Submit application
6. Wait for approval email (typically within 24-48 hours)
7. Log in to partner dashboard to get:
   - Unique affiliate links
   - Promotional banners and graphics
   - Real-time tracking dashboard
   - Payment reports

### Important Restrictions
- **Paid advertising with brand keywords is prohibited** (no Google Ads with "TradingView", "tradingview.com", "trade view", etc.)
- Organic promotion only (Reddit, blog, email, social, etc.)
- Complies with platform-specific rules (e.g., Reddit's self-promotion limits)

### Best Use Case
Promote TradingView in your Reddit posts, blog, or email newsletters to dividend/stock investors who want better charting tools.

### Resources
- Partner Program: https://www.tradingview.com/partner-program/
- Partner Rules: https://www.tradingview.com/partner-rules/
- Partner Documentation: https://www.tv-hub.org/Documentation/AffiliateProgram

## 2. Sharesight Affiliate Program

### Overview
Sharesight is a portfolio tracking tool for self-directed investors who want detailed holdings tracking, dividend income analysis, and performance reporting. Ideal complement to the Notion template for investors wanting more automation.

### Commission Structure
- **Commission Rate**: Up to 50% commission for every new subscription
- **Duration**: Up to 12 months' worth of subscription fees (recurring, then drops to next tier or ends)
- **Payment**: Monthly via bank transfer
- **Promotional Materials**: Sharesight provides banners and images you can use
- **No Restrictions**: No paid advertising restrictions (unlike TradingView)

### Step-by-Step Signup (< 5 minutes)

1. Visit **https://www.sharesight.com/affiliates/** (or your regional variant like /us/affiliates/)
2. Click "Become an Affiliate" or the signup form
3. Fill in application form:
   - Name and email
   - Website, blog, or social media platform where you'll promote
   - Brief description of your audience (dividend investors, passive income builders, etc.)
   - Expected referral volume (optional but helpful)
4. Submit application
5. Sharesight team will follow up via email (typically within 24-48 hours)
6. Once approved, receive:
   - Unique affiliate link
   - Banner images and marketing materials
   - Access to affiliate dashboard with tracking
   - Payment details and reporting

### Best Use Case
Promote Sharesight as a complement to the Notion template for investors who want automated portfolio sync, tax reports, and dividend tracking from a dedicated platform.

### Referral Program Alternative
If you don't want to wait for affiliate approval, Sharesight also offers a basic referral program (https://www.sharesight.com/referral-terms-and-conditions/). Existing users get a credit per referral, lower commission but faster to access.

### Resources
- Affiliate Program: https://www.sharesight.com/affiliates/
- Become a Partner: https://www.sharesight.com/become-a-partner/
- Terms: https://www.sharesight.com/us/referral-terms-and-conditions/
