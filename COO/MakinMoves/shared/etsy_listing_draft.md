## Listing Description (High SEO + Engagement)

### Opening Hook
Organize your investment portfolio like a pro. This Notion template gives you a complete dashboard for tracking dividend stocks, monitoring performance, and projecting passive income — all in one database.

### What's Included

**Core Features:**
- Holdings Dashboard: symbol, shares, cost basis, current price, dividend yield, annual income
- Dividend Schedule: upcoming and past dividend payments with ex-dates and amounts
- Portfolio Performance Tracker: YTD dividend income, projections, growth analysis
- Sector & Allocation View: pie charts breaking down your portfolio by industry and company size
- Tax Report Helper: track gains, losses, and dividend income by year
- Watchlist: track dividend candidates before you buy
- Mobile-friendly: works on phone, tablet, and desktop

**Database Features:**
- Relations link holdings to dividend schedule (no duplicate data entry)
- Formulas calculate dividend yield, income projections, and portfolio metrics
- Filters and sorts let you view by sector, yield, or purchase date
- Charts and rollups show allocation and performance at a glance

### Who It's For
- Self-directed investors building dividend income streams
- FIRE/passive income enthusiasts who want to track progress
- Stock portfolio owners tired of spreadsheets
- Anyone wanting a centralized investment dashboard

### How It Works
1. Duplicate this template into your Notion workspace (takes 2 min)
2. Add your holdings (company, symbol, shares, cost basis, current price)
3. Template auto-calculates dividend yield, annual income, and portfolio allocation
4. Check your dashboard to see dividend payments, upcoming ex-dates, and income projections
5. Customize fields and views to match your investing strategy

### Customization
- Change sector categories to match your portfolio
- Add or remove columns as needed
- Color-code dividend stocks by strategy (growth, income, value, etc.)
- Integrate with portfolio sync tools like Sharesight or TradingView API (optional)

### Technical Requirements
- Notion account (free or paid)
- Basic Notion database knowledge (beginner-friendly, no coding required)
- Works with Notion Web and Notion iOS/Android apps

### Preview
You can preview the full template before buying to ensure it meets your needs.

### Support
Once you purchase, you receive:
- Full template with example data (ready to duplicate and customize)
- Setup guide with step-by-step instructions
- FAQ addressing common customization questions
- Email support for technical issues

### Why Buy vs. Build?
This template saves you 10+ hours of setup time. Formulas, database relations, and views are pre-configured. Just add your data and you're tracking.
