## 3. DIFFERENTIATION STRATEGIES OBSERVED

### Free Samples / Lead Magnets

**Winning Strategy:** Offer FREE high-quality teaser → PAID premium tier

**Example:** Think Scale AI
- Free Tier: "AI Prompt Engineering: Free Starter Guide" (4.9 stars, 224 reviews)
- Premium Tier: "All In One Booster AI Kit" ($147, 5.0 stars, 7 reviews)
- Funneling strategy: Build credibility with free, convert top 2-5% to $147 bundle

**For Us (MakinMoves):**
- Offer: Free 5-prompt sample AI pack ($0)
- Convert: Upsell to $24 full pack + Notion template
- Compound: Bundle all three at $49

### Testimonial Strategies (For Zero-Audience Launches)

**Observed:** Most top products show 1-7 reviews at launch success:
- Products with 5 stars + even 2-5 reviews rank high on Discover
- Review volume matters less than rating consistency
- Early reviews heavily influence algorithm visibility

**Zero-Audience Launch Tactics:**
1. **Email your network** (even 5-10 people) → Get initial reviews before launch
2. **Creator network activation** — Reach out to complementary creators, offer free access for review
3. **Beta testers** — Run closed beta, offer 50% off for feedback/review
4. **Waitlist** — Pre-register users before launch → Ask reviews on day 1

**Critical:** First 5-10 reviews are gold. Algorithm prioritizes recent 5-star reviews on Discover.

### Bundle vs. Individual Pricing Psychology

**Observed:**
- **Individual items:** $9.99-29.99 (most viable price point)
- **Bundles:** $49-160 (perceived value 2-3x individual cost)
- **Bundle conversion:** Higher AOV, lower unit volume

**Example:** Our positioning
- AI Prompts: $24 (individual)
- Notion Template: $39 (individual, TOO HIGH — should be $15-20)
- Bundle: $49 (35% discount = great perceived value)

**Competitor Bundles Seen:**
- Suno 3-pack: $9.99
- All In One Kit: $147 (10x+ value vs. individual prompts)
- Think Scale Ultimate: $160/year (lifetime access)

**Recommendation:** **Keep bundle at $49, reduce Notion to $19.99, keep AI Prompts at $24.**

## 4. GUMROAD DISCOVER MECHANICS

### How Products Get Featured

**Key Signals (Observed Algorithm Behavior):**

1. **Rating + Review Velocity**
   - Products with 4.9-5.0 stars + recent reviews rank higher
   - >100 reviews = "established"; 5-50 reviews = "rising"
   - Our products currently have 0 reviews (new) → Immediate rankings on Discover likely

2. **Search Terms & Tags**
   - "Freelancer" (search 185 results for "freelancer prompts")
   - "AI Prompts" (5,269 results — highly competitive)
   - "Notion Template" (19,557 results — extremely saturated)
   - **Our advantage:** Niche combination "Freelancer" + "AI Prompts" = less saturated

3. **Social Proof Cascade**
   - High ratings + reviews trigger Discover feature
   - Features drive new sales → More reviews → More features
   - First 5-10 reviews are CRITICAL for momentum

4. **Recently Added / Updated**
   - Newly listed products get Discover feature window
   - Updates reset the "new" flag
   - Window: 1-3 weeks of visibility before organic ranking takes over

5. **Creator Brand Momentum**
   - Established creators' new products get Discover priority
   - New creator with high-quality product: 5-10 days to prove ranking

### Tags That Perform Best

**Observed Tags (from competitor products):**
- "AI Prompts" (highly competitive)
- "Freelancer Tools" (medium competition, good fit)
- "Notion Template" (mega-competitive)
- "Business Tools" (competitive)
- "Digital Products" (catch-all)
- "Productivity" (competitive)

**Recommendation:** Tag with "Freelancer + AI," "Freelancer + Productivity," specific tools (Not generic "digital products")

### Rating Thresholds That Matter

- **4.5+ stars:** Eligible for Discover features
- **4.8+ stars:** High priority on Discover
- **5.0 stars:** Top priority (but small sample = less weight long-term)
- **50+ reviews:** "Proven" signal (breaks top 20% of category)
- **100+ reviews:** Established bestseller

**Current MakinMoves Products:** 0 reviews = complete unknown. First 10 reviews = CRITICAL.
