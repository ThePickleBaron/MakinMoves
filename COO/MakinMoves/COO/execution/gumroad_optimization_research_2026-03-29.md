## COVER IMAGE & THUMBNAIL STRATEGY

**Critical Specs:**
- **Dimensions:** 1280 × 720 px (16:9 widescreen)
- **Thumbnail:** Auto-cropped square from center — **place all key text/graphics in center zone only**
- **Conversion Lift:** Products with video/image covers convert 2× better than text-only

**Design Rules:**
- Large, bold title (visible in square crop)
- No vital text on left/right edges — will be cut off in thumbnail
- Products WITHOUT covers convert at baseline; adding covers = immediate 2× lift
- Video covers perform even better than static images

**Action:** Design both covers centered. Test video cover for the prompt pack first.

## PRICING PSYCHOLOGY

**Fixed vs. Pay-What-You-Want (PWYW):**
- No definitive conversion data found, but PWYW with $1+ minimum outperforms $0 floor
- **Key insight:** $1 minimum lowers psychological barrier ("cheap entry") then encourages higher tips
- For your price points ($24 / $39), stick with fixed pricing — PWYW works better for loss leaders

**Pricing Tiers Strategy:**
- Basic / Pro / Premium model (e.g., $29 / $49 / $99) drives 70% to middle tier
- **For templates:** $5–$15 low-cost entry converts best for beginners; bundle adds perceived value

**Recommendation for Your Products:**
- AI Prompt Pack: $24 fixed (premium niche, proven ask for AI templates)
- Notion Template: $39 fixed (proven pricing for Notion bundles)
- Consider $15 "lite" or starter version of Notion template to funnel beginners upmarket

## PRODUCT DESCRIPTION & CONVERSION

**What Works:**
- Lead with **benefits, not features** — how does it solve pain?
- Use scannable format: bullet points, clear sections, short benefit statements
- Include **preview content** (sample pages, screenshots) — significantly increases conversions
- Add **social proof**: testimonials from beta testers or early users

**For Prompt Pack:**
- Lead with: "Save X hours per project" or "Generate client-quality copy instantly"
- Show 2–3 sample prompts with before/after output
- Include: exact use cases, workflows, time savings

**For Notion Template:**
- Lead with: "Track invoices, get paid faster, 1-click reports"
- Show dashboard screenshots, sample invoice, report templates
- Include: automation setup time, export options, client visibility features

## EMAIL AUTOMATION (Gumroad Workflows)

**Setup (5 min):**
1. Trigger: "Purchase" → select product
2. Create automated sequence with delays (hours/days/weeks)
3. Each email has custom delay + content

**Proven Sequence (IMMEDIATE → 5 DAYS):**
- **0 hours:** Instant thank you + download link + quick-start guide link
- **1 day:** "Getting started" tips (1–2 wins they can achieve immediately)
- **3 days:** Case study or advanced use case
- **5 days:** Upsell related product OR exclusive offer (build future revenue)

**Action:** Build workflow for each product. Start with 3-email sequence minimum (thank you, quick-start, follow-up).
