### Key Findings:

1. **Competitor Landscape:** n8n template market is dominated by bulk packs (4,000–8,000 templates, $30–100). Low quality due to volume.

## Proposed Product #3 Specification (PRE-CRITIQUE)

### Scope: 8 Production-Ready Workflows

1. **Client Onboarding Automation** — Triggers when new client added, sends intake questionnaire, saves responses to database
2. **Invoice Follow-Up Drip** — Tracks invoice age, sends reminder sequences (Day 3, Day 7, Day 14)
3. **Cold Outreach Sequencing** — Multi-step drip campaign (initial email, follow-up, final touch)
4. **Proposal Automation** — Template generation based on project scope, sends via email with tracking
5. **Weekly Revenue Report** — Aggregates all invoices, payments, outstanding balances, emails summary every Friday
6. **Meeting Prep Brief** — Compiles client context (history, projects, recent communications) for pre-meeting review
7. **Project Status Update** — Auto-sends weekly status to clients with project progress + budget tracking
8. **Lead Alert Automation** — Monitors email/Slack for high-intent keywords, creates task in project management tool

### Pricing Strategy:
- **Standard:** $49 (single purchase, lifetime access)
- **Launch Window:** $39 (early-bird discount, April 5–12)
- **Bundle with Product #1:** $59 (both products | saves $4 vs. separate)

### Positioning:
"8 n8n workflows that save freelancers 5+ hours/week on repetitive client admin tasks. Built for service providers, not founders."
