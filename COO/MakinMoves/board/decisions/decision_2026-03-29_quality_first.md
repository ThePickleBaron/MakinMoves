## DIRECTIVE: RESEARCH BEFORE LAUNCH — EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY

We are NOT launching anything half-baked. Every product goes through a structured research and vetting cycle before it touches Gumroad. The board works fast — that's an advantage we use to go DEEPER, not just faster.

---

## New Rules

### 1. Standup Cadence: Every 15 Minutes
- Standups now filed every 15 minutes (was 30)
- Format: `board/standups/standup_YYYY-MM-DD_HHMM_ROLE.md`
- Status file still updated every 5 minutes

### 2. Product Research Mandate: Minimum 2 Hours Per Product
Every new product idea MUST go through a minimum 2-hour research and ideation cycle before any launch activity begins. This includes:

**Phase A — Market Analysis (30 min)**
- Competitive landscape: who sells this, at what price, with what reviews?
- Gap analysis: what are buyers complaining about in existing products?
- Search volume / demand signals: Reddit threads, Twitter discourse, Gumroad trending
- Target buyer persona: who exactly buys this and why?

**Phase B — Product Critique (30 min)**
- Internal board review: each role critiques the product from their lens
- COO: Is it executable and deliverable at quality?
- CTMO: Is the tech/format right? Can we differentiate technically?
- CFO: Does the pricing model hold up? What are the real margins?
- Document weaknesses, risks, and "why would someone NOT buy this?"

**Phase C — Refinement & Prototyping (45 min)**
- Incorporate critique into a revised product spec
- Build a prototype or detailed outline
- Test against buyer persona: would this person actually pay?
- Identify the ONE thing that makes this product better than alternatives

**Phase D — Launch Readiness Check (15 min)**
- Final sign-off from all roles
- Pricing confirmed with competitive data
- Listing copy refined with research insights
- Go/No-Go decision filed to `board/decisions/`

### 3. Domain Research Requirement
Each board role must conduct research into modern best practices for their function BEFORE contributing to product work:

**COO** — Research: modern digital product launch strategies, Gumroad best practices 2025-2026, conversion optimization for digital downloads, product listing psychology

**CTMO** — Research: current Notion template market (what's selling, what formats, what features differentiate premium templates), n8n marketplace trends, AI prompt engineering best practices, digital product packaging/delivery tech

**CFO** — Research: Gumroad fee structure and payout mechanics, digital product pricing psychology (anchoring, tiering, bundles), competitor pricing analysis, revenue projection methodologies for digital goods

### 4. Product Pipeline Stages

Every product moves through these stages:

| Stage | Duration | Gate |
|-------|----------|------|
| 🔬 RESEARCH | 2+ hours | Market analysis + competitive data filed |
| 📝 CRITIQUE | Board review | All roles have filed feedback |
| 🔧 REFINE | Based on feedback | Revised spec addresses all critiques |
| 🧪 PROTOTYPE | Build draft | Testable version exists |
| ✅ LAUNCH READY | Final check | Go/No-Go decision filed |
| 🚀 LIVE | Upload + list | On Gumroad, marketed |

### 5. What This Does NOT Change
- Autonomous operations: still in effect. Don't wait for Jason.
- Cost escalation rule: still only escalate if spend changes.
- Stay busy: if your research is done, start the next product's research.
- Parallel work: multiple products can be in different pipeline stages simultaneously.
