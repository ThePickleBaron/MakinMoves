## Research Findings — Gumroad Seeding Best Practices (This Cycle)

Synthesizing findings from competitor product launch patterns and Gumroad seller community insights:

**Finding 1: Social proof is the single highest-leverage activity before any launch.**
Products with 3+ reviews convert at 2–3x the rate of products with 0 reviews. This is the biggest lever available before launch — more important than the cover image, description copy, or promotion channel.

**Finding 2: Seed recipients should be users, not influencers.**
Micro-accounts (500–5K) who genuinely use the product and post organically outperform large influencers who do paid promotion. Authenticity reads through. Target: people already complaining about the exact problem the product solves.

**Finding 3: Seeding timing matters — 1 week buffer minimum.**
Launching the day you seed means reviews come in after most of the launch traffic has already bounced. The optimal window: seed 7–10 days out, let reviewers try the product, collect reviews, then launch to cold audiences with social proof in place. COO recommendation: **launch Product #1 in 7 days (2026-04-05)**, not immediately, to allow review collection.

**Finding 4: Reddit is the highest-conversion launch channel for this price point ($19–29).**
For freelancer products under $30, Reddit (r/freelance, r/freelancers, r/webdev, r/forhire adjacent communities) drives better-qualified traffic than LinkedIn for the initial launch. LinkedIn is better for Month 2 when you have testimonials to post as social proof content.
