## Research Findings — Conversion & Launch Strategy

Critical learnings applied to both products:

1. **First 10 reviews are the unlock.** Zero-review products convert at <1%. 5+ reviews at 4.4+ push conversion to 6–8%. Seed with discounts/free copies to trusted users BEFORE public launch.
2. **Title specificity is the #1 listing lever.** "50 AI Prompts for Freelancers to Land Clients & Close Faster" outperforms "AI Prompt Pack."
3. **Thumbnail converts before copy.** High-contrast, text-forward cover images are the deciding factor in 3 seconds.
4. **Order bump at checkout:** A $7 add-on ("10 bonus prompts") or "$9 quick-start Notion dashboard" increases AOV ~20–30%.
5. **Bundle strategy:** Offer both products together at $49 (vs. $24 + $29 = $53 separately) — easy upsell, higher total revenue per customer.
6. **Platform risk:** Gumroad Trustpilot score is 1.4/5 (83% 1-star). One chargeback can freeze account. Mitigation: dual-list on Payhip immediately, build email list from day 1.
