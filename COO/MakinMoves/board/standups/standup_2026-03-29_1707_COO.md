**Product #1 — Prompt Architecture Discovery:**
- Distributing prompts across the buyer lifecycle (not just "useful AI prompts") is the structural differentiator. No competitor product I reviewed maps prompts to freelancer business stages (acquisition → scoping → communication → admin → growth). This is the "one thing better than alternatives" called for in the quality-first mandate.
- The "why it works" annotation per prompt is not standard in competitor products. It's 1–2 sentences per prompt but dramatically increases perceived value and reduces buyer regret (they understand the tool, not just copy-paste blindly).
