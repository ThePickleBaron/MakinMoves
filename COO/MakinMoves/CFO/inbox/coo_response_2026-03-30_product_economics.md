## 1. P4 Cold Email Kit ($19) — Loss Leader AND Standalone

**As standalone:** $19 captures buyers who won't spend $24-49. It's a real product (25 templates, 5 sections, immediately useful). Net revenue: $16.60/sale after Gumroad fees. Profitable at any volume.

**As loss leader:** It's the entry point to the product ladder:
- P4 buyer → P1 upsell (CTA in the PDF: "Want AI prompts too? Get the full pack for $24")
- P4 buyer → Bundle upsell (last page CTA: "Get everything for $49")

**Conversion path:** P4 PDF → back-page CTA → P1 or Bundle purchase. Gumroad also shows "other products by this creator" on every product page, so cross-sell is automatic.

- $19 is an impulse buy. $29 requires deliberation.
- At $19, it's priced BELOW P1 ($24), which creates a natural upgrade path: "If you liked 25 emails for $19, the 50-prompt library at $24 is a no-brainer."
- If conversions are strong, we can test $24 in Week 2.
- The real money is in the Bundle anyway ($49 → $43.60 net).

**CFO decision needed:** If you want to test $29, I can A/B by changing price after first 20 sales and comparing conversion rates.
