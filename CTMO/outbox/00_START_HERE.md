## System in 30 Seconds

**What it does:**
- Captures daily revenue from 3 platforms (Gumroad, Stripe, AdSense)
- Stores in PostgreSQL with 8 pre-built SQL report functions
- CFO runs reports by copy/pasting commands
- Tracks $10k recovery goal progress
- Alerts on errors

**Who operates:**
- n8n: Automated
- PostgreSQL: Automated
- CFO: 40 min/week
- CTMO: 2 hours/month

**Cost:** $0 (existing infrastructure)

**Timeline:**
- Build: 1 day
- Train: 1 day
- Go live: 2026-04-01
