================================================================================
RISK MITIGATION
================================================================================

Risk 1: API Failures
  Mitigation: Auto-retry logic (3 retries), fallback to CSV + cron
  Escalation: CTMO email if >24h down

Risk 2: Testing Inconclusive
  Mitigation: Extend test through Week 3, collect more data
  Fallback: Try different hypothesis if tie detected
  Escalation: Flag in report, doesn't block decision

Risk 3: Revenue Higher Than Expected
  Mitigation: Continue tests anyway, don't assume luck
  Opportunity: Consider scaling spend on highest-performing lever

Risk 4: n8n Publishing Fails
  Mitigation: Alert if no publishes in 24h
  Fallback: Manual publishing queue or COO publishes manually
  Escalation: CRITICAL if >3 days, CTMO investigates

Risk 5: Database Corruption
  Mitigation: Daily backups, restore procedure tested
  Fallback: Restore from backup, lose <24h data
  Escalation: If unrecoverable, reconstruct from platform APIs

All risks contained; worst case is manual workaround while debugging.
