## When to Upgrade Costs

| Condition | Current Cost | Upgraded Cost | When |
|-----------|------------|--------------|------|
| **Need more Claude articles** | $25/mo | $50/mo | When content demand > 100/mo |
| **WordPress slow/unstable** | $0 | $5/mo | If free hosting fails (unlikely) |
| **Want email marketing** | $0 | $20/mo | If > 1000 email subscribers |
| **Want advanced SEO tools** | $0 | $50+/mo | If > $1,000/month revenue (optional) |
| **Want paid traffic** | $0 | $200+/mo | Only if ROI > 3:1 (test first) |

**Key rule:** Never spend money on upgrades until revenue proves ROI. All upgrades are optional and can wait.
