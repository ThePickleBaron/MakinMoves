## Key Costs Explained

### Digital Products (Gumroad)
- Free platform, free hosting
- Gumroad takes 10% + $0.50 per sale
- You keep 87% of each sale
- **No additional costs beyond that**

### Freelance Writing (Claude API + WordPress)
- Claude API: $0.005 per article (~$25/month for 5,000 articles)
- WordPress domain: $1/month
- WordPress hosting: $0/month (free tier)
- Medium Partner Program: $0 (you earn 50% of member revenue)
- **Total: ~$25/month**

### Affiliate Marketing (WordPress-based)
- Domain: $1/month (shared with Writing)
- WordPress hosting: $0/month (free, shared)
- Affiliate tools: $0–10/month (Pretty Links optional)
- **Total: ~$10/month**

## What Drives Costs

**VARIABLE (you control):**
- Claude API usage (0–$200/month depending on volume)
- WordPress plugins ($0–100/month)
- SEO tools ($0–100/month)
- Paid traffic ads ($0–500/month)

**FIXED (minimal):**
- Domain: $1/month
- Platform fees: Built into revenue

**KEY INSIGHT:** You can run Trinity on $0/month (just domain) or $700/month (with tools + ads). Revenue doesn't depend on tool spending. Use cheap/free tools first, upgrade only if ROI positive.

## Risk Assessment

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |

| Gumroad sales don't materialize | -30% revenue | Low (20%) | Diversification (Writing + Affiliate offset) |

**Overall risk: LOW.** Diversification across three streams mitigates single-platform failure.
