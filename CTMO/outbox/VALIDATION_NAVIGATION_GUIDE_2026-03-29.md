## Key Findings (One Page)

### Question 1: Are the 3 niches viable?
**Answer: YES ✓**
- Market size confirmed (searches exist)
- Affiliate programs exist and pay
- Business model proven by competitors (Zapier, Ahrefs, etc.)
- All 3 pass market validation

### Question 2: Is the revenue projection realistic?
**Answer: NO ❌**

| Niche | CEO M6 | Realistic M6 | Gap |
|-------|--------|--------|-----|
| Email | $7,500 | $960-1,200 | -87% |
| Freelance | $5,460 | $750-900 | -86% |
| AI | $15,000 | $2,160-2,880 | -82% |
| **Total** | **$27,960** | **$3,870-5,000** | **-86%** |

### Question 3: Why the gap?
**Root cause:** 3 cascading errors

1. **SEO timeline compressed** (CEO Month 3-4; realistic 6-10 months)
   - Impact: Visitors are 40-60% lower than projected

2. **Conversion rates overstated** (CEO 2-5%; realistic 1-1.5%)
   - Impact: Each visitor generates 50% fewer conversions
   - Evidence: Industry benchmarks (SaaS affiliate networks)

3. **Content execution underbudgeted** (6 articles/week at SEO quality)
   - Impact: If not founder-led, requires $3.6K+/month freelance budget
   - CEO's model assumes this cost is zero

### Question 4: Does this break the recovery model?
**Answer: NO ✓**
- Realistic P3 Month 6: $3,870
- Blended P1+P2+P3 Month 6: $12,660
- Recovery target: $10,000
- Result: Recovery achieved Month 6-7 (vs. CEO's Month 3-4)
- Impact: 3-4 month delay, acceptable

### Question 5: Should we proceed?
**Answer: YES, with 4 conditions ✓**

**Conditions:**
1. **Reset timeline:** 8-10 months to $15K+ run-rate (not 6 months to $27K)
2. **Commit content:** Founder/COO 6-10 hours/week for Months 1-3 (in-house quality)
3. **Stagger launch:** AI (M1) → Freelance (M2) → Email (M3), not simultaneous
4. **Monitor escalation:** Month 4 decision point — if revenue <$100, pause P3

**Why proceed?**
- Niches are real and viable
- Long-term value is strong ($24K-36K/year per niche by Year 2)
- Risk is manageable (all mitigation strategies in place)
- Blended portfolio still hits recovery by Month 7-8
- Zero upfront cost to test
