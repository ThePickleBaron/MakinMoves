## CTMO Recommendation

### PRIMARY: Zapier Pro ($50/month)

**When to use:** If budget allows and P2 is a priority

**Advantages:**
- Fastest setup (4-6 hours)
- 100% automation (all flows)
- Best client experience
- Complete autonomy post-March-31
- Ready by March 31 deadline
- Cost is negligible vs. P2 revenue ($50 = 1 invoice)

**Financial impact:** +$50/mo, saves $4,200 net Year 1

---

### FALLBACK 1: n8n Hybrid ($0 additional)

**When to use:** Budget is tight, timeline flexible (April 5-7 acceptable)

**Advantages:**
- No additional cost (already budgeted)
- Consolidates Trinity + P2 on single platform
- Best long-term savings ($4,800/year)
- 100% automation

**Trade-off:** Complex setup (12-16 hours), requires CTMO technical work, April launch

**Financial impact:** $0 additional, saves $4,800 net Year 1 (BEST SAVINGS)

---

### FALLBACK 2: Make.com ($10/month)

**When to use:** Setup time is constrained (6-8 hour window available)

**Advantages:**
- Good balance of cost and speed
- Ready by April 1-2 (acceptable delay)
- Cloud-hosted, low maintenance
- Easier than n8n

**Trade-off:** Smaller platform, reporting slightly limited

**Financial impact:** +$10/mo, saves $4,560 net Year 1

### NOT RECOMMENDED: Manual Hybrid ($0)

- ~$2,000/mo revenue loss from friction (unacceptable)
- COO manual work (1h/month)
- Poor client experience (invoices not instant)
- Breaks at scale (can't handle 10+ projects/month)

**Financial impact:** Loses $26,400 Year 1
