### Question 4: "Time from outreach to payment?"

**Median timeline:**
- Outreach send → First reply: 3-7 days
- Reply → Scheduled call: 1-2 days
- Call → Proposal sent: 2-4 days
- Proposal → Decision: 5-14 days (negotiation period)
- Signature → Payment: 5-10 days (payment terms)
- **Total:** 21-42 days (median: 30 days)

**Compressed (best case):** 14-21 days (requires fast-moving buyer)
**Current deadline (March 31):** Only 2 days remaining — impossible for closes, realistic for call scheduling
