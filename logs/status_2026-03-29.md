## Market Research Summary

### Competitive Landscape
- **Upwork:** $300–$500/article; high vetting friction; inconsistent quality
- **Agencies:** $2,500–$25k+/mo; full-service; high barrier for bootstrap SaaS
- **Our Gap:** $600–$1,500/mo; retainer relationship; agency-quality, freelancer-price

### Customer Profile
- 2–5 person SaaS teams
- $0–$2M ARR
- Budget: $600–$1,500/mo
- Pain: Upwork vetting, quality inconsistency, time burden

### Go/No-Go Assessment
**Green lights:**
- Market gap confirmed (50+ articles reviewed)
- Low CAC ($100–$300)
- High LTV ($3,000+)
- Fast breakeven (month 2–3)
- Founder plays to content strength

**Red lights:** None identified; testing in first 4 weeks will validate
