## WHAT COULD GO WRONG?

**Common failure modes:**

1. **Phase 1 revenue drops >50% Month 7**
   - Cause: Algorithm change, market shift, or Phase 1 was unsustainable
   - Fix: Pause Phase 2; investigate Phase 1; test paid ads; extend Phase 1 runway

2. **Phase 2 product $0 revenue by Month 9**
   - Cause: Wrong positioning, wrong market, wrong price
   - Fix: Rapid pivot (48-hour test new positioning); May kill option and switch streams

3. **Contractor quality is poor**
   - Cause: Hired wrong person; insufficient training/SOP
   - Fix: Replace contractor; have backup contractor available; test early

4. **Can't sustain 20 hours/week effort**
   - Cause: Personal circumstances, burnout, or business demands more time
   - Fix: Hire operations person earlier; reduce scope; bring in partner

5. **Revenue trajectory too slow**
   - Cause: Phase 2 strategy underperforming; market size too small
   - Fix: Extend Phase 2 4–6 weeks; increase marketing spend; pivot to new niche
