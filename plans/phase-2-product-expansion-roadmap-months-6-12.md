# Phase 2 Product Expansion Roadmap (Month 6–12)
## Building from $1,000/month to $10,000+/month

**Strategic Planning Document for CEO Decision-Making**
**Timeline:** Month 6–12 (July 2026 – December 2026)
**Assumption:** Phase 1 (Days 1–90, April–May) successfully validates 2–3 revenue streams
**Goal:** Build a sustainable $10,000–$25,000/month business, not just recover and stop

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

By Month 5 (end of Phase 1), you will have proven which streams work:
- **Digital Products:** Easiest to scale (templates → SaaS tools)
- **Print-on-Demand:** Highest volume potential (designs → branded merchandise)
- **Affiliate Marketing:** Most passive (content authority → email list monetization)
- **Freelance/Services:** Highest margins if we prove repeatability

**Phase 2 Expansion (Month 6–12) strategy:**
1. **Months 6–7:** Let Phase 1 run on autopilot; analyze winning stream
2. **Months 7–8:** Build parallel revenue stream #2 (50% effort)
3. **Months 9–10:** Automate Phase 1 fulfillment; hire first contractor
4. **Months 11–12:** Test SaaS MVP or premium membership model; plan Year 2 (2–3 team)

**Expected outcome by Month 12:**
- $5,000–$8,000/month (conservative)
- $8,000–$15,000/month (realistic)
- $15,000–$25,000/month (aggressive)
- 2–3 contractors on payroll
- 1–2 proven product lines ready to scale

**Success definition:** Generating enough consistent revenue that you could hire someone full-time (or work part-time on the business) AND prove the model is repeatable and scalable.

## PART 1: MONTH 6-7 ANALYSIS PHASE — IDENTIFY THE WINNING STREAM

### Month 6 (June 2026): Full Passive Monitoring

**What happens:** Phase 1 products continue selling with zero daily effort from you. Systems (n8n, email marketing, payment processing) run entirely on autopilot.

**CEO's job (15 min/week):**
1. **Monday standup:** Revenue dashboard review
   - Total cumulative revenue (Phase 1 products)
   - Revenue breakdown by stream: Digital, PoD, Affiliate
   - New customers, repeat customers, churn
2. **Friday checkpoint:** Email 30-second summary to board
   - Which stream generated most revenue this week?
   - Any platform issues or customer complaints?

**What you're measuring:**
| Metric | Target | Action if Miss |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| **Total monthly revenue** | $500–$1,500 | Healthy trajectory; continue monitoring |
| **Repeat customer rate** | 10–20% on Digital | Indicates product quality |
| **Email list engagement** | 20%+ open rate | Content is resonating |
| **Affiliate commissions** | $0–$50 (expected) | Normal; SEO takes 6+ months |
| **PoD sales trend** | Flat or +10% week-over-week | Volume model; should trend up |

**Decision checkpoint (End of Month 6):**
- Which ONE stream generated 40–50% of revenue?
- Which TWO streams have proven repeatability (3+ sales/week)?
- Which stream is dying (0 sales for 2+ weeks)?

**Likely outcomes by Month 6:**

**Scenario A: Digital Products Winning ($150–$400/month)**
- Templates proven; niche validated; repeat buyers emerging
- Decision: Build SaaS tools OR expand template line to 8–10 SKUs
- Month 7 action: Research SaaS MVP + create 2–3 new template designs

**Scenario B: Print-on-Demand Winning ($250–$600/month)**
- Designs consistently selling; bestsellers identified; volume scaling
- Decision: Test branded merchandise (custom merch) OR build print shop POD network
- Month 7 action: Increase design production to 40–50 designs; test $19.99+ pricing

**Scenario C: Affiliate Marketing Winning ($0–$50/month, BUT 200+ email list)**
- Content authority building; email list engaged; first commissions incoming
- Decision: Build email course OR test affiliate promotions more aggressively
- Month 7 action: Launch email sequence; test 1–2 higher-ticket affiliate products

**Scenario D: All Three Performing Equally ($300–$500 combined)**
- Diversification working; no single point of failure
- Decision: Build 2nd stream in parallel; don't force specialization yet
- Month 7 action: Pick #2 stream (most scalable) and build parallel expansion

## PART 2: MONTHS 8-10 EXPANSION PHASE — BUILD PRODUCT #2

### Month 8 (August 2026): Launch Secondary Revenue Stream

Based on Month 6–7 analysis, you'll launch a Phase 2 product in your winning niche. Here's the framework by stream:

---

#### **IF DIGITAL PRODUCTS IS WINNING:**

**Phase 2 Product: SaaS Tool or Premium Template Bundle**

*Time commitment: 10–15 hours total (spread over Month 8)*

**Option A: SaaS Tool (Invoice Generator, Design Tool, etc.)**
- **What:** Simple web app solving a niche problem
- **Revenue model:** $5–$10/month subscription or $29–$79 lifetime
- **Time to launch:** 4 weeks (CTMO builds; CEO validates)
- **Example:** "AI Notion Template Generator" for $29/month = 10 customers = $290/month
- **Who builds:** CTMO (Node.js + simple UI)
- **Validation:** Get 20+ email waitlist signups before building

**Option B: Premium Template Bundle ($49–$199)**
- **What:** 5–10 templates packaged as "Ultimate [Niche] Toolkit"
- **Time to launch:** 2 weeks (repackage existing + 2–3 new templates)
- **Revenue model:** One-time purchase + upsell "update library" membership ($5/mo)
- **Example:** "Ultimate Notion Project Management Toolkit" = $79 + $5/month subscribers
- **Who builds:** You (templates) + CTMO (packaging + automation)

**Option C: Productized Freelance Service ($500–$2K/month retainers)**
- **What:** "Done-for-you Notion setup" or "Template customization service"
- **Time per client:** 3–5 hours
- **Revenue:** $500–$2,000 per client, 1–2 clients/month = $500–$4,000/month
- **Who handles:** You sell/scope; freelancer or AI tool executes

**Recommended:** Option A (SaaS) if you can spare CTMO 40 hours; Option B (Bundle) if you need faster revenue. Option C if you want cash immediately.

**Month 8 Execution:**
- Week 1: Validate idea (get 20+ email signups or presales)
- Weeks 2–3: Build MVP or bundle
- Week 4: Launch, promote to existing email list
- **Expected revenue Month 8:** $50–$300 (small cohort; growth comes Month 9+)

---

#### **IF PRINT-ON-DEMAND IS WINNING:**

**Phase 2 Product: Branded Merchandise Line or Niche Shop Network**

*Time commitment: 10–15 hours total (spread over Month 8)*

**Option A: Custom Branded Merch ($1,500–$3,000 upfront, $200–$800/month revenue)**
- **What:** Partner with print shop (Printful, Scalable Press) to offer branded merch under your brand
- **Effort:** Design 10–15 branded items; set up shop; $1,500 upfront for inventory (if drop-ship: $0)
- **Example:** "MakinMoves branded hoodies/hats" sold on your own shop
- **Revenue:** 20–40 sales/month × $15 profit = $300–$600/month
- **Who handles:** You design; Printful/Scalable prints + ships (you handle customer service)

**Option B: Multi-Niche Shop Network ($0 startup, $150–$600/month revenue)**
- **What:** Launch 2–3 new PoD shops in adjacent niches (e.g., if succeeding in "indie hackers," launch "startup founders" and "remote workers" shops)
- **Effort:** 30 designs/shop × 3 shops = 90 designs (doable in 6 weeks)
- **Revenue:** 3 shops × $50–$200/month = $150–$600/month by Month 10

**Option C: Print-on-Demand Partner Network ($200–$500/month revenue)**
- **What:** License your designs to other PoD platforms (Teespring, Printful marketplace)
- **Effort:** Upload designs + set pricing; zero ongoing effort
- **Revenue:** Passive licensing revenue 5–15% of PoD sales

**Recommended:** Option B (Multi-Niche) if you have design momentum; Option A (Branded) if you want cash + brand building.

**Month 8 Execution:**
- Week 1: Identify 2 adjacent niches; validate demand (Reddit, Google Trends)
- Weeks 2–3: Create 20 designs per new niche (can be variations of proven winners)
- Week 4: Launch; promote to existing audience + new communities
- **Expected revenue Month 8:** $100–$300 (new shops ramping; expect payoff Month 9+)

---

#### **IF AFFILIATE MARKETING IS WINNING (Email List 200+):**

**Phase 2 Product: Email Course, Lead Magnet, or Premium Newsletter**

*Time commitment: 8–12 hours total (spread over Month 8)*

**Option A: Free Email Course + Upsell ($1,000–$3,000/month revenue)**
- **What:** 5–7 part email sequence teaching [niche topic]; final email is soft-sell for affiliate products or your products
- **Time:** 8–10 hours to write course
- **Lead magnet:** Offer free course (PDF, checklist, template) in exchange for email signup
- **Expected conversion:** 20–30 new subscribers/week × 5% upsell rate × $50 average order = $500–$750/month by Month 10
- **Example:** "7-Day AI Tool Mastery Course" for indie hackers; last lesson promotes affiliate tools + your SaaS

**Option B: Premium Membership Newsletter ($10–$20/month)**
- **What:** Paid tier on top of free newsletter offering exclusive analysis, tools, deeper insights
- **Time:** 3–4 hours/week to write premium content
- **Conversion:** 100 subscribers × 10% conversion to paid × $15/month = $150/month + growth
- **Example:** Free weekly newsletter (general tips); paid tier ($15/mo) = deep dives, tool reviews, code snippets

**Option C: Affiliate Acceleration (Aggressive Monetization)**
- **What:** Increase affiliate promotions in your email list (more affiliate product recommendations)
- **Time:** 1–2 hours/week to research + write promos
- **Expected revenue:** 200 email subscribers × 5% click rate × 3% conversion × $20 commission = $600/month by Month 9
- **Risk:** Unsubscribes may increase; monitor list health

**Recommended:** Option A (Email Course) if you want build trust first; Option C (Aggressive) if you want fastest revenue.

**Month 8 Execution:**
- Week 1: Write email course outline; validate topic with list (poll 3–5 subscribers)
- Weeks 2–3: Write course (3–5 hours)
- Week 4: Launch; promote lead magnet; measure conversion rate
- **Expected revenue Month 8:** $50–$200 (slow burn; payoff Month 9+)

---

#### **IF FREELANCE/RETAINER SERVICES WINNING (2–3 active clients):**

**Phase 2 Product: Productized Service or Service Plus Digital Product**

*Time commitment: 10–15 hours total (spread over Month 8)*

**Option A: Systematize Service into Productized Offering ($1,500–$4,000/month revenue)**
- **What:** Define scope, process, deliverables (e.g., "Content Audit Service = $800, 5-day turnaround, specific deliverables")
- **Time:** 3–5 hours to document process; then hire freelancer or use AI to systematize
- **Revenue:** 3–4 clients/month × $750–$1,200 = $2,250–$4,800/month by Month 9
- **Who handles:** You sales/project manage; freelancer or AI executes

**Option B: Service Plus Template Bundle ($2,000–$5,000/month revenue)**
- **What:** Offer done-for-you service ($500–$1K) + sell templates/tools to self-serve market ($29–$99)
- **Example:** "Done-for-you blog strategy ($1K)" + "DIY Blog Strategy Template ($29)"
- **Revenue:** 2 premium clients/month + 10–20 template sales = $2,000–$4,000/month

**Option C: Training/Certification Program ($3,000–$10,000/month revenue)**
- **What:** Course teaching your service methodology; students pay $97–$297 for training, then hire you
- **Time:** 8–10 hours to create course
- **Revenue:** 20–30 students/month × $150 average = $3,000–$4,500/month + upsell to services

**Recommended:** Option A if you want scaling without team; Option B if you want diversification; Option C if you have unique methodology.

**Month 8 Execution:**
- Week 1: Document your service process (what are the steps?)
- Weeks 2–3: Create productized offering or template
- Week 4: Launch; promote to existing clients + warm network
- **Expected revenue Month 8:** $300–$1,000 (depends on service type; faster than other streams)
