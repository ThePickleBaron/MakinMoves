# Writing Retainer Service — Executive Summary
## Financial Opportunity & Positioning (For CFO/Board)

**Date:** March 29, 2026
**Service:** B2B/SaaS Writing Retainer ($600–$1,500/mo)
**Market:** Bootstrap/early-stage SaaS teams (ICP: $0–$2M ARR)

---

## Market Opportunity

### Market Size
- **Total addressable market:** ~50,000 SaaS companies in $0–$2M ARR range
- **Serviceable addressable market (SAM):** 10,000 companies actively seeking writers (20%)
- **Serviceable obtainable market (SOM):** 500–1,000 customers reachable in year 1–2 (conservative)

### Market Dynamics
- **Upwork dominance:** 80% of SaaS hiring for content starts on Upwork (pain point: vetting, inconsistency)
- **Agency gap:** Direct competitors ($2,500–$25,000/mo) leave $600–$1,500 segment unserved
- **Demand signal:** Founders consistently cite "finding reliable writers" as top content marketing friction
- **Low CAC:** Organic channels (Indie Hackers, Twitter, cold outreach) cost $150–$300/customer
- **High LTV:** Retainer model = predictable 12+ month customer lifetime; 60% retention typical

## Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Writer quality issues** | Churn spikes; brand damage | Pre-vet rigorously; QA every article; backup writers |
| **Customer churn** | Revenue drops | Guarantee satisfaction; weekly check-ins; community building |
| **Acquisition costs too high** | Profitability delayed | Focus on organic channels (warm network, communities) first |
| **Can't scale writers** | Revenue ceiling at $5k–$10k MRR | Hire 3–5 writers month 1; contract at $150–250/article |
| **Founder time bottleneck** | Unsustainable to manage | Hire freelance ops person by month 3 ($1,000/mo) |

## Differentiation from Competitors

### Why We Win vs. Upwork
- **Consistency:** Same writer every month; knows your product and customers
- **Vetting:** We vet for you; you get pre-screened quality
- **Relationship:** Person, not marketplace; accountability through relationship
- **Cost:** 10–20% less total cost when including your hiring time

### Why We Win vs. Agencies ($2,500+)
- **Price:** 50–70% cheaper than mid-market agencies
- **Speed to setup:** 2 days vs. 4–8 weeks
- **Simplicity:** No complex strategy or governance; just writing
- **No lock-in:** Month-to-month vs. 6–12 month minimums

### Why We Win vs. Freelancers
- **Reliability:** Service-backed guarantee vs. individual risk
- **Vetting:** Vetted pool vs. you finding someone
- **Accountability:** Service replaces writer if needed; individual freelancer doesn't
