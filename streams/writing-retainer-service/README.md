## Key Metrics to Track (Weekly)

**Acquisition Metrics:**
- Leads sourced (by channel: warm email, Indie Hackers, Twitter, cold LinkedIn)
- Conversion rate per channel
- CAC (customer acquisition cost)
- Time from lead to close

**Retention Metrics:**
- Monthly churn rate
- Customer LTV (lifetime value)
- NPS (net promoter score)
- Article turnaround time

**Financial Metrics:**
- MRR (monthly recurring revenue)
- Gross margin (should be 60%+)
- Net profit (after ops costs)
- Payback period (should be <1 month)

**Operational Metrics:**
- Articles delivered on time (target: 100%)
- Revision cycles (target: <2 rounds per article)
- Writer utilization (% of capacity used)
- Writer satisfaction

---

## Red Flags (Escalate Immediately)

1. **Writer quality issues:** Customer complaints about writing quality; replace writer immediately
2. **High churn (>15%/month):** Indicates satisfaction or fit problem; investigate and fix
3. **Low conversion (<10%):** Messaging not resonating; test and adjust
4. **CAC >$500:** Acquisition channels too expensive; focus on organic
5. **Founder bottleneck:** Operations taking >15 hrs/week; hire ops person
