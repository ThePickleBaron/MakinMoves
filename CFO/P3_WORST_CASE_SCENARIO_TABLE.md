**If Month 4 revenue <$100:**

**If Month 6 revenue <$500:**

**If Month 6 revenue <$100:**
- P3 model fundamentally broken
- **CFO decision:** PAUSE P3, investigate keyword/niche strategy
- **Reallocation:** Move $50/month to P1/P2 acceleration
