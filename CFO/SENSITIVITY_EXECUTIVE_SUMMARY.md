## CRITICAL ASSUMPTIONS: VALIDATION GATES (Week 1)

### Gate 1: P2 Warm Conversion Rate
**The Question:** Can we get 5%+ inquiry rate from warm email list?

**What We Need:** Test with first 100 emails
- **Baseline expectation:** 5 inquiries (5% inquiry rate)
- **Red flag:** <2 inquiries (conversion too weak, pivot to cold)
- **Timeline:** Send test by April 1, results by April 7

**Impact if it fails:**
- Missing: $13,000 revenue (25% of total)
- Fallback: Activate aggressive cold outreach (same team, different channel, slightly slower)
- Recovery delay: 15-30 days (still viable)

**Impact if it succeeds:**
- Gain: $5,600 revenue (11% upside)
- Acceleration: Recovery 20 days faster
- Confidence: 3x strategy confirmed as viable

**Recommendation:** This is the #1 validation. If inquiry rate <2%, pivot to cold immediately. Do not wait.

---

### Gate 2: P1 Gumroad Shadowban Risk
**The Question:** Does Gumroad flag white-label resales as suspicious?

**What We Need:** Upload 5 test templates and monitor
- **Baseline expectation:** No throttling, normal impression/sales curve
- **Red flag:** Impressions drop 70%+ or "alert" email from Gumroad (policy violation)
- **Timeline:** Upload by April 2, 48-hour observation window

**Impact if it fails:**
- Missing: $2,700 revenue (5% of total)
- Fallback: Shift 50% to Etsy (higher fees, but no shadowban risk), keep 50% on Gumroad
- Recovery delay: 7 days (pivot week)

**Impact if it succeeds:**
- Gain: $900 revenue (2% upside)
- Confidence: P1 white-label model validated
- Scaling: Full 30-template launch Week 2

**Recommendation:** This is the #2 validation. Low probability of shadowban (most creators succeed), but high impact if it fails. Test quickly.

---

### Gate 3: CEO Execution Effort (Burnout Prevention)
**The Question:** Can CEO deliver 3x strategy without overload?

**What We Need:** Time tracking for P1 white-label rebrand (Week 1)
- **Baseline expectation:** 12 hours to rebrand 30 templates (4 hours per 10)
- **Red flag:** >20 hours by April 7 (indicates process too manual)
- **Target:** <8 hours/week total by April 15

**Impact if it fails:**
- Missing: $5,000-8,000 revenue (burnout compounds, execution quality drops)
- **PLUS: Operational risk** — CEO exits mid-execution (catastrophic)
- Fallback: Outsource design work (cost $200-400), reduce scope to 15 templates

**Impact if it succeeds:**
- Gain: $2,800 revenue (5% upside)
- PLUS: Operational sustainability (can run 6 months without collapse)
- Confidence: 3x strategy sustainable at scale

**Recommendation:** This is the #3 validation. CEO must be at <180 hrs/month or entire operation fails M4+. **Monitor weekly**, intervene immediately if trending above.
