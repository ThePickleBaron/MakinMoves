## SEO TIMELINE REALITY CHECK

**Industry standards for new domain ranking:**
- Month 1-2: Indexing, crawling by search engines
- Month 3-4: Ranking for long-tail, low-competition keywords
- Month 5-8: Ranking for medium-competition keywords
- Month 9+: Ranking for competitive keywords (requires DA 30+)

**Our scenario (affiliate sites, 3-niche approach):**
- Domain authority: 0 (brand new)
- Target: High-volume, low-competition affiliate keywords (e.g., "best email marketing tools")
- Backlinks: 5-10 from partnerships by M3
- Content: 12-15 articles (4/week × 3-4 weeks)

**Expected ranking timeline:**
- M1: 0 keywords ranking (too new)
- M2: 5-10 long-tail keywords ranking (e.g., "email tools for solopreneurs")
- M3: 10-20 medium keywords ranking, ~50-200 UU/day
- M4: 30+ keywords ranking, 200-500 UU/day

**Affiliate conversion at each stage:**
- M1-2: 0 UU = $0 revenue (expected)
- M3: 50-200 UU, 0.5% conversion = 0.25-1 affiliate sale/day = $7.50-30/day = $225-900/mo
- M4: 200-500 UU, 0.5% conversion = 1-2.5 sales/day = $600-2,500/mo

**CFO Assessment:** Timeline is optimistic but NOT impossible
- With 5+ quality backlinks + 15+ articles, M3 $300 is achievable
- More likely: M3 $100-300 (conservative), M4 $600-2,000 (optimistic)

## PARTNERSHIP TIMELINE BOTTLENECK

**Assumption: CEO can secure 5+ partnerships in W2-3 of launch (by April 7)**

**Reality test:**
- Cold partnership outreach: 2-week response time typical
- Quality guest post acceptance: 20-30% response rate
- Time to publish guest post: 1-4 weeks after acceptance
- Backlink quality varies: Some partners no-follow, some follow

**Best case:**
- CEO sends 20 partnership requests Week 1
- 5-6 accept by Week 2
- 3-4 published by end of M1
- **Backlinks: 3-4 by M1 end** ✅ (good)

**Likely case:**
- CEO sends 10 requests Week 1
- 2-3 accept by Week 3
- 1-2 published by M2
- **Backlinks: 1-2 by M1 end** ⚠️ (slow)

**Worst case:**
- CEO sends 5 requests, all rejected or ghosted
- 0 partnerships by M2
- **Backlinks: 0 by M1 end** 🔴 (no-go)

**Risk factor:** Partnership success depends entirely on CEO's outreach effectiveness
- If CEO has existing relationships: 5+ partnerships likely
- If CEO is cold outreaching: 1-2 partnerships likely
- **2.5x timeline assumes 5 partnerships, but 1-2 is more realistic**
