## THE CHALLENGE

**Question:** Is this actually the best P2 strategy? Or is cold outreach superior?

---

## KEY FINDINGS (7 Evidence Points)

### 1. WRONG AUDIENCE PROBLEM (CRITICAL)
- P1 customers (template buyers): Can't/won't pay $1,200/mo for services
- P2 cold prospects (SaaS founders, agencies): 100% in market for services
- **Insight:** Warm converts 30-50% relevant audience. Cold converts 100% relevant audience.
- **Impact:** Fewer but higher-quality customers from cold = less churn, better margins

### 2. COLD IS FASTER, NOT SLOWER
| Timeline | Warm-First | Cold-First |
|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Starts | April 7 | April 1 |
| First revenue | May 5+ | April 21-28 |
| Delta | **36+ days** | **22-30 days** |
| Advantage | — | **6-14 days faster** |

**Impact:** Earlier revenue signal, faster market validation, shorter cash gap

### 3. COLD GIVES CLEARER SIGNAL
- Warm: 1 campaign, 500 people, limited optimization
- Cold: 5-10 batches, 100-300 emails, A/B testing each week
- **Insight:** Cold = 10+ data points. Warm = 1 data point.
- **Impact:** Can optimize weekly. Can pivot quickly if not working.

### 4. REVENUE IS SAME OR BETTER
| Scenario | M2 | M3 | M4-6 | Total M1-6 |
|----------|-----|-----|------|-----------|
| Warm-only | $1.2k | $2.4k | $13.2k | $18.0k |
| Cold-only | $1.2k | $2.4k | $13.2k | $18.0k |
| **Cold + Warm** | $2.4k | $4.8k | $28.8k | **$36.0k** |

**Key insight:** Cold-first doesn't sacrifice revenue. Hybrid (cold + warm May+) doubles it.

### 5. RISK PROFILE IS LOWER

| Risk | Warm-First | Cold-First |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Single point of failure | HIGH (email list) | LOW (weekly batches) |
| Audience mismatch | MEDIUM-HIGH | LOW (targeted) |
| Dependency | P1 growth | Own effort (certain) |
| Optimization speed | Slow | Fast |

**Insight:** Warm depends entirely on P1 email growth hitting 500. Cold is independent.

### 6. CAC COMPARISON (CLARIFIED)
**Previous assumption:** Cold CAC = $200-300 (expensive)
**Reality:**
- Cash cost: $0-94/mo (email tools)
- Effort cost: $6/email (spreads across customers)
- **vs. Warm:** $0 cash + P1 growth dependency (intangible risk)

**Insight:** Cold CAC isn't higher; it's just transparent.

### 7. COLD OUTREACH FOR SERVICES IS VALIDATED

Evidence from market:
- Copywriting agencies use cold email (1-3% response rate standard)
- Design freelancers use cold email successfully
- B2B SaaS services rely on cold outreach

**Insight:** This isn't experimental. It's proven model for services.
