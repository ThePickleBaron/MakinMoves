## QUICK REFERENCE

### The Question
**Current priority:** P1 (revenue now) > P2 (revenue soon) > P3 (revenue later)

**Does this optimize for the right metric?**

### The Answer
**No.** Current plan maximizes M1-3 revenue but minimizes Year 2+ potential and sustainability.

### The Alternative
**P3-First (foundation) > P2-Parallel (service) > P1-Opportunistic (wins)**

### The Tradeoff
- **Current plan:** $5-8K/month by M12, high effort, hits ceilings, fragile recovery
- **Alternative plan:** $8-16K/month by M12, lower effort M6+, no ceiling, defensible recovery
- **Recovery timeline:** Both hit $10K by Month 10 (only 1 month difference)

### The Recommendation
**Approve alternative plan** with commitment gates at M3, M4, M6. Monitor revenue inflection. Pivot if gates fail.
