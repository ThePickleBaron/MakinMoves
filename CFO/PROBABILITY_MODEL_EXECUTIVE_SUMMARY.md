## CRITICAL RISKS (Both Models)

### Risk 1: Email List Doesn't Grow (P1 Specific)
- **Impact:** Revenue drops 60-80%
- **Trigger:** Email <100 by April 15
- **Mitigation:** Daily outreach (Twitter, Discord, cold email)

### Risk 2: Shadowban Hits (White-Label Specific)
- **Impact:** Revenue drops 70%, platform throttles account
- **Trigger:** Gumroad flags resold content Week 2-4
- **Mitigation:** Customize templates, diversify to Etsy/Teespring

### Risk 3: Traffic Generation Fails (Both Models)
- **Impact:** Both models stall (need 100-300 UU/day by May 1)
- **Trigger:** CEO capacity exhausted or paid ads exceed budget
- **Mitigation:** Pre-test traffic channels, allocate budget early

### Risk 4: CEO Burnout (Both Models)
- **Impact:** Quality collapse Week 5-6, execution stalls
- **Trigger:** Slog phase (Week 4-6) with no relief capacity
- **Mitigation:** Hire writer by Day 25 (covered in exec risk decision)

## RECOMMENDATION

### Primary Strategy: Creator Toolkit (P1)
- **Why:** 3.7x better expected value, founder expertise, repeat revenue potential
- **Conditions:** Email list grows to 100+ by Apr 1, traffic generation executes
- **Decision Horizon:** 30 days (April 29 evaluation)

- **When:** If P1 gates fail by April 15 (email <50 + revenue $0)

- **Action:** CEO reduces content production (4→2 articles/week), focuses on P3 (n8n workflows)
- **Outcome:** Longer recovery timeline (Aug→Oct), but sustainable execution
