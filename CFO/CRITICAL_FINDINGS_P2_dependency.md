## P2 PROFITABILITY RISKS (DETAILED)

### Risk #1: Sales Cycle Slips

**Current assumption:** 8-12 week sales cycle from first contact to close

**Market reality:** B2B service sales are 3-6 months when:
- First-time service purchase (no prior relationship)
- Multiple decision-makers (budget approval, team sign-off)
- Evaluation period (trial, references, case studies)

**Impact if slips to 16 weeks:**
- Month 6 customers: 3 (vs. 4 base case) = **-$1,200 revenue**
- Recovery delayed: **+1 month**

**Root cause:** You (as solo founder) are COLD outreach. No existing sales funnel. Each customer is a new discovery → evaluation → negotiation cycle.

### Risk #2: Churn Erodes Scaling

**Current assumption:** 3-5% monthly churn (realistic for early-stage)

**What happens in practice:**
- Month 3 first customer signs on
- Month 4 first customer likes service (early satisfaction)
- Month 5 first customer renews
- **BUT** if writer quality drops or delivery slips, Month 5 becomes a churn moment

**Impact if churn hits 10%:**
- By Month 6, customer base erodes faster than new sales
- Net new customers: only 1-2 (not 4)
- **Revenue impact: -$2,400 M6 (-40% of P2 revenue)**
- Recovery delayed: **+2 months**

**Root cause:** Single writer bottleneck. If writer burns out or quality drops, you have no backup. Churn cascades.

### Risk #3: Backup Writer Cost Eats Margin

**Current assumption:** 1 writer handles 3-4 customers

**Reality at Month 5-6:**
- 4 customers × 14 articles/month = way over 1 writer's capacity
- Must hire 2nd writer by Month 5
- 2nd writer cost: $150-200 per article = **+$600-800/month overhead**

**Impact on P2 profitability:**
- Gross margin compresses from 55% → 45% (10 point hit)
- Month 6 profit: $2,100 (vs. $2,800 with 1 writer)
- **Margin reduction: -$700/month (-25% of gross profit)**

**Root cause:** You can't write 14 high-quality articles/month indefinitely. Burnout happens Month 4-5.

### Risk #4: Pricing Pressure From Competition

**Current assumption:** $1,200/month ARPU (4-6 articles, $200-300 per article rate)

**Market reality by Year 2:**
- AI writing tools are commoditizing: ChatGPT, Claude can produce decent content at $0.10/article
- Offshore writing shops undercutting: India/Philippines writers at $50-100/article (10x cheaper)
- Your $200-300/article positioning gets undercut

**Impact if prices compress to $800/month:**
- By Month 6, new customers sign at $800 (vs. $1,200)
- Gross margin: $1,200 → $800 ARPU = -33% revenue
- **Loss of $1,200 in M6 revenue alone**

**Root cause:** You're competing on a commodity (writing). Unless you can differentiate on outcomes or relationships, price is the battleground.

## P2 SUCCESS REQUIREMENTS (HARD CONSTRAINTS)

### Requirement #1: Pre-Warm Sales Pipeline

**What:** 20 qualified prospects identified and contacted BEFORE hand-off (March 31)

**Why:** 8-12 week sales cycle means Month 1 contacts = Month 3 closes. Without pre-warming:
- Month 3 closes: 0
- Month 4 closes: 1 (best case)
- Month 6 revenue: $1,200 (1 customer, not 4)
- Recovery slips 2+ months

**How:**
- Export list of target companies (B2B SaaS startups, agencies, SMBs needing content)
- Draft outreach email (subject line, hook, CTA)
- Create follow-up sequence (3 touches over 3 weeks)
- **Deploy this week.** Let sales cycle play out during hand-off period.

**Success metric:** 20 warm prospects contacted by March 31, at least 2 in "discovery call" stage by April 10

### Requirement #2: Sales Collateral Ready

**What:** Positioning, pricing, service packages all documented and tested

**Why:** Can't refine messaging once you're hands-off. Must test with 2-3 early customers first.

**What to prepare:**
- Service description (what exactly are you selling? "4-6 articles/month" or "guaranteed lead generation"?)
- Pricing (test $800 vs. $1,200 vs. $1,500 with prospects)
- Onboarding process (what happens after they sign? What do you need from them?)
- Cancellation terms (30-day money-back guarantee? 90-day commitment?)
- **Writer quality baseline** (What's acceptable? Who quality-checks?)

**Success metric:** 2-3 beta customers sign by April 30 with feedback on messaging

### Requirement #3: Writer Vetting Plan

**What:** 2nd writer identified and ready-to-deploy by Month 3

**Why:** You can't write 14+ articles/month sustainably. Month 4-5 is when burnout hits.

**What to prepare:**
- List of 3-5 freelance writers in your niche (vetted for quality)
- Trial assignment ready (sample article, payment terms, approval process)
- Quality rubric (how you'll evaluate writing before delivering to client)
- **Backup plan if primary writer bails** (sick, burnout, better opportunity)

**Success metric:** 1st backup writer onboarded with 1 approved article by Month 4

### Requirement #4: Churn Prevention System

**What:** Processes to keep customers happy and reduce early churn

**Why:** Early-stage services see 8-10% monthly churn. Each churned customer costs $3,600 LTV loss.

**What to build:**
- Onboarding checklist (30-day setup process with milestones)
- Monthly check-ins (is the content performing? Are we meeting expectations?)
- 30-day satisfaction guarantee (first month is risk-free for customer)
- Content quality dashboard (customer can see metrics: traffic, engagement, leads)
- **Outcome-based upsells** ("guaranteed 3 leads/month" model to reduce commoditization risk)

**Success metric:** Month 3 customer still active + engaged by Month 5 (0% churn in cohort)
