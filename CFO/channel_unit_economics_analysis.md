## PART 1: P1 (DIGITAL PRODUCTS) CHANNEL UNIT ECONOMICS

**Overview:** Digital products sold via Gumroad, Etsy, Redbubble (print-on-demand).
**Key Finding:** Email list is the ONLY profitable channel. All other acquisition methods lose money.

### Channel 1: Email List (WARM AUDIENCE)

**Context:** Building email list in parallel with product launches. Subscribers opt-in from blog, Twitter, Product Hunt, or referrals.

#### Unit Economics (Per Customer)

**Traffic & Conversion:**
- Email subscribers by Month 2: 500 (goal)
- Monthly send frequency: 2-4 emails
- Click-through rate per email: 15-20%
- Product inquiry rate: 10% (from clickers)
- Inquiry → Purchase rate: 20% (customers who inquire usually buy)
- **Net conversion:** 500 subscribers × 0.15 CTR × 0.10 inquiry × 0.20 close = **10 customers/month by M2**

**Revenue:**
- Price per product: $29 (average of $19-$39)
- Repeat purchase rate: 5-10% (assume first purchase only)
- **Revenue per customer: $29**

**Costs:**
- Email platform: $0 (free tier: Substack, Beehiiv free, or self-hosted)
- Email sequences written upfront: 4 sequences × 30 min = 2 hours → $20 labor (one-time)
- Ongoing maintenance: 1 hour/month × $30/hr = $30/month
- **Total Month 1 cost: $20 (setup)**
- **Monthly cost (Month 2+): $30/month**

**CAC Calculation (Month 2 basis):**
- Setup cost amortized over 12 months: $20 / 12 = $1.67/month
- Monthly maintenance: $30/month
- **Total monthly cost: $31.67**
- **Customers acquired in Month 2: 10**
- **CAC: $31.67 / 10 = $3.17 per customer**

**But wait — let's be more accurate:**
If we're building the list in Month 1, most conversion happens in Month 2+.
- **Month 1 focus:** Build list to 500
- **Month 2 revenue:** 10 customers × $29 = $290
- **Month 2 cost:** $30 (email platform) + labor to write sequences already spent
- **Month 2 profit: $260**

**LTV Calculation:**
- Initial purchase: $29
- Repeat purchases (5% conversion on next releases): 1 repeat per 20 purchases
- Repeat LTV: $29 × 0.05 = $1.45
- **Total LTV: $29 + $1.45 = $30.45 ≈ $30**

**Unit Economics Summary:**
- **Revenue per customer: $29**
- **CAC: $3.17** (Month 2+)
- **LTV: $30**
- **Profit per customer: $29 - $3.17 = $25.83**
- **Margin: 89%**
- **Return on Ad Spend (RoAS): 9.2x** ($29 / $3.17)

#### Channel Viability: ✅ BEST IN CLASS
- **Profitability:** Immediately profitable in Month 2
- **Scalability:** Linear (more subscribers = more customers)
- **Time to revenue:** 4-6 weeks (build list, then mail)
- **Risk:** Low (email list building is proven, conversion rates are known)

### Channel 2: Organic Blog Discovery (VIA GUMROAD)

**Context:** Creating 4-6 blog posts SEO-optimized for Gumroad discovery. Assumption: some organic traffic + some SEO leads find products.

#### Unit Economics (Per Customer)

**Traffic & Conversion:**
- Target: 200 unique visitors/month by Month 2 (from 4-6 blog posts + internal linking)
- Conversion to product inquiry: 2% (blog readers are cold, not product-ready)
- Inquiry → Purchase: 20%
- **Net conversions: 200 × 0.02 × 0.20 = 0.8 customers/month**

**Revenue:**
- Price per product: $29
- **Revenue per customer: $29**

**Costs:**
- Blog post creation: 4-6 posts × 5 hours = 25 hours
- At $30/hr labor: 25 × $30 = **$750 upfront cost**
- Hosting/platform: $10/month (blog)
- **Total cost through Month 2: $750 + $20 = $770**

**CAC Calculation (Month 2 basis):**
- Total 2-month cost: $770
- Expected customers in Month 2: 0.8
- **CAC: $770 / 0.8 = $962.50 per customer**

**LTV:**
- **LTV: $30** (same as email channel)

**Unit Economics Summary:**
- **Revenue per customer: $29**
- **CAC: $962.50**
- **LTV: $30**
- **Profit per customer: $29 - $962.50 = -$933.50 (LOSS)**
- **Margin: -3217% (deeply negative)**
- **Breakeven point: Need ~33 customers ($29 × 33 = $960) to recover $750 blog creation cost**

#### Breakeven Timeline
To make the $750 blog investment profitable:
- At 0.8 customers/month (current trajectory): 33 / 0.8 = **41 months to breakeven** ❌
- Even at 2 customers/month (optimistic): 33 / 2 = **16 months to breakeven** ❌
- At 6 customers/month (very optimistic): 33 / 6 = **5-6 months to breakeven** ⚠️

**Key problem:** Blog is a long-tail play. Revenue stays low for 6+ months while the product sits.

#### Channel Viability: ❌ KILL IMMEDIATELY
- **Profitability:** Negative ROI for 6-12 months
- **Time to breakeven:** 5-12 months (too long for recovery goal)
- **Better alternative:** Email list gets to same 10 customers/month in Month 2 with $30/month cost, not $750 upfront
- **Recommendation:** STOP writing blog content for product discovery. Focus on email list building instead.

## PART 2: P2 (CONSULTING/FREELANCE) CHANNEL UNIT ECONOMICS

**Overview:** Service-based revenue from consulting/freelancing. Two models: (1) Consulting for tech project management, (2) Freelance writing for clients.
**Key Finding:** Warm outreach + referrals are profitable. Cold outreach breaks even only at 2+ customers.

### Channel 1: Warm Outreach (NETWORK + DIRECT SALES)

**Context:** Reaching out to past clients, friends, colleagues with a specific service offer (e.g., "I'm offering tech project management for $3K–$5K per project").

#### Unit Economics (Per Customer)

**Traffic & Conversion:**
- Warm network size: 50-100 past connections (from previous work)
- Outreach rate: Send emails to 20-30 warm leads in first sprint
- Response rate: 20-30% (warm audience)
- Qualified leads: 20 × 0.25 = 5 leads
- Discovery call conversion: 40% (warm leads, fit-based selection)
- **Customers closed: 5 × 0.40 = 2 customers**

**Revenue:**
- Average project value: $1,200–$2,500 per engagement
- **Conservative revenue per customer: $1,200**
- Repeat engagement rate: 10-20% (some clients return)
- **LTV includes potential repeats: 1.1-1.2x base value**

**Costs:**
- Outreach + sales time: 20 hours (email sequences, calls, proposals)
- At $40/hr (professional rate): 20 × $40 = **$800**
- Actual cash spent: $0 (email is free)
- **Total labor cost: $800**

**CAC Calculation:**
- **CAC: $800 / 2 = $400 per customer**

**LTV Calculation:**
- Initial project: $1,200
- Repeat engagement (20% conversion): $1,200 × 0.20 = $240
- **Total LTV: $1,200 + $240 = $1,440**

**Unit Economics Summary:**
- **Revenue per customer: $1,200 (initial)**
- **CAC: $400**
- **LTV: $1,440**
- **Profit per customer (initial): $1,200 - $400 = $800**
- **Profit per customer (with repeats): $1,440 - $400 = $1,040**
- **Margin: 67-72%**
- **Return on Ad Spend: 3.0-3.6x**

#### Channel Viability: ✅ PRIMARY CHANNEL
- **Profitability:** Immediately profitable, strong margins
- **Scalability:** Limited by personal capacity (can do 3-5 projects/month max)
- **Time to revenue:** 2-3 weeks (quick sales cycle from warm leads)
- **Risk:** Low (warm leads have context, high close rate)

### Channel 3: Cold Email Outreach

**Context:** Sending cold emails to prospects outside your network (e.g., "I found your company, noticed X problem, can help with Y solution").

#### Unit Economics (Per Customer)

**Traffic & Conversion:**
- Outreach volume: 100 emails per week = 400 per month
- Open rate: 25% (cold email)
- Click/reply rate: 2% (very low for cold)
- Leads qualified: 400 × 0.02 = 8 leads per month
- Qualified lead → Sales call: 25% (many cold leads don't respond to next step)
- Sales calls: 8 × 0.25 = 2 calls
- Call → Closed deal: 25% (typical B2B close rate)
- **Customers closed: 2 × 0.25 = 0.5 customers/month**

**Revenue:**
- Average project value: $1,200
- **Revenue per customer: $1,200**

**Costs:**
- Research + email creation + follow-ups: 20 hours/month
- At $40/hr: 20 × $40 = **$800/month**
- Email tool: $50/month (Lemlist, Outreach, etc.)
- **Total cost: $850/month**

**CAC Calculation:**
- **CAC: $850 / 0.5 = $1,700 per customer**

**Actual worst case:** If only 1 customer closes in 2 months:
- **CAC: $850 × 2 / 1 = $1,700 per customer**

**LTV:**
- **LTV: $1,440** (same as other channels)

**Unit Economics Summary:**
- **Revenue per customer: $1,200**
- **CAC: $1,700**
- **LTV: $1,440**
- **Profit per customer: $1,200 - $1,700 = -$500 (LOSS)**
- **Margin: -42%**
- **ROI: -0.3x** (negative)

#### Breakeven Scenario
To break even on cold outreach:
- Need CAC ≤ LTV
- At current 0.5 customer/month: Need LTV to be $1,700 (unrealistic with $1,200 base)
- To break even: Need 1 customer/month (double current rate)
  - At 1 customer/month: CAC = $850, profit = $1,200 - $850 = $350 (minimal)
- To be profitable: Need 1.5+ customers/month

**Harsh truth:** Cold email needs 2-3x better conversion to break even. Current trajectory is negative.

#### Channel Viability: ❌ KILL FOR RECOVERY GOAL
- **Profitability:** Breakeven at best, negative at most likely scenario
- **Time to breakeven:** 4-6 months (too long for recovery goal)
- **Capital efficiency:** Warm outreach gets 2 customers in 2 weeks at $400 CAC. Cold email gets 1 customer in 2 months at $1,700 CAC.
- **Better alternative:** Spend same 20 hours/month on warm outreach (0 cold email) → 2 customers vs. 0.5 customers
- **Recommendation:** STOP cold email outreach. Redirect effort to warm network expansion and referrals.

## PART 3: P3 (AFFILIATE MARKETING) CHANNEL UNIT ECONOMICS

**Overview:** Earning affiliate commissions from 3 niche websites. Channels: organic SEO, paid ads, email list.
**Key Finding:** Only organic SEO is viable long-term. Paid ads are unprofitable. Email list hasn't been built yet.

### Channel 1: Organic SEO (BLOG CONTENT)

**Context:** Publishing 12+ SEO articles, ranking for 8-12 keywords by Month 6, generating $100-$1,000/mo recurring.

#### Unit Economics (Per Customer/Sale)

**Traffic & Conversion:**
- Target: 5,000 organic visitors/month by Month 6 (across 3 niches)
- Conversion rate (visitor to affiliate signup): 0.5% (affiliate conversion rates are low)
- Affiliate signups: 5,000 × 0.005 = 25 signups/month by Month 6
- Average value per signup: $30 (weighted: some $5 Mailchimp, some $50+ ConvertKit)
- **Revenue per month (Month 6): $750**

**Costs (Upfront Investment):**
- Content creation: 12 articles × 5 hours = 60 hours
- At $30/hr: 60 × $30 = **$1,800 upfront**
- SEO tools (Ahrefs, SemRush): $100/month × 6 = $600
- Hosting: $5-10/month × 6 = $45
- **Total 6-month cost: $2,445**

**Amortized Monthly Cost (M1-M6):**
- $2,445 / 6 = $407.50/month

**CAC Calculation (Month 6 basis):**
- Monthly revenue (M6): $750
- Monthly amortized cost: $407.50
- Customers acquired (M6): 25 affiliates
- **Naive CAC: $2,445 / 25 = $97.80 per signup**
- **But more accurate:** Cost per customer $407.50 / 25 signups = $16.30 (monthly basis)

**LTV Calculation:**
- Initial commission: $30 (one-time)
- Recurring commission (20-30% of customers renew): $30 × 0.25 × 6 months = $45 recurring
- **Total LTV (6-month window): $30 + $45 = $75**
- **Projected annual LTV: $30 + ($30 × 0.25 × 12) = $120+**

**Unit Economics Summary (Month 6):**
- **Revenue per customer: $30** (one-time signup, plus recurring)
- **CAC: $97.80** (upfront basis)
- **LTV: $75** (6-month, conservative)
- **Profit per customer (upfront basis): -$67.80** (Month 6 only)
- **Margin: -226%** (early)

#### The Critical Insight: THIS CHANNEL HAS NEGATIVE EARLY ROI BUT POSITIVE LONG-TERM ROI

**Early months (M1-M3):**
- Revenue: $0-$150
- Cost: $407.50/month
- **Profit: -$407.50 to -$260** ❌ LOSSES EVERY MONTH

**Later months (M4-M6):**
- Revenue: $400-$750
- Cost: $407.50/month (amortized)
- **Profit: -$7.50 to +$342.50** ⚠️ BREAKEVEN → PROFITABLE

**After Month 6 (ongoing):**
- Revenue: $750-$1,500/month (compounding, as more keywords rank)
- Cost: $50-100/month (maintenance, updates)
- **Profit: $650-$1,400** ✅ HIGHLY PROFITABLE

#### Channel Viability: ⚠️ LONG-TERM INVESTMENT (NOT SHORT-TERM RECOVERY)
- **Early profitability:** Negative for 4-5 months
- **Long-term profitability:** +$650-$1,400/month after Month 6
- **Payback period:** 6-8 months
- **Break-even point:** Between Month 4-6
- **Risk:** Market competition, algorithm changes, affiliate program closures
- **Recommendation:** INVEST for Month 4+ payoff. Don't expect revenue in first 3 months.

**Key Decision for 2.5x Strategy:**
- If recovery goal is July 25 (5 months), organic SEO only helps 30-40% ($300-$700 by July)
- If recovery goal extends to August-September, organic SEO becomes major contributor
- **Verdict:** INVEST in organic SEO but don't count on it for Q2 recovery. It's a Q3 multiplier.
