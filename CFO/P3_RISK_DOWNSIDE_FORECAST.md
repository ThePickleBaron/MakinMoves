## CFO Decision Framework: P3 Downside Risk

### If P3 Follows REALISTIC Path (Most Likely)
- Recovery delayed by 1 month (Month 7 vs. Month 6)
- Monthly run rate by Month 8: $7,660 (blended)
- 6-month total: $9,425 (94% of target) — essentially on plan

**CFO action:** No change. Realistic scenario is still on-track for recovery.

### If P3 Follows PESSIMISTIC Path (Worst Case)
- Recovery delayed by 2 months (Month 8 vs. Month 6)
- Monthly run rate by Month 8: $4,546 (blended)
- 6-month total: $5,526 (55% of target) — below plan

**CFO action required:**
1. Accelerate P1 or P2 if possible
2. Extend recovery timeline from 6 months to 8 months
3. Monitor P3 metrics monthly; pivot if Month 3-4 shows <200 visitors

## Monthly Metrics to Track (For CFO Monitoring)

### Key Indicators of P3 Health

**Month 1-2:**
- Website traffic: Target >50 total visitors (across all 3 sites)
- Affiliate signups: Target >1-2 per month
- Revenue: Target >$50

**Month 3:**
- Website traffic: Target >300+ visitors
- Affiliate signups: Target >3-5 per month
- Revenue: Target >$200-300

**Month 4:**
- Website traffic: Target >700+ visitors (if on realistic path)
- Affiliate signups: Target >10+ per month
- Revenue: Target >$400+

**If actual is <50% of targets:**
- Month 1-2: Content/SEO issue, investigate keywords
- Month 3: SEO timing off, reassess ranking difficulty
- Month 4: May need keyword pivot or additional content

## Bottom Line for CFO

1. **Optimistic scenario is unlikely** — Industry data suggests 6-12 month ranking timelines, not 3-4
2. **Realistic scenario is manageable** — Delays recovery by 1 month only
3. **Pessimistic scenario is still viable** — Delays recovery by 2 months, but doesn't break model
4. **Blended portfolio (P1+P2+P3) absorbs P3 risk** — Even if P3 underperforms, P1+P2 can cover
5. **Monthly monitoring is critical** — Flag P3 underperformance by Month 3-4 to pivot early

**Recommendation:** Approve P3 as planned, but track metrics monthly and be ready to reallocate resources to P1/P2 if P3 underperforms vs. realistic scenario by Month 4.
