## Decision Tree: When to Pivot or Double Down

### Week 1 Decision Points (By Day 7)

**If total revenue < $100 (Below Conservative):**
- Action: Review product quality + positioning
- Week 1 is discovery phase; margins for error are high
- Recommendation: No pivot yet; assess competitor positioning, tweak copy
- If still $0 by Day 3: Escalate to CEO for niche/product validation

**If total revenue $100–400 (Conservative → Realistic):**
- Action: On track. Continue current execution.
- Recommendation: Minor optimization only (copy tweaks, 1 more design, 1 more article)

**If total revenue > $400 (Above Realistic):**
- Action: Consider adding ad spend budget
- Recommendation: Test $20–50/week ad budget on highest-performing stream
- CFO approval: YES, if ROI is 2:1+ and runway allows

---

### Week 2 Decision Points (By Day 14)

**Cumulative revenue < $600 (Below Conservative trajectory):**
- Action: Identify stuck stream
- Recommendation: If Digital Products stuck, review template quality + placement. If PoD stuck, increase design uploads + platform diversity. If Affiliate stuck, review article SEO + email strategy.
- Escalation: To CEO for stream pivot decision

**Cumulative revenue $600–$2,500 (Conservative-Realistic range):**
- Action: On track for Day 90 goal. Maintain effort.
- Recommendation: Begin scaling highest-performing stream (more designs, more templates, more articles)

**Cumulative revenue > $2,500 (Above Realistic):**
- Action: Accelerate execution + consider ad spend
- Recommendation: Deploy $50–100/week ad budget on highest-ROI stream
- CFO approval: YES; project shows strong return

---

### Week 3 Decision Points (By Day 21)

**Total revenue < $1,500 (Below Conservative):**
- Action: Assess fundamental viability
- Options:
  - Pivot one stream (highest-growth niche shift)
  - Combine streams (e.g., affiliate + digital product bundle)
  - Escalate to CEO for recovery goal recalibration
- Timeline: Decision by Day 24; pivot execution starts Day 25

**Total revenue $1,500–$5,000 (Conservative-Realistic range):**
- Action: On track for $10k goal (6–8 months)
- Recommendation: Scale winning stream; maintain others; prepare for Phase 2 (automation, email list growth, content depth)

**Total revenue > $5,000 (Above Realistic, trending Optimistic):**
- Action: Aggressive scaling
- Recommendations:
  - Deploy $100–200/week ad spend
  - Hire freelancer for design/content (1–2 hours/week)
  - Target $500+/week revenue by Day 45
- CFO approval: YES; project runway extended

## CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) & LTV (Lifetime Value) Analysis

### Digital Products

**Assumptions:**
- CAC (paid): $2–5 per customer (via ads or organic social)
- CAC (organic): $0 (word-of-mouth, SEO, launch hype)
- Average order value: $25–50
- Repeat purchase rate: 20–30% (bundle buys, future products)
- Payback period (paid CAC): 1–2 weeks

**Lifetime Value Calculation (Conservative):**
- Initial purchase: $25–50 LTV
- 1st repeat (25% of customers): $12 additional LTV
- 2nd repeat (10% of repeat): $5 additional LTV
- **Total LTV: $37–67 per customer**
- **CAC payback:** 1–3 weeks (strong unit economics)

**Repeat Purchase Rate Factors:**
- Avg customer acquires 1 product now, 1–2 in future
- Bundle offers can increase LTV by 40%
- Email list retention: ~5–10% open rate with 1–2 promotions/month

---

### Print-on-Demand

**Assumptions:**
- CAC (organic/platform): $0 (Redbubble/Merch algorithm does marketing)
- CAC (paid ads): $3–8 per order
- Average order value: $15–25
- Net per order: $7–12
- Repeat purchase rate: 5–10% (brand recognition)
- Payback period: 1–3 months for paid ads

**Lifetime Value Calculation (Conservative):**
- Initial purchase: $10 LTV (net margin)
- Repeat customers (7% of base): $0.70 additional LTV
- Average customer lifetime: 2–3 designs per niche
- **Total LTV: $11–14 per customer**
- **CAC payback (organic):** Immediate (free marketing)
- **CAC payback (paid at $5 CAC):** 2–3 months

**Challenges:**
- PoD has lower LTV than Digital Products
- Organic growth is slow but cheap
- Paid growth can be profitable but requires volume

---

### Affiliate Marketing

**Assumptions:**
- CAC (content): $0 (organic SEO)
- CAC (email): $0.50–$2 per subscriber (if purchased lists)
- Average conversion rate: 2–5%
- Commission per sale: $1–$100 (varies by product)
- Email list value: $1–$5 per subscriber per year
- Payback period: 4–12 weeks

**Lifetime Value Calculation (Conservative):**
- Email subscriber (non-purchaser): $1–5 LTV (future promotions)
- Email subscriber (purchaser): $10–50 LTV (multiple recommendations)
- Organic article visitor: $0.50–$5 LTV (long-tail monetization)
- **Total LTV per subscriber: $2–15**
- **CAC payback (organic):** 8–12 weeks
- **CAC payback (paid list):** 4–8 weeks (if list quality good)

**Optimization:**
- Build owned audience (email) first; monetize later
- Week 1–3: Focus on subscriber growth (target 50+)
- Week 4–8: Begin monetization (affiliate promotions, sponsored content)
