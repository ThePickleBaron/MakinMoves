## CFO Contingencies

### If Revenue Falls Below Conservative

**Day 3–7:** No action; discovery phase
**Day 10:** Review product quality + positioning
**Day 14:** Escalate to CEO for niche validation
**Day 21:** Pivot decision required

### If One Stream Goes to Zero

**Action:** Immediate COO/CEO discussion
**Options:** (A) Fix setup issue, (B) Pause stream, (C) Pivot niche
**Deadline:** Decision by Day 21

### If Cash Burns Faster Than Expected

**Trigger:** Daily costs exceed daily revenue for 3+ days
**Action:** Freeze non-essential spend; escalate
**Fallback:** Reduce ad spend or pause new platform sign-ups

### If Payment Processor Fails

**Trigger:** Payout delayed >2 days or account flagged
**Action:** Switch to backup method (PayPal); document issue
**Escalation:** Alert CTMO + CEO if not resolved in 4 hours
