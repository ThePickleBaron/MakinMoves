## THREE CORE FINDINGS

### Finding 1: P1 (Digital Products) Hits Ceiling by Month 6
- Niche market size: ~500-1,000 annual buyers max
- Repeat purchase: 30-60% (doesn't compound)
- Revenue trajectory: $250 M1 → $650 M2 → $1,000 M3 → $1,500 M6 → $2,000 M12 (plateau)
- Effort to scale: Each new product has lower ROI (diminishing returns)
- Real ROI: $25/hour M1-3, $94/hour M6 average, but monthly rate stalling by M6

### Finding 2: P2 (Freelance) Hits Labor Ceiling by Month 6
- You can deliver max 65 hours/month
- That's 5 clients × 13 hours/month each
- Can't scale beyond $3-5K/month without hiring
- Hiring erases margin (need $2K+ clients to justify contractor cost)
- Real ROI: $0/hour M1 (sales), $20/hour M2-3, $46/hour M6, then capped

### Finding 3: P3 (Affiliate) Doesn't Plateau Until Year 2+
- M1-2: Zero revenue (content investment)
- M3-4: Inflection point ($100-300 first commissions)
- M6: $1,500-2,000/month (traffic compounding)
- M12: $5,000-10,000/month (still accelerating)
- Year 2: $10,000-20,000/month (passive, no ceiling)
- Real ROI: $0/hour M1-2, $10/hour M6 average, $114-171/hour by M12, then improves further
