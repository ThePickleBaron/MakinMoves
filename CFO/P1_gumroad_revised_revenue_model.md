## SCENARIO SELECTION FRAMEWORK

- Recommendation: **Do NOT plan cash flow expecting this.** This is fallback.

**OPTION B: Base Case (RECOMMENDED for planning)**
- Use if: Email list 200-500 built before launch OR Twitter 500+ OR Medium articles queued
- Probability: 50%
- Timeline: Month 1 shows $50-300; Month 2 builds momentum
- Recommendation: **Use this for baseline cash flow projections.**

**OPTION C: Optimistic / Upside Case**
- Use if: Email list 500+, Twitter 2K+, multiple traffic sources active
- Probability: 20%
- Timeline: Month 1 shows $200-500; strong growth trajectory
- Recommendation: Use for upside scenarios; show to board for motivation.

## REVISED ASSUMPTION: EXTERNAL TRAFFIC IS PREREQUISITE

### Critical Finding
**30% of Gumroad product failures are due to zero traffic, not product quality.**

Original CEO model implied products would find audience via Gumroad algorithm. Market reality: Gumroad Discover only amplifies products with existing external traction.

### Traffic Source Contribution Model

| Traffic Source | Contribution to Revenue | Build Timeline | Effort | Ongoing |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Email List (Warm Launch)** | 40-60% Month 1 revenue | 4-8 weeks before launch | Medium (email sequence) | Daily sends |
| **Twitter/X (Community)** | 20-30% Month 1-3 | 8-12 weeks OR 2K+ existing | Medium (daily posts) | Daily engagement |
| **Medium Articles (Organic)** | 10-20% ongoing | 2-3 weeks (3-5 articles) | Low (1-2 hrs each) | Monthly updates |
| **Reddit (Spike Traffic)** | 0-20% (volatile) | On-demand (timing-dependent) | Low (1 good post) | Monitor, reply |
| **YouTube (Long-tail)** | 10-30% (if audience exists) | 12+ weeks OR pre-built | High | Monthly content |
| **Gumroad Discover (Organic)** | 0-10% Month 1-2 | Automatic (if other traction) | None | Automatic |

**Total Revenue Dependency:** 70-90% of early revenue comes from external traffic, NOT from Gumroad discovery.

## RISK ADJUSTMENTS TO REVENUE FORECAST

### If Traffic Strategy NOT Executed
- Adjust Base Case down 70% → Becomes ~$0-300 Month 1-2
- Model becomes Downside Case
- Recovery timeline extends to Month 4-5
- **Action:** Flag this immediately to CEO; course-correct by Day 30.

### If Wrong Niche Selected (Photography/Gaming Instead of Writing)
- Adjust revenue down 50-60%
- Base Case becomes: $2K-6K (6-month) instead of $4.3K-11K
- **Action:** Pre-validate with 20 beta customers before launch.

### If Email List Building Delayed
- Adjust Month 1-2 down 60%
- Each 100 email subscribers lost = ~$150-300 Month 1 impact
- **Action:** Start email building immediately; don't wait for product completion.

### If Competitor Launches Similar Product
- Adjust revenue down 30% (market splits)
- Timing is critical; first-mover advantage worth 30% premium
- **Action:** Accelerate launch timeline; move from "perfect" to "good enough" if needed.
