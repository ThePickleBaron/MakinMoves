## DETAILED SCENARIO MODELING

### SCENARIO A: P1 FAILS (Shadowban + Low Conversion)

**Trigger:** Gumroad shadowban + white-label both fail to gain visibility

**Assumptions:**
- P1 shadowban probability: 40%
- If shadowban: visibility cut 75% (not total ban, but algorithmic throttle)
- White-label conversion: 0.3% (not 1.5%)
- Combined impact: P1 revenue = $0-500 M1-6 (vs $10,530 optimal)

**Cascade Effect on P2:**
- P1 email list growth: 0 (no P1 opt-ins to feed P2 warm list)
- P2 email list by M2: 50-100 (only external growth, slow)
- P2 warm conversion: 50% of forecast ($6,000 vs $12,000)
- P2 cold outreach: Required earlier (forces CEO pivot)

**Total Impact:**
- P1 revenue M1-6: $500
- P2 revenue (warm stalls): $6,000
- P3 unaffected: $3,000
- **M1-6 total: $9,500 (vs $50,000 optimal, -81% miss)**

**Mitigation:**
- White-label was backup, can pivot Day 1 (no sunk cost)
- Cold outreach fallback for P2 (lower ROI but covers)
- Cost preservation: Cut burn to $350/mo, extends runway

**Recovery Path:**
- Day 1-3: Kill P1 frameworks, launch 2-3 white-label test templates
- Day 4-7: Ramp cold P2 outreach (CEO manual effort)
- By M2: P1 stabilizes at $200-400/mo, P2 at $600-1,200 (cold-based)
- **Revised M1-6: $15,000 (vs $9,500 crisis, +58% recovery)**

### SCENARIO B: EMAIL LIST MISSES (Growth Stalls Below 300)

**Trigger:** External traffic generation fails, P1 opt-ins weak

- By M3: 350 (vs 750)

**Impact on P2 Warm:**

- P2 warm revenue M2: $0 (launch delayed to M3)
- P2 cold forced immediately: Ramps to $600-1,200 M2

**Impact on P1:**

- P3 unaffected: $3,000

**Mitigation:**
- P2 pivot to cold-first (planned as backup)

- Referral program (free traffic source, compensates for email weakness)

**Recovery Path:**

- M3: Warm + cold hybrid, combined P2 revenue $2,000+
- **Revised M1-6: $32,000 (vs $22,000 crisis, +45% recovery)**

### SCENARIO C: CEO BURNOUT (Weeks 3-4 Capacity Wall)

**Trigger:** CEO hits 100+ hours/week, quality drops

**Assumptions:**
- Week 1-2: 60 hours/week, high quality (on plan)
- Week 3-4: 100+ hours/week burnout kicks in
- Quality drops: Pitch weaker (-30% cold response), writing slower (-50% blog output), customer support ignored

**Cascade Effect:**
- P2 cold email: Weaker pitch = response rate 1% (vs 2%), close rate 5% (vs 10%)
  - Result: M2-3 P2 revenue cut 60%
- P1 organic: Blog output slows, only 2 articles posted instead of 4
  - Result: P1 organic revenue delayed to M4 (vs M3)
- P1 customer support: Ignored, creates negative reviews, -10% repeat purchase
  - Result: P1 repeat purchase drops from 20% to 10%

**Total Impact:**
- P1 revenue: $8,000 (vs $10,530, -24%)
- P2 revenue: $6,000 (vs $15,000, -60%)
- P3 revenue: $1,000 (vs $3,000, -67%, blog slowed)
- **M1-6 total: $15,000 (vs $50,000 optimal, -70% miss)**

**Mitigation:**
- CEO capacity cap: 60 hours/week, non-negotiable
- Hire part-time writer for blog ($800/month, 20 articles) — frees CEO 40 hours
- Delegate cold email to COO (if available) — frees CEO 20 hours

**Recovery Path:**
- M2: Implement writer hire ($800 spend)
- M3: CEO back to <60 hours/week, quality recovers
- M4: Blog accelerates, P1 organic revenue kicks in
- **Revised M1-6: $30,000 (vs $15,000 crisis, +100% recovery with $800 investment)**

### SCENARIO D: MARKET SATURATION (Competitor Launch)

**Trigger:** By M2, competitor launches Creator Toolkit at $9

**Assumptions:**
- Competitor has 10K Twitter followers (vs CEO 0)
- Competitor pricing $9 (vs CEO $39)
- Your $39 price can't compete
- Your conversion drops 70% (not 30%)
- Email-only viable channel (paid/organic traffic lost to competitor)

**Impact:**
- P1 revenue: $2,000 (vs $10,530, -81%)
- P2: Unaffected, $15,000 (warm email still works)
- P3: Unaffected, $3,000
- **M1-6 total: $20,000 (vs $50,000 optimal, -60% miss)**

**Mitigation:**
- Differentiate: Position as "frameworks + done-with-you" (not commodity templates)
- Bundle strategy: 3-framework bundle at $39 (vs $9 competitor single framework)
- Raise prices: If conversion drops 70%, consider $49-69 price to recover revenue
  - At $49: Conversion 0.5%, 1.2K units, revenue $5,880 (beats $2K)
- Email-first: Focus on warm email (only advantage over competitor with no list)

**Recovery Path:**
- Week 1: Reposition messaging ("Done-with-You" vs "DIY Templates")
- Week 2: Implement bundle pricing $49 (less units, higher revenue)
- Week 3: Email becomes primary channel (only advantage)
- **Revised M1-6: $25,000 (vs $20,000 crisis, +25% recovery via price increase)**

**Mitigation:**

- If migrations fail: Fundraise $2,000 bridge by April 1

**Recovery Path:**
- Pre-March 31: Complete all migrations, verify working
- Confirm new burn: $176/mo (actual, not theoretical)
- If confirmed: Runway extends to 170+ days (safe)
- If failed: Requires external funding (fatal without)
