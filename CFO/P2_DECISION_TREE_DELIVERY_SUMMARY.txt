ADVANTAGE ANALYSIS:
  + Hybrid has $525 higher expected value than warm-first
  + Hybrid has 8x lower worst-case probability than warm-first (5% vs 40%)
  + Hybrid has no single point of failure (dual paths insure each other)
  + Hybrid generates earlier revenue (April 21 cold close + May 15 warm close)
  + Hybrid allows testing both channels before committing to winner

WHY NOT WARM-FIRST?
  - Email list must hit 500 by April 1 (only 3 days, 15% probability)
  - If email list fails (40% probability), entire strategy fails and pivots late
  - Forced cold pivot loses 2-3 weeks of revenue
  - Audience quality uncertain (template buyers not equal service buyers)

WHY NOT COLD-FIRST?
  - CEO cold execution uncertain (depends on prior experience)
  - Misses upside from warm channel if email list actually grows
  - No backup if cold campaign underperforms
  - Cannot test which channel works better
