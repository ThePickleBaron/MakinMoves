## UNIT ECONOMICS BY STREAM (At Month 4 volumes)

### P1 GUMROAD — Digital Products

**Baseline (Month 4 forecast):**
- Revenue: $1,200/mo
- Transactions: ~60 (at $20 AOV)
- Unit cost: 2% Gumroad fee = $0.40/transaction
- Fixed cost allocation: $36 (infra + tools)
- **Net margin per transaction: $19.60** (98%)

**Price elasticity test (Month 1-2):**
- Test 1: $19 price point
  - Expected conversion: 2% (500 visits → 10 sales = $190)
- Test 2: $29 price point
  - Expected conversion: 1.5% (500 visits → 7.5 sales = $217.50)
  - **Better revenue despite lower conversion**
- Test 3: $49 price point
  - Expected conversion: 0.8% (500 visits → 4 sales = $196)
  - **Worse revenue due to conversion cliff**

**Recommendation:** Optimize to $29-39 range (highest revenue, still >1% conversion)

**Marketing ROI (Month 3-4 scenario):**
- Assume 100 visits/day organic (from email list seeding)
- At 1% conversion: 30 sales/mo × $30 = $900/mo
- At $500 paid traffic spend: +200 visits/day = +60 sales = +$1,800 revenue
- **ROI: 360% (3.6x return on $500 spend)**

**Scaling economics:**
- Current cost per customer: ~$0.40 (Gumroad fee)
- Customer LTV: ~$30 (one-time purchase, limited repeat)
- CAC (organic): $0 (no spend yet)
- CAC (paid): $8-10 per customer (at $500 spend for 60 customers)
- **Payback period (paid):** <1 month (LTV $30 >> CAC $10)

**Strategic implication for CEO:**
- ✅ P1 is highest-margin stream, best for paid acceleration
- ✅ Should get 60% of early marketing budget
- ✅ Optimize price to $29-39 (not lower)
- ✅ Email list growth is CRITICAL lever (free traffic)

### P2 FREELANCE WRITING — Service

**Baseline (Month 4 forecast):**
- Revenue: $3,600/mo (3 clients @ $1,200/mo avg)
- Cost per client: ~$250/mo (email, CRM, scheduling tools after cuts)
- Revenue cost: 2.9% Stripe + $0.30/txn (bundled into $250 estimate)
- Fixed cost allocation: $50 (shared infra)
- **Net margin per client: $950/mo** (93% on direct, 26% on all-in)

**Sales cycle analysis (Critical metric):**
- Cold email conversion: 1-2% (need 500 emails for 5-10 leads, 1-2 close)
- Warm conversion from P1: 5-10% (need 50 emails for 5 leads, 2-3 close)
- **Warm is 5-10x better, but slower cycle (2-4 weeks vs 1 week cold)**

**Customer acquisition cost:**
- Cold outreach: $0 (internal effort, ~5 hrs/client = $50 value) = $50 CAC
- Warm outreach: $0 (already paid for P1 email list) = $0 CAC
- **Warm CAC is 5x lower if P1 succeeds**

**Customer lifetime value:**
- Assumed 3-month retention (typical for freelance writers)
- LTV = $1,200 × 3 = $3,600
- CAC payback: 1 month (cold), <1 month (warm)

**Marketing ROI (Month 3-4 scenario):**
- Assume P1 list has 500+ warm subscribers
- Email warm sequence: 500 list × 5% conversion = 25 leads
- Close rate: 10% = 2-3 paying clients = $2,400-3,600/mo additional
- Spend: $50 (email + landing page, mostly time)
- **ROI: 4,800-7,200% (if warm conversion works)**

**But if warm stalls (email opt-in <60% or sequence weak):**
- Fallback to cold outreach: Need $2,000 spend (50 hours @ $40/hr effort)
- For same 3 clients: 1,500 cold emails needed (expensive effort)
- **ROI collapses to 180% (not great)**

**Strategic implication for CEO:**
- ⚠️ P2 is HIGH-margin but MEDIUM-risk
- ✅ DEPENDS entirely on P1 email list success
- ✅ Cold outreach fallback exists but expensive (effort cost)
- ✅ Should get 30% of marketing budget (after P1)
- ⚠️ IF P1 email list <300 by April 1, reduce P2 targets to cold-only

   - Could raise AOV from $30 to $45
   - No additional cost (leverage existing template)
   - Action: Add 2-3 upsells in Month 2

### P2 Potential Leaks:
1. **Delivery cost creep:** Assumed $250/mo tool cost, but might be higher
   - Email: $0 (Mailchimp free)
   - CRM: $0 (HubSpot free)
   - Scheduling: $0 (Calendly free)
   - Stripe: $0.30 + 2.9% per txn (built-in, $3/mo avg at $100/mo revenue)
   - Actual: ~$3/mo (vs $250 forecast) — **HUGE overestimate!**
   - Revised margin: 99% (not 93%)
   - Action: Update model immediately

2. **Service delivery efficiency:** Assumed 10 hours/client/month
   - If actual is 15 hours: margin drops to 60%
   - If actual is 5 hours: margin rises to 200%+
   - Action: Track time per client in Month 1, optimize workflow

3. **Cold email CAC:** Assumed $50 effort cost
   - If 100 cold emails to land 1 client = $150-200 CAC (vs. $50)

### P3 Potential Leaks:
1. **Content recycling inefficiency:** Are we writing 3 different articles per week (9/week total)?

   - If separate: 9 articles = $450/mo content cost (built into P2)
   - If recycled: 4 articles = $200/mo content cost
   - Action: Clarify content strategy with CEO (recycled is better for margins)

2. **Affiliate program fee structure:** Currently $20-40/commission
   - Some programs pay 5% (Shareasale), others 15% (CJ Affiliate)
   - Expected: $30 avg commission
   - Action: Audit which niches use which programs, shift to higher-payout networks
