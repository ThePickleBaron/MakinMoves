## P1 CREATOR TOOLKIT: CHANNEL ECONOMICS

### CHANNEL 1: Email List (Warm, from P1 purchasers)

**Traffic Source:** P1 customer emails (opt-in from P1 purchase)
- P1 sales M1-6: ~240 units (at $39 bundle price)
- Email opt-in rate: 40% = 96 subscribers
- Email list grows from P1: 96 subscribers by end M1-6

**Conversion Funnel:**
- List size: 96 by M2
- Open rate: 40% (warm list, 40-50 is typical for engaged)
- Click rate (click to product page): 5% of opens
- Monthly emails sent: 4 (1 per week intro sequence)
- Clicks: 96 × 40% × 5% × 4 = 7.7 clicks/month
- Purchase conversion (clicks to buyers): 10%
- Monthly buyers from email: 0.77 ≈ 1/month

**Economics:**
- Revenue per sale: $39
- Monthly revenue (from P1 email): $39
- Effort to maintain: Email sequences written once ($50 labor), then automated
- Ongoing cost: $0 (free email tier)
- **CAC: $50 / 1 = $50 amortized**
- **LTV: $39 (single purchase assumed)**
- **Unit economics: -$11 per customer (NEGATIVE)**

**But:** This ignores repeat purchase (framework buyers may buy more frameworks)
- With 20% repeat purchase: LTV = $39 × 1.2 = $47
- **Adjusted unit economics: -$3 (still negative)**

**Conclusion:** Email channel is negative ROI initially, but subsidized by initial email list (built from P1 sales, not paid acquisition). Gets better if repeat purchase exceeds 30%.

### CHANNEL 2: Blog/Organic SEO

**Traffic Source:** Google search, blog content
- Traffic generated by 4-6 blog posts by M3
- Expected traffic: 100-200 UU/day (50-100 from organic)
- Monthly organic traffic: 1,500-3,000 UU

**Conversion Funnel:**
- Monthly traffic: 2,000 UU (mid-range estimate)
- Conversion rate: 1.5% (organic traffic is lower intent than email)
- Monthly buyers: 30
- Monthly revenue: 30 × $39 = $1,170

**Economics:**
- Blog production: 4-6 posts × 5 hours × $30/hr labor = $600-900 upfront
- Ongoing maintenance: 1 post/month × 5 hours × $30 = $150/month ongoing
- **Upfront CAC: $600 / 30 = $20/customer (M1-3 amortized)**
- **Ongoing CAC: $150 / 30 = $5/customer (M4+)**
- **LTV: $39**
- **Unit economics M1-3: $39 - $20 = +$19 (positive)**
- **Unit economics M4+: $39 - $5 = +$34 (much better)**

**Conclusion:** Organic is positive ROI after M3. Invest now, payoff later. This is the long-term compounding asset.

## P2 FREELANCE SERVICES: CHANNEL ECONOMICS

### CHANNEL 1: Warm Email (P1 list)

**Traffic Source:** Email to P1 customer list
- List size by M2: 100-150 (from P1 opt-ins + external growth)
- Open rate: 40%
- Click rate (to landing page): 5%
- Inquiry conversion: 10%
- Closes: 10% of inquiries
- Expected closes M2: 0.75 clients

**Economics:**
- Email sequence cost: 4 hours × $40/hr = $160
- Discovery call time: 1 hour × $40/hr = $40
- Closing time: 2 hours × $40/hr = $80
- **Total effort: 7 hours = $280 CAC**
- **Revenue per close: $1,200**
- **LTV: $1,200 (single engagement, minimal repeat)**
- **Unit economics: $1,200 - $280 = +$920 (77% margin)**
- **RoR: 4.3x**

**Conclusion:** Warm email is the best P2 channel. Primary strategy.

### CHANNEL 2: Cold Email Outreach

**Traffic Source:** Outbound cold email
- Volume: 100 emails/week = 400/month
- Response rate: 2% = 8 responses
- Inquiry conversion: 20% = 1.6 qualified leads
- Close rate: 10% = 0.16 closes/month

**Economics:**
- Research + email writing: 20 hours/month × $40/hr = $800
- Closing time: 2 hours × $40/hr = $80 (per close)
- **Total CAC: ($800 + $80) / 0.16 = $5,500 per close**
- **Revenue per close: $1,200**
- **Unit economics: $1,200 - $5,500 = -$4,300 (NEGATIVE)**
- **RoR: 0.22x (disaster)**

**Conclusion:** Cold email is uneconomical at current response rates. Need 5%+ response rate to break even. Kill until you can improve pitch or warm up list first.
