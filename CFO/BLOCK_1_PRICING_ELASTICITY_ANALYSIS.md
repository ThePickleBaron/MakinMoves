## P1 CREATOR TOOLKIT: 4-PRICE-POINT DEMAND CURVE

**Scenario Setup:**
- 500-email list by M2
- External traffic: 100-200 UU/day (from blog + organic)
- Monthly traffic: ~3,000-6,000 UU
- Baseline conversion (industry): 2.8%

**Price Point Analysis:**

### Price Point 1: $9 (Aggressive, volume play)
- WTP assumption: "Affordable, low-risk purchase"
- Expected conversion rate: 3.5% (highest volume)
- Monthly customers (3,000 UU × 3.5%): 105
- Monthly revenue: 105 × $9 = $945
- Gumroad net (after 2.2% fee): $924
- Margin: 97%
- **M1-6 revenue: ~$5,700 (scaling from $300 M1 to $945 M6)**

### Price Point 2: $19 (Balanced, sweet spot candidate)
- WTP assumption: "Good value, templates + frameworks included"
- Expected conversion rate: 2.0% (balanced)
- Monthly customers (3,000 UU × 2.0%): 60
- Monthly revenue: 60 × $19 = $1,140
- Gumroad net: $1,115
- Margin: 97%
- **M1-6 revenue: ~$6,840 (scaling from $200 M1 to $1,140 M6)**

### Price Point 3: $29 (Current plan, premium positioning)
- WTP assumption: "Premium frameworks, differentiated IP"
- Expected conversion rate: 1.2% (lower volume, higher quality)
- Monthly customers (3,000 UU × 1.2%): 36
- Monthly revenue: 36 × $29 = $1,044
- Gumroad net: $1,021
- Margin: 97%
- **M1-6 revenue: ~$6,264 (scaling from $125 M1 to $1,044 M6)**

### Price Point 4: $49 (Premium, high-intent)
- WTP assumption: "Advanced frameworks, done-with-you support"
- Expected conversion rate: 0.5% (very selective)
- Monthly customers (3,000 UU × 0.5%): 15
- Monthly revenue: 15 × $49 = $735
- Gumroad net: $719
- Margin: 97%
- **M1-6 revenue: ~$4,410 (scaling from $50 M1 to $735 M6)**

**Revenue Optimization Result:**

| Price | Monthly Customers | Monthly Revenue | M1-6 Total | Winner? |
|-------|------------------|-----------------|-----------|---------|
| $9 | 105 | $945 | $5,700 | 2nd |
| $19 | 60 | $1,140 | **$6,840** | **🟢 OPTIMAL** |
| $29 | 36 | $1,044 | $6,264 | 3rd |
| $49 | 15 | $735 | $4,410 | 4th |

**Decision: Launch Creator Toolkit at $19, not $29**
- $19 maximizes revenue ($6,840 vs $6,264 at $29)
- Higher conversion (2% vs 1.2%) provides better validation signal
- Volume allows faster customer testimonial gathering (60 vs 36)
- Can always test $29 later via A/B test (keep $19 as control)

## BUNDLE PRICING STRATEGY (Multi-Framework Approach)

**Option 1: Single Framework at Price Point**
- 1 framework (e.g., "Copywriting Fundamentals"): $19
- Revenue from 60 customers: $1,140/month

**Option 2: Bundle of 3 Frameworks**
- 3 frameworks (Copywriting + Email + Funnel): $39 (32% discount vs $57 separate)
- Expected conversion: 1.5% (lower than single, bundle adds friction)
- Monthly customers (3,000 UU × 1.5%): 45
- Monthly revenue: 45 × $39 = $1,755
- **Bundle outperforms single by +$615/month (+54%)**

**Option 3: Bundle of 5 Frameworks (Complete Toolkit)**
- 5 frameworks (all methods): $59 (34% discount vs $95 separate)
- Expected conversion: 0.8% (bundle complexity, high commitment)
- Monthly customers (3,000 UU × 0.8%): 24
- Monthly revenue: 24 × $59 = $1,416
- **Bundle underperforms 3-bundle by -$339/month (-19%)**

**Recommendation: Launch with 3-Framework Bundle at $39**
- Maximizes revenue ($1,755 vs $1,140 single, $1,416 5-bundle)
- Sweet spot between price and conversion
- Gives customer more value (3 frameworks for 2x price = perceived value)
- Easier to explain: "Complete System" vs individual frameworks
- **M1-6 revenue: ~$10,530 (vs $6,840 single, +54%)**

## VALUE-BASED PRICING (Outcome-Focused)

**Current Model (Feature-Based):**
- "Copywriting Framework Bundle": $39
- Customer buys because: "I want to learn copywriting"
- Perceived value: ~$39 (matches price)
- Conversion: 1.5%

**Value-Based Model (Outcome-Focused):**
- "Turn Your Freelance Skills Into $3K/Month Revenue System": $79
- Customer buys because: "I want to make $3K/month"
- Perceived value: $500+ (outcome worth much more)
- Expected conversion: 0.6% (higher price, more selectivity)
- Monthly customers: 18
- Monthly revenue: 18 × $79 = $1,422

**Comparison:**

| Model | Price | Conversion | Monthly Revenue | M1-6 Total |
|-------|-------|-----------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Feature-Based (Bundle)** | $39 | 1.5% | $1,755 | $10,530 |
| **Value-Based (Outcome)** | $79 | 0.6% | $1,422 | $8,532 |

**Result: Feature-based bundle wins on revenue**

**But:** Value-based attracts higher-intent customers (smaller audience, but better fit)
- Recommendation: Test both (use value-based for retargeting, bundle for new traffic)
- Keep primary offer at $39 (feature), secondary at $79 (value)
- Offer bundle upsell: "Upgrade from Framework to Complete System" ($39 → $79)

## P2 FREELANCE PRICING (Service)

**Current Model: Flat $1,200 per engagement**

**4-Price-Point Test (Tiered Service):**

### Tier 1: Quick Start ($500)
- 4 discovery calls, 1 email sequence, 2-week turnaround
- Expected close rate: 40% (high intent, low friction)
- Lead volume: 100 cold leads, 5 warm leads = 105 total
- Closes: 42
- Revenue: 42 × $500 = $21,000
- **M2-6 cumulative: ~$35,000**

### Tier 2: Full Engagement ($1,200, current)
- 6 discovery calls, full strategy, 4-week delivery
- Expected close rate: 10% (medium friction)
- Closes: 10-12
- Revenue: 10-12 × $1,200 = $12,000-14,400
- **M2-6 cumulative: ~$45,000**

### Tier 3: Premium ($2,500)
- Unlimited calls, 12-week retainer, implementation support
- Expected close rate: 2% (high friction, only serious clients)
- Closes: 2-3
- Revenue: 2-3 × $2,500 = $5,000-7,500
- **M2-6 cumulative: ~$30,000**

**Tiered Strategy (Offer All Three):**
- Tier 1 captures price-sensitive customers (low CAC, high volume)
- Tier 2 captures mainstream (best ROI)
- Tier 3 captures premium (highest LTV, but rare)
- Blended revenue: $21k (T1) + $12k (T2) + $5k (T3) = $38,000 M2-6

**Recommendation: Launch with Tier 2 only ($1,200), add Tier 1 ($500) in M3**
- Start with sweet spot (1.2x better revenue than tiered approach)
- Tier 1 later captures price-sensitive leads that accumulate over time
- Keep simple for CEO (negotiate once, not 3 variants)
