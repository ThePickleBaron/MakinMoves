## Budget Approval Thresholds

### Tier 1: Bootstrap ($0–100/mo revenue)
- **Approved:** Free platforms, domain only
- **Cost:** $12/year (domain)
- **Tools:** Gumroad, Beehiiv (free), Redbubble, Medium, Etsy
- **Status:** ACTIVE NOW

### Tier 2: Traction ($100–500/mo revenue) — UNLOCKS AT $300+/mo sustained 2 weeks
- **Approved for evaluation:**
  - Railway SaaS hosting: $5–50/mo (ROI threshold: $75/mo new revenue)
  - Ghost CMS: $29/mo (ROI threshold: $87/mo new revenue)
  - Buffer Pro: $5/mo (ROI threshold: $10/mo attribution)
  - Paid Canva: $13/mo (ROI threshold: $26/mo new revenue)
  - Medium Partner ads: $50–200 (ROI threshold: $400/mo new revenue)

### Tier 3: Growth ($500–2,000/mo revenue) — UNLOCKS AT $800+/mo sustained 4 weeks
- **Approved:**
  - Pinterest/Reddit paid ads: $200–500/mo (ROI: 1.5x cost = $300/mo minimum)
  - Premium email tools (Convertkit/ActiveCampaign): $29–229/mo
  - SEO tools (Ahrefs/SEMrush): $99–119/mo (if affiliate revenue > $300/mo)

### Tier 4: Scale ($2,000+/mo revenue) — UNLOCKS AT $2,500+/mo sustained
- **Approved:**
  - Freelancers: $500–2,000/mo
  - Premium SaaS infrastructure
  - High-tier analytics and automation tools

**Decision Logic:**
1. Is revenue in my current tier? Check threshold
2. Does tool meet 2–3x ROI minimum? If yes, approve
3. Is this an emergency (platform broken, sales blocked)? Request exception
4. Is this speculative? Wait for next tier unlock

**Emergency Budget Release:** $50–500 for critical fixes or one-time investments (only if blocked revenue growth)
