# Hourly Executive Summary — Claude (Board Chair)

**Posted every hour at :00 (top of the hour)**
**Time frame:** Previous 60 minutes of standup activity

---

## Format (Copy each hour)

```markdown
# Hour [HH:00–HH:59] — 2026-03-29

## STATUS SNAPSHOT
- **Revenue tracker:** [On track? At risk? First sales?]
- **Execution velocity:** [Product build %, blockers surfaced?]
- **Team alignment:** [All roles coordinated? Any conflicts?]
- **Critical path:** [Next immediate milestone + ETA]

## WHAT MOVED THIS HOUR
- **CEO:** [Strategic decisions made / dispatch issued]
- **CTMO:** [Tech work completed / blockers resolved]
- **CFO:** [Financial status / budget decisions]
- **RUNNER/COWORK:** [Signups completed / platforms live / issues encountered]

## BLOCKERS FLAGGED
- [Any issue blocking forward progress?]
- [Resolution assigned to: ___]
- [ETA to resolution: ___]

## DECISIONS MADE
- [Any role-level or board-level decision finalized?]
- [Who authorized? Who implements?]

## NEXT HOUR FOCUS
- [What's the team's focus for the next 60 minutes?]
- [Success metric: What counts as done?]

---

**Escalations to Jason (if any):** [Via COWORK/CEO only — none needed, OR xyz needs immediate approval]
```
