# CFO Value-Add Responsibilities (When Awaiting Input)

While waiting for COO on Gumroad vs. Payhip, CFO has significant value-add work.

## Tier 1 — Complete Pricing Analysis

### Scenario Modeling Without COO Input
- Build 3+ complete scenarios: Gumroad only, Payhip only, both, direct+Gumroad
- Unit economics at each price point, margin projections, break-even
- Effort: 6-8 hours. Value: Present 4 scenarios instantly when COO decides

### Customer Acquisition Cost Analysis
- By channel: organic, Reddit, content, affiliate, paid ads
- Calculate profitability per channel
- Effort: 4-6 hours. Value: Know which marketing channels work

### Retention Economics
- Customer lifetime value (initial + repeat purchases)
- How repeat sales change pricing strategy
- Effort: 2-3 hours. Value: Long-term revenue model

## Tier 2 — Phase 2 Financial Strategy

### SaaS Unit Economics Framework
- Subscription pricing options, CAC, LTV, payback period
- Year 1/2/3 projections
- Effort: 6-8 hours. Value: Phase 2 revenue strategy locked

### Expansion Revenue Modeling
- If Phase 1 hits $887/month, what's Phase 2 impact?
- Growth curve over 12 months
- Effort: 3-4 hours. Value: Revenue trajectory for planning

### Tax & Compliance Cost Model
- Sales tax, income tax, business structure, accounting costs
- True margin after all obligations
- Effort: 2-3 hours. Value: Realistic profit expectations

## Tier 3 — Strategic Planning

### Funding Scenario Analysis
- Bootstrap vs SBA loan vs Angel investment
- Revenue trajectory and founder equity at exit
- Effort: 4-6 hours. Value: Growth options clear

### Competitive Margin Analysis
- Analyze 20 competitors (price, margin, positioning)
- Where do your products sit?
- Effort: 3-4 hours. Value: Pricing power confidence

### Burn Rate & Runway Planning
- Monthly costs, runway months, break-even target
- Effort: 1-2 hours. Value: Risk management clarity
