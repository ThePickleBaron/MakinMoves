# Hiring Investigation Summary: Key Findings & Recommendations

**Investigation Date:** 2026-03-29
**Investigator:** CEO (Independent research + analysis)
**Status:** COMPLETE — Ready for board briefing
**Time to read:** 5 minutes

---

## THE QUESTION
Should CEO hire MORE support to accelerate recovery? Where else is CEO the bottleneck?

## THE ANSWER

**YES.** Approve a 3-tier hiring plan (writer + social manager + email designer).

**Why:** Founder capacity is the critical bottleneck, not platform capacity or market demand. Without hiring by Day 25, burnout will collapse content quality and revenue by Week 5-6.

---

## CRITICAL FINDINGS

### Finding 1: Founder Capacity is at the Edge (27-34 hrs/week)

**Current workload breakdown:**
- P1 (Digital products): 7-9 hrs/week
- P2 (Affiliate blog): 10-13 hrs/week
- P3 (Print-on-demand): 7-9 hrs/week
- Admin/standup: 2-3 hrs/week
- **TOTAL: 27-34 hrs/week**

**Sustainability threshold:** <25 hrs/week for 90-day marathon
**Status:** ON THE EDGE (sustainable for 3 weeks, risky beyond Week 4)

**Burnout timeline without hiring:**
- Week 1-2: Fresh, high quality ✓
- Week 3-4: Fatigue signals 🟡
- Week 5-6: Quality collapse (typos, generic copy, missed deadlines) 🔴

---

### Finding 2: Writer Hire is Necessary But Not Sufficient

**Day 25 Writer Hire ($1.6K/mo):**
- Frees 8-10 hrs/week (blog writing + email copy)
- Enables P2 customer scaling from 3 to 5-6
- Adds $1.5K-2K/month revenue
- **ROI: 1.3x**
- **Result:** Founder capacity reduced to 18-24 hrs/week (sustainable)

**BUT:** Remaining 18-24 hrs/week still includes bottleneck:
- P1 (4-5 hrs): Research + product design
- P1 (3-4 hrs): Email list building
- **P3 (3-4 hrs): Daily Twitter/Substack posting** ← New bottleneck
- P3 (4-5 hrs): PoD design + QA
- Admin (2-3 hrs): Invoicing, standups, writer management

**Verdict:** Writer alone reduces burnout but doesn't solve capacity problem.

---

### Finding 3: Optimal Hiring Sequence (By ROI)

| Tier | Role | When | Cost | Capacity Freed | Revenue Upside | ROI | Priority |
|------|------|------|------|-----------------|-----------------|-----|----------|
| 1 | Writer | Day 25 | $1.6K/mo | 8-10 hrs | +$1.5K-2K | 1.3x | 🔴 CRITICAL |
| 2 | Social Manager | Day 50 | $1.2K/mo | 4-5 hrs | +$800-1.5K | 1.0-1.5x | 🟡 HIGH |
| 3 | Email Designer | Day 75+ | $1.0K/mo | 2-3 hrs | +$1.5K-3K | 2.0-3.0x | 🟡 MEDIUM |

**Why this order:**
1. **Writer first** = prevents system failure (P2 burnout)
2. **Social manager second** = unlocks founder visibility upside (which multiplies all streams)
3. **Email designer third** = maximizes email synergy (if budget available)

---

### Finding 4: Hiring vs. Automation Trade-offs

**Social Media (Hire vs. Automate):**
- Automation (Buffer): $150/mo, saves 60% time, loses engagement + viral upside
- **Verdict:** Hire, not automate (engagement is critical to founder moat)

**Email Sequences (Hire vs. Automate):**
- Automation (Zapier free tier + ConvertKit): $0-100/mo, saves 70% time, lower conversion
- **Verdict:** Partially automate, hire by Month 4 if needed

**Blog Writing (Hire vs. Automate):**
- AI draft + editing: $50/mo + 5-6 hrs/week, lower SEO quality
- **Verdict:** Hire, not automate (SEO ranking suffers with thin content)

---

### Finding 5: Financial Viability (Hiring ROI)

**6-Month Revenue Impact (May-Oct):**

| Scenario | Hiring Cost | P1+P2+P3 Revenue | Net Profit | ROI |
|----------|-------------|------------------|-----------|-----|
| **No hiring (writer only)** | $8K | $17K | +$9K | 1.1x |
| **All three hires** | $16.4K | $24.8K | +$8.4K | 1.5x |
| **Writer + Social Manager** | $12K | $23K | +$11K | 1.9x |

**Key insight:** Hiring actually accelerates revenue MORE than saves time. Each hire compounds the whole system.
