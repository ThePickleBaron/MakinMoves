## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The CEO's revenue projections ($1,100–2,000 by Day 90, $10K by early August) are **built on realistic baseline assumptions BUT critically underestimate expansion revenue potential** and **overestimate cross-sell payoff timing**.

**Key Findings:**
- ✅ **CAC assumptions are conservative** (organic = $0, paid ads = reasonable)
- ⚠️ **LTV expansion claims lack unit economics proof** (upsells assumed, not validated)
- ❌ **Cohort revenue analysis is shallow** (no retention/churn curves built in)
- ❌ **Expansion revenue projections are optimistic** (cross-sells assumed 1–5% but untested)
- ⚠️ **Payback periods realistic BUT expansion timeline compressed** (expecting Month 3 expansion, reality is Month 5–6)

**Bottom Line:** Baseline revenue targets ($1,100–2,000 by Day 90) are achievable. **But "$9K–14K cross-sell revenue by Month 3" is fiction.** Realistic expansion revenue = $2K–4K by Month 3 (mostly repeat purchases, not cross-sells).

## 1. CUSTOMER ACQUISITION COST (CAC) BY STREAM

### P1: Digital Products (Gumroad, $24.99 Template)

**Assumption 1: Organic (Email/Twitter Launch)**
- **Baseline:** 200–500 email list OR 500+ Twitter followers
- **Reality check:** CEO planning to build list in parallel with launch
- **Organic CAC: $0** ✅
- **But:** What's the implicit cost of email/Twitter cultivation?
  - Email building: 2–3 weeks (10–20 hours of content/engagement)
  - Twitter: Same
  - **Amortized cost over 100 customers:** ~$10–20 per customer (if you value time at $50/hour)

**Finding 1:** Organic CAC is "free platform" but not "zero labor cost."

---

**Assumption 2: Paid Ads ($3–10 CAC)**
- **Reality test:** What platforms? Facebook/Google ads to $24.99 template buyers?
- **Facebook cost-per-click:** $0.50–2.00 (freelancer niche)
- **Estimated conversion rate:** 1–3% (templates are low-intent purchase)
- **Implied CAC:** $50–200 (inverse of 1–3% conversion)
- **CEO assumption CAC $3–10:** Only achievable at **5–8% conversion rate**

**Finding 2:** Paid ads CAC is **2–5x higher** than CEO assumes. Realistic paid CAC = $20–50 per customer if using cold traffic (Facebook/Google).

**Revised P1 CAC:**
- Organic: $0 (platform) + ~$10–20 (amortized time)
- Warm list (email 200+): $0–5 (platform) per sale
- Cold traffic (paid ads): $20–50 per sale

**Current CEO plan is organic-only, so $0 CAC assumption is reasonable for baseline.**
