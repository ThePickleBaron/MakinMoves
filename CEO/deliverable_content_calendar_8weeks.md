## Content Execution Formula

### P2 Substack Articles (2/week)
- **Format:** Personal narrative or case study, 1,500–2,000 words
- **CTA:** "If you need professional content written at scale, let's talk" (link to P2 packages)
- **Publish:** Monday 9 AM (subscriber engagement peak)
- **Promotion:** Twitter, LinkedIn, cross-post to Medium

### P2 Medium Articles (2/week)
- **Format:** Thought leadership / how-to, 1,800–2,500 words
- **CTA:** Link back to Substack for full service details
- **Publish:** Wednesday 9 AM
- **SEO:** Optimized for "freelance writing" + "content marketing" keywords

### P3 Affiliate Articles (2-3/week per site)
- **Format:** Comparison, pillar, or tutorial, 2,000–3,000 words
- **CTA:** Affiliate links embedded naturally in "Best tools" / "How to set up" sections
- **Publish:** Staggered (Tue, Thu, Fri) to keep sites active
- **SEO:** Optimized for "[Tool name] review," "[Tool A] vs [Tool B]," "[Tool name] tutorial"
