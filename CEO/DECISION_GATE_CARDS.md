**Question: Launch Sprint Validation — Did products launch? Any early revenue?**

```

  - Cumulative revenue: $______

  - Customer feedback: [list themes]

  - GSC impressions: _____
  - First clicks? Yes / No
  - Affiliate apps approved? Yes / No / Pending

  - Designs live: _____
  - Clicks: _____
  - Sales: _____
  - Revenue: $______

COMBINED REVENUE: $______
```

### Decision Outcomes

**IF Combined >$200:**
```
✅ GO: Continue Phase 1B (Weeks 5–8)

ACTIONS:
☐ Proceed with full scaling plan
☐ Schedule Week 5 kickoff meeting
☐ Prepare for Day 45 staffing decision
☐ Document decision to board/decisions/decision_2026-04-26_DAY30.md
```

**IF Combined $100–200:**
```
⚠️ CONDITIONAL: Rebalance (Scenario 2)

ACTIONS:
☐ Identify strongest stream (P1? P2? P3?)
☐ Accelerate strongest, de-prioritize weakest
☐ If P1 <$40: Pivot niche (Week 5 restart with new product)
☐ If P3 <$10: Shift to P1+P2 focus
☐ Document rebalance plan to board/decisions/
```

**IF Combined <$100:**
```
🛑 NO-GO: Pause + Pivot (Scenario 3)

ACTIONS:
☐ Pause Week 5 execution
☐ 2-week diagnostic (week of Apr 29):
   - P1: Was niche wrong? Positioning? Traffic?
   - P2: (Normal for affiliate, continue)
   - P3: Design quality? Niche fit?
☐ Plan revised approach (new niche/product)
☐ Week 5 restart with corrected model
☐ Document pivot plan to board/decisions/
```

### Risk Mitigation (If Close to Gate)
- **Revenue at $180?** → Can you push $20–30 more this week?
  - Extra Twitter posts, Reddit seeding, email list push?
  - Reframe as "final push before decision day"
- **P1 at $35, P3 at $10, P2 normal?** → CONDITIONAL scenario
  - Accelerate P2 (affiliate is longer game)
  - Prepare P1 niche pivot for Week 5
  - Decide: pivot P1 or shift all to P2 focus?

## Emergency Decision Triggers (Before Scheduled Gates)

### Trigger 1: RED FLAG — Any stream <10% of forecast by Week 2
**When:** Before Apr 12 standup
**Action:** Escalate immediately to CEO
**Timeline:** 48 hours to decide (continue or pivot)

### Trigger 2: RED FLAG — Platform rejection (Stripe, Merch, etc.) by Day 7
**When:** By Apr 6
**Action:** Activate fallback immediately
**Timeline:** 24 hours to live (PayPal, Redbubble, CJ Affiliate)

### Trigger 3: YELLOW FLAG — Email list <1% signup rate by Week 3
**When:** By Apr 19
**Action:** Improve CTA, test lead magnet, add popup
**Timeline:** Implement by Week 4

### Trigger 4: RED FLAG — Combined revenue <$100 by Week 3 (Apr 19)
**When:** Early warning for Day 30 gate
**Action:** Prepare for Scenario 3 (Pivot) in standup
**Timeline:** Confirm decision by Apr 26
