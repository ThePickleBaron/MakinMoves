## Questions & Answers

**Q: Should we delay P3 until Month 6 if P1+P2 are succeeding?**
A: No. Start P3 immediately (Week 1-2) to let SEO mature over 6-8 months. If you wait until Month 6 to start, you won't have meaningful revenue until Month 12-14. The goal is patient, parallel execution.

**Q: What if Month 4 revenue is exactly $100? Escalate or continue?**
A: If trending upward ($25 → $50 → $100), continue. If flat or declining, escalate. The trigger is momentum, not the exact number.

**Q: Can we hire a freelancer to speed up content creation?**
A: Only after Month 4 IF revenue > $500/month. Before that, in-house effort is better (lower cost, ensure quality). After Month 4, you can afford $300-400/month freelancer if needed.

**Q: What's the biggest risk factor?**
A: SEO timeline. If we target the wrong keywords or the niches are more competitive than expected, we could take 10-12 months instead of 8 months. This is manageable but requires patient execution.

**Q: Do we need to update the CEO roadmap?**
A: Yes. Change P3 from "6-month ramp, Month 6 $27,960" to "8-10 month ramp, Month 8 $20,000 run rate." Reframe it as sustainability stream, not quick win.
