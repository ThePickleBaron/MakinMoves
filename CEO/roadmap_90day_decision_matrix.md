## Rebalance Rules (If Scenario 2 Activated)

**When to rebalance at Day 30 or Day 60:**

### P1 (Digital Products) <$40 by Day 30
**Rebalance Actions:**
1. Analyze customer feedback — is niche wrong? positioning? price too high?
2. Test NEW niche (completely different product category) for Week 5 launch
3. OR test new positioning of same niche (e.g., "for freelancers" → "for agencies")
4. Option C: Pivot to course/template format if bundle not working
5. Decision deadline: May 1 (start new product by May 5)

### P2 (Affiliate) Slow Ranking by Day 60
**Rebalance Actions:**
1. This is NORMAL (SEO takes 4–8 weeks) — do NOT panic
2. Continue 2 articles/week through Day 90
3. Focus on email list growth (not affiliate sales yet)
4. By Week 11–12, should see first meaningful affiliate clicks
5. If still 0 clicks by Week 12, check: keywords? GSC setup? traffic sources?

### P3 (Print-on-Demand) <$10 revenue by Day 30
**Rebalance Actions:**
1. Analyze design performance — are any designs getting clicks?
2. If ANY design >2 clicks: create 10 variations on that theme (same niche, different angles)
3. If ALL designs <1 click: new niche (pivot completely by Week 5)
4. Test new niche with 5 designs (Week 5 launch)
5. Keep Redbubble + MBA active, shift focus if MBA still pending
