```markdown

**Between:** ExsituInnovations ("Company")
**And:** [Partner Name] ("Affiliate")

**Effective Date:** [DATE]

---

## 1. PARTNERSHIP OVERVIEW

The Company grants Affiliate the right to promote Company products (digital templates, courses, services) and earn commission on referred sales.

## 2. AFFILIATE RESPONSIBILITIES

- Promote Company products to Affiliate's audience via [email / social / blog / etc.]
- Use Company-provided materials (images, descriptions, links)
- Disclose affiliate relationship to audience (transparency)
- Not make false or misleading claims about products
- Not engage in deceptive marketing practices

## 3. COMPANY RESPONSIBILITIES

- Provide Affiliate with unique tracking link and promo materials
- Pay commissions within 30 days of month-end
- Track and report clicks, conversions, revenue monthly
- Support Affiliate with product updates and new offers

## 4. COMMISSION STRUCTURE

**Commission Rate:** [15-30%] per sale

**Payment Method:** [Stripe, PayPal, Direct Deposit]

**Payment Schedule:** Monthly, net-30 (paid by [Date] of following month)

**Minimum Payout:** $50 (payments below $50 carried forward to next month)

**Cookie Window:** 30 days (customer has 30 days to purchase after clicking Affiliate link)

## 5. TRACKING & REPORTING

- Company tracks all clicks, conversions, and revenue via [Tracking Platform]
- Affiliate receives monthly dashboard access to view performance
- Discrepancies reported within 30 days of invoice date
- No adjustments accepted after 30 days

## 6. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.

Upon termination:
- All promotions must stop immediately
- Affiliate links become inactive
- Final payment issued for earned commissions (net-30)

## 7. AFFILIATE CODE OF CONDUCT

Affiliate agrees to:
- [ ] Not spam or send unsolicited emails
- [ ] Not use paid advertising without Company approval
- [ ] Not create misleading landing pages
- [ ] Not bid on Company branded keywords (if applicable)
- [ ] Comply with FTC disclosure requirements (disclose affiliate relationship)

## 8. LIABILITY & INDEMNIFICATION

- Affiliate responsible for own marketing and content
- Company not liable for Affiliate's actions or claims
- Affiliate indemnifies Company against third-party claims arising from Affiliate's promotions

## 9. CONFIDENTIALITY

- Affiliate must keep Commission Rate and Partner terms confidential
- Affiliate may not share detailed performance data publicly

## 10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This agreement constitutes the entire agreement between parties. Any changes require written amendment signed by both parties.

## Commission & Payout Best Practices

**Commission Rate Strategy:**
- Digital Templates (P1): 25% commission (high margin product, can afford higher payout)
- Freelance Writing (P2): 15% commission (service/hourly product, lower margins)
- Affiliate Programs (P3): Pass-through commission (whatever the original program offers)

**Payout Minimums:**
- Set minimum payout at: $50 (too many small affiliates below this)
- Payment frequency: Monthly (standard)
- Payment method: Direct deposit, PayPal (most affiliates prefer)

**Expected Affiliate Economics:**
- 100 active affiliates → 50–100 clicks/day → 1–3 conversions/day → $25–75/day revenue attributed
- At 25% commission: $6–19/day to affiliates
- Break-even/profitable at Day 15+ (initial setup cost absorbed)

## 4. TRACKING & ATTRIBUTION SETUP (CTMO Checklist)

**For:** CTMO to implement tracking infrastructure

```markdown
# Affiliate Tracking & Attribution Setup

## Overview

Track all partner-referred traffic and conversions back to individual affiliate source.

## Technical Setup

### 1. Unique Affiliate Codes (Per Partner)

**Format:** `[partner_source]_[unique_id]`

**Examples:**
- `substack_writer_001`
- `twitter_creator_jsmith`
- `linkedin_exec_acme`
- `medium_publication_startup`

**Implementation:**
- Store in: Database table `affiliate_partners`
- Columns: partner_id, partner_name, product, affiliate_link, commission_pct, date_created
- Generate unique code: UUID or simple sequential code
- Track: Every unique code gets unique tracking link

### 2. UTM Parameter Strategy

**Structure:** `https://yoursite.com/product?utm_source=[partner]&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=[product]&utm_content=[link_id]`

**Examples:**
```
https://gumroad.com/exsituinnovations?utm_source=substack_writer_001&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=p1_digital_products&utm_content=notion_template_bundle

https://gumroad.com/exsituinnovations?utm_source=twitter_creator_jsmith&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=p1_digital_products
```

**Where to add UTM:**
- Gumroad: Add to product link (modify link before sharing with partner)
- Upwork/Bonsai: Use platform's native affiliate link (they handle UTM)
- Email: Add UTM to all links in partner content

### 3. Google Analytics Tracking

**Setup:**
- [ ] Enable UTM tracking in GA4
- [ ] Create: Custom dimension for "Affiliate Partner"
- [ ] Create: Custom metric for "Affiliate Revenue"
- [ ] Dashboard: Real-time affiliate performance view
- [ ] Reports: Monthly attribution by partner

**Queries:**
```sql
-- Top partners by clicks/conversions
SELECT
  utm_source,
  COUNT(*) as clicks,
  COUNT(DISTINCT session_id) as sessions,
  SUM(transaction_value) as revenue
FROM web_analytics
WHERE utm_medium = 'affiliate'
GROUP BY utm_source
ORDER BY revenue DESC;

-- Conversion rate by partner
SELECT
  utm_source,
  COUNT(DISTINCT user_id) as users,
  COUNT(DISTINCT transaction_id) as conversions,
  ROUND(COUNT(DISTINCT transaction_id) / COUNT(DISTINCT user_id), 4) as conversion_rate
FROM web_analytics
WHERE utm_medium = 'affiliate'
GROUP BY utm_source;
```

### 4. Database Schema (PostgreSQL)

```sql
-- Track affiliate partners
CREATE TABLE affiliate_partners (
  id SERIAL PRIMARY KEY,
  partner_name VARCHAR(255) NOT NULL,
  email VARCHAR(255),
  product VARCHAR(50), -- P1, P2, P3
  unique_code VARCHAR(50) UNIQUE,
  affiliate_link TEXT,
  commission_pct DECIMAL(5,2),
  status VARCHAR(20), -- active, inactive, paused
  date_created TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW(),
  date_activated TIMESTAMP,
  date_paused TIMESTAMP
);

-- Track affiliate clicks and conversions
CREATE TABLE affiliate_clicks (
  id SERIAL PRIMARY KEY,
  partner_id INT REFERENCES affiliate_partners(id),
  session_id VARCHAR(100),
  utm_source VARCHAR(100),
  click_date TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW(),
  referrer_url TEXT,
  landing_page VARCHAR(255)
);

CREATE TABLE affiliate_conversions (
  id SERIAL PRIMARY KEY,
  partner_id INT REFERENCES affiliate_partners(id),
  affiliate_click_id INT REFERENCES affiliate_clicks(id),
  session_id VARCHAR(100),
  product_id VARCHAR(50),
  amount DECIMAL(10,2),
  commission_amount DECIMAL(10,2),
  conversion_date TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW(),
  order_id VARCHAR(100) UNIQUE
);

-- Monthly reconciliation
CREATE TABLE affiliate_monthly_report (
  id SERIAL PRIMARY KEY,
  partner_id INT REFERENCES affiliate_partners(id),
  report_month DATE, -- 2026-03-01, 2026-04-01, etc.
  clicks INT,
  conversions INT,
  conversion_rate DECIMAL(6,4),
  total_revenue DECIMAL(12,2),
  commission_earned DECIMAL(12,2),
  status VARCHAR(20), -- pending, approved, paid
  date_generated TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW(),
  date_paid TIMESTAMP
);
```

### 5. Integration Points

**Where revenue enters the system:**

1. **Gumroad Sales**
   - Gumroad webhook → Capture sale event
   - Extract: utm_source → Match to affiliate_partners
   - Log to: affiliate_conversions table
   - Attribution: Automatic (via UTM in original link)

2. **Upwork/Bonsai Referrals**
   - Platform sends: Referral notification → Your email
   - Manual entry: affiliate_conversions (or auto-sync via API if available)
   - Track: Commission payout separately (platform pays you, you pay affiliate)

3. **Email Promotions**
   - Partner sends email with tracking link
   - Customer clicks → Lands on site with UTM
   - GA captures UTM → Database logs click
   - If purchase: Database logs conversion with partner_id

4. **Social Promotions**
   - Partner tweets/posts with tracking link
   - Same flow as email (UTM → GA → database)

### 6. n8n Automation (Daily Syncs)

**Workflow: Sync Gumroad sales to affiliate_conversions**

```
Trigger: Daily (9am)
- Fetch Gumroad sales from past 24 hrs (via API)
- For each sale:
  - Extract UTM source from original Gumroad link
  - Match utm_source to affiliate_partners.unique_code
  - If match found:
    - Log to affiliate_conversions table
    - Update affiliate monthly stats
  - If no match:
    - Log as "organic" (non-partner traffic)
- Send summary email: [Partner, Revenue, Commission]
```

**Workflow: Generate monthly affiliate invoices**

```
Trigger: Monthly (last day of month)
- Query: All conversions for past month, grouped by partner_id
- Calculate: total_revenue, commission_earned (based on commission_pct)
- Create: affiliate_monthly_report record
- Send email to partner:
  - Conversions: X
  - Revenue: $X
  - Commission earned: $X
  - Payment date: [Net-30]
```

### 8. Payment Processing (Manual Process - Week 2+)

**Monthly (by 5th of following month):**

1. Query: All approved monthly reports where status = "pending" and date_generated < last month
2. Calculate: Total commission owed to each partner
3. Initiate payment: Stripe/PayPal/Direct Deposit per partner preference
4. Update: affiliate_monthly_report.status = "paid"
5. Send: Payment confirmation email to partner (amount, date, receipt)

**Minimum payout:** $50 (if below, carry forward to next month)

**Payment method:** Partner's preference (Stripe, PayPal, Direct Deposit)

## Critical Implementation Notes

**For CTMO:**

1. **Gumroad Limitations:**
   - Gumroad doesn't let you customize the referral link with UTM params
   - Workaround: Create bitly/short link that redirects to Gumroad with UTM appended
   - Example: `bit.ly/exsitu-p1-substack-001` → redirects to `gumroad.com/...?utm_source=substack_writer_001`

2. **Tracking Delays:**
   - GA may take 24–48 hrs to populate fully
   - Gumroad API may lag 2–4 hrs
   - Use n8n for near-real-time sync (but expect minor delays)

3. **Attribution Windows:**
   - Set cookie window to 30 days (standard)
   - If customer clicks link on Day 1, purchases on Day 30, still attribute to partner
   - After 30 days, new click resets the window

4. **Multi-touch Attribution (Future):**
   - Current setup: Last-click attribution (whoever's link they clicked last gets credit)
   - Better approach (later): Share credit if multiple partners influenced the sale
   - For now: Keep simple (last-click only)
