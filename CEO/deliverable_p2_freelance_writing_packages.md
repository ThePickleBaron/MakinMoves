# P2: Freelance Writing Service Packages

## Service Offerings

- **Scope:** 2 articles/month, 2,000 words each, client-provided topics, SEO-optimized
- **Price:** $600/month
- **Turnaround:** 7 business days per article
- **Deliverables:** Draft + 1 revision round + internal links + meta descriptions

### Package 2: "Blog Stack" (SaaS Edition)
- **Scope:** 4 articles/month, 1,500 words each, topics from content calendar, publication-ready
- **Price:** $1,200/month
- **Turnaround:** 5 business days per article
- **Deliverables:** Final-draft quality + publication + promotion on 2 channels
- **Target:** B2B SaaS companies, product newsletters, developer platforms

### Package 3: "Content Director" (Agency/Publisher Edition)
- **Scope:** 6 articles/month, mixed 1,500–2,500 words, strategy consultation included
- **Price:** $2,000/month
