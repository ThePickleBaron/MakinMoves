## What Was Tested

CEO's P3 affiliate niche site model projects **$59,988 over 6 months** across 3 sites (email marketing, freelance tools, AI writing). This stress test evaluated:

1. **SEO ranking timeline** — Does 3-4 months match industry reality?
2. **Affiliate conversion rates** — Is 2-3% realistic for cold organic traffic?
3. **Commission sustainability** — Will platform rates stay stable?
4. **Content quality + volume** — Can we execute 6 articles/week at SEO quality?
5. **Downside scenarios** — What if all risks compound?

---

## Key Findings

### Finding 1: SEO Timeline is Optimistic
**Industry data 2025-2026:**
- New domains typically rank for competitive keywords in 6-12 months
- "Best email marketing platform" is high-competition (ConvertKit, HubSpot, Beehiiv already rank)
- Long-tail keywords can rank in 3-6 months
- CEO's Month 3-4 Page 1 ranking is 50-75% too aggressive

**Verdict:** SEO timeline should be 8-10 months for main keywords, not 3-4 months.

### Finding 2: Conversion Rate is Optimistic
**Industry benchmarks:**
- Email affiliate (warm traffic): 5-8% conversion
- Organic SEO (cold traffic): 1-2% conversion
- Our traffic: Mixed (40% informational, 40% high-intent, 20% tutorial) = 1-1.5% realistic

**Verdict:** Target 1-1.5% baseline, not 2-3%. Upside if we exceed.

### Finding 3: Commission Risk is Low
**Status check:**
- ConvertKit: Stable at 30% (no announced cuts)
- Beehiiv, Jasper, Monday.com: All stable
- Diversified across 4-5 programs per niche
- Historical concern: Substack cut program, Amazon/Udemy reduced rates

**Verdict:** Commission cut risk is real but manageable. Platform cuts would impact 10-20% of revenue, not 100%.

### Finding 4: Content Volume is Achievable But Requires Commitment
**CEO assumption:** 6 articles/week at SEO quality
**Reality:**
- P2 freelancers can do 3-4 articles/week at publishable quality
- Founder/COO can do 2-3 articles/week at high SEO quality
- 6 articles/week requires either $3,600-4,300/month freelance budget OR 10-15 hours/week founder time

**Verdict:** Achievable if founder/COO commits 6-10 hours/week for first 6 months. Otherwise, slow to 3-4 articles/week.

**CEO stance:** Allows course correction without sunk loss.
