## ONE-PAGE SUMMARY

**Gumroad is a $200M annual marketplace where top creators earn $50K-$500K+ per product.** The platform is NOT oversaturated—it's undersegmented. Broad categories (Photography: 4,200 products, $2.1K avg) are flooded. Niche categories (Writing: 226 products, $15.75K avg) are wide open.

**Fastest path to $1K revenue:**
- Pick underserved niche (Writing, Fitness niche, Affinity Software, Business Automation)
- Build audience via Twitter (64% of successful creators use this channel)
- Launch 1 product (AI prompt library, Notion template, eBook, or mini-course)
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks (if audience pre-built) or 8-12 weeks (cold start)

**Recommended first move:** Launch niche Notion template at $29 in Writing & Publishing category (lowest competition, highest revenue per product).

## AUDIENCE REALITY: YOU NEED THIS FIRST

**Finding:** 64% of successful Gumroad creators grew via Twitter, NOT Gumroad search.

### No Audience = Slow Start
- Timeline to first $1K: 8-12 weeks
- Method: Build 2K-5K Twitter followers + 500-1K email list, THEN launch

### With Audience (1K+ Twitter, 500+ email) = Fast Start
- Timeline to first $1K: 2-4 weeks
- Method: Announce to existing followers + email list
- Conversion: 30-50 sales @ $30-40 = $1K

**CEO Decision Point:**
- Do we have audience? → Launch product in 2-4 weeks
- No audience? → Build Twitter (3 months) + product (parallel) → Launch month 4
