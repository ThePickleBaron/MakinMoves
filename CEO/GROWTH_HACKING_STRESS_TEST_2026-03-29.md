# Growth Hacking Strategy Stress Test
**Date:** 2026-03-29
**Purpose:** Validate CEO's P1/P2/P3 growth tactics against market reality
**Status:** CRITICAL EXECUTION DECISIONS REQUIRED

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**The Plan:** 14 ambitious growth hacks across P1 (Gumroad), P2 (Freelance), and P3 (Affiliate)

**The Reality:** 5-6 of these are high-ROI quick wins. The other 8-9 are either unrealistic, low-priority, or resource-intensive traps.

**The Verdict:**
- Week 1 Focus: 3 tactics (P1 email launch, P2 niche targeting, P3 long-tail SEO)
- Month 2+: 5 additional tactics (affiliate partnerships, testimonials, bundling)
- Deprioritize: 6 tactics (20 affiliates, micro-influencer, giveaway campaigns, community building, 50 keywords Month 1, referral program)
- Conditional: 2 tactics (customer testimonials, guest posts) - only if first revenue validates

**Bottom Line:** Jason can realistically execute 3-4 tactics simultaneously. Adding more than 5 tactics in Month 1 = burnout risk.
