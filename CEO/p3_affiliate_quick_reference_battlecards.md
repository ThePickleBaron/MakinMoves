## CARD 1: EMAIL MARKETING STACK FOR CREATORS

### One-Liner Differentiator
**"Email tools for creators who are ready to build an audience, not just send emails."**

### Top 3 Competitors
1. **EmailToolTester.com** — 15K-25K traffic/mo; feature-focused; high DA (55+)
2. **WPBeginner.com** — 200K+ traffic/mo; blogger-focused; massive DA (70+)
3. **Zapier.com/blog** — 500K+ traffic/mo; workflow-focused; highest DA (80+)

### What We Own (vs. Them)
- **Creator Journey** vs. Feature Lists
- **Monetization Clarity** vs. Product Specs
- **Community Stories** vs. Impersonal Comparisons
- **Speed/Setup** vs. Comprehensive Coverage

### Top 3 Positioning Gaps
1. **Audience-First Angle** — Nobody segments platforms by creator type (newsletter writers vs. course creators)
2. **Monetization Stories** — Missing "how much money can you make?" angle
3. **Community Culture** — Platforms reviewed as products; missing culture/community factor

1. "Substack alternatives for paid newsletters 2026" (2-3K/mo search, low competition)
2. "Beehiiv affiliate income: how much can you make?" (1-1.5K/mo, very low comp.)
3. "Email sequences that drive paid newsletter conversions" (1.2-1.8K/mo, medium comp.)
4. "How to transition from Mailchimp to ConvertKit" (500-800/mo, low comp.)
5. "Email marketing for cohort-based courses" (400-600/mo, very low comp.)

### Revenue Projection (6 Months)
- Month 1-2: $50-150/mo (early indexing)
- Month 3-4: $800-2.5K/mo (ranking, affiliate clicks)
- Month 5-6: $5K-7.5K/mo (authority building)
- **6-Month Total:** $16K-18K
