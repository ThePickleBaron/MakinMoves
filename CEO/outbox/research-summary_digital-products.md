## Risk Analysis

### Low Risk Factors ✓
- Zero startup costs (nothing to lose)
- No inventory or fulfillment
- Reversible (can pivot without sunk costs)
- Organic marketing proven effective
- Multiple revenue paths reduce single-product risk
- Part-time compatible

### Potential Challenges ⚠
- Takes 3-6 months to reach $5K/month
- Requires consistent audience building (daily content)
- Cheap/generic products don't sell (need differentiation)
- Some niches saturated (need niche selection)
- Quality matters more than quantity

### Mitigation Strategies
- Start with underserved niche (not general fitness/productivity)
- Test pricing early via pre-sales
- Build personal brand + product simultaneously
- Choose category matching expertise/passion
