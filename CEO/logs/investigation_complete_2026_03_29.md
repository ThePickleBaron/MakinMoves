## KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY

### Email Synergy Claims vs. Reality

| Assumption | Claimed | Realistic | Variance |
|---|---|---|---|
| Email list by Month 6 | 500 | 250-300 | -40-50% |
| P2 email customers | 5-7 | 0.5-1 | -92% |
| P2 email revenue | $3K-4.2K | $600-1.2K | -73% |
| P3 affiliate email | $400-600 | $50-100 | -92% |
| **TOTAL EMAIL UPSIDE** | **$9K-14K** | **$1K-2K** | **-80%** |

### Why the Math Breaks

1. **Email list is 50% smaller** (250 vs 500) due to realistic blog opt-in rates
2. **P2 conversion is 3-5x lower** (0.5% vs 1-2% application; 35% vs 60% close)
3. **P3 affiliate is nearly zero** (1.5% vs 4-6%; low commission rates)
4. **Email fatigue kills upside** (15-20% unsubscribe reduces reach)
5. **Attribution missing** (CTMO work not allocated; CEO can't measure ROI)
6. **Execution deprioritized** (Email competes with P1/P2 core work; loses)

## CEO DECISION REQUIRED

### Three Paths

**Path A: Full Email Synergy**
- Commitment: 10+ hours setup + 6 hours/month
- Expected: +$1K-2K
- Risk: High
- Only if email list on track for 150+ by April 15

**Path B: Lightweight Email Synergy (RECOMMENDED)**
- Commitment: 2-3 hours setup + <1 hour/month
- Implementation: P1 welcome sequence only; skip P2/P3
- Expected: +$200-500
- Risk: Low
- Sweet spot for capacity-constrained CEO

**Path C: Defer Email Synergy**
- Commitment: Skip it; revisit Month 4-5
- Rationale: Core P1/P2/P3 is higher ROI; email is optional add-on
- Expected: No Month 1-3 upside; available as contingency
- Risk: Low
- Safest choice if email list <150 by April 15

## RECOMMENDATION

Board recommends **Path B (Lightweight Email Synergy)**.

- Realistic upside: +$300-500 (not +$9K-14K)
- Time investment: 2-3 hours (not 10+ hours)
- Risk: Low (easy to kill if not working)
- Leaves CEO capacity for P1/P2/P3 core work
