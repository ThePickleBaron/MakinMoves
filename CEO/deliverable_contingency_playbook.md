# Contingency Playbook: If Revenue Streams Underperform

---

## SCENARIO 1: P1 GUMROAD FAILS (0-100 revenue by May 15)

**Diagnosis:** External traffic strategy not working (low email list, low Twitter engagement, medium underperforming).

### Immediate Pivot Actions (May 15-31)
1. **Kill external traffic experiment, test alternatives:**
   - Shift budget: $500 budget for paid ads (Google Ads + Facebook Ads targeting indie hackers)
   - Timeline: 2-week test, measure CPC + conversion rate
   - Decision gate: If ROAS > 2x, scale to $1K/month. Else, abandon P1.

2. **Product repositioning:**
   - Segment 1: Sell to existing audience (Twitter/Substack followers) as "insider access"
   - Segment 2: Bundle with P2 service (offer bundle: "content + template" discount)
   - Test: 2-3 different messaging angles (problem-focused vs. outcome-focused vs. fear-of-missing-out)

3. **Alternative sales channels:**
   - ProductHunt launch (Week 2 of pivot): Use viral feedback to improve product
   - Indie Hackers community post: "I built X, here's how" + embedded Gumroad link
   - Affiliate bootstrap: Partner with 5 indie hackers to cross-promote (revenue share)

### Fallback Plan (If pivot fails by Jun 15)
- **Sunset P1:** Reallocate 20 hrs/week to P2 + P3 instead
- **Passive P1:** Keep product live but stop active promotion (maintenance mode, organic sales only)
- **Alternative:** Pivot product to "Freelancer Template Bundle" (tie to P2 service)

**Recovery path:** If P1 sunset, target $10K recovery from P2 ($5K) + P3 ($5K) instead, extend timeline to Month 4-5.

## SCENARIO 2: P2 FREELANCE WRITING FAILS (0 qualified leads by May 15)

**Diagnosis:** Sales outreach not resonating OR service offering not aligned to market demand.

### Immediate Pivot Actions (May 15-31)
1. **Validate market demand with surveys:**
   - Send 50 cold emails (different angles: 3-5 variations)
   - Add sales question: "If you don't need writing service, what DO you need?"
   - Analyze responses: What pain point resonates most?

2. **Reposition service:**
   - If response: "Too expensive" → Lower entry price ($300/mo "Starter Writing")
   - If response: "Not enough capacity" → Offer part-time (8 hrs/week retainer)
   - If response: "Need video content" → Expand to video scriptwriting (high-demand variation)
   - If response: "Need one-time project" → Offer $1,500 project-based "Campaign Writing" (not retainer)

3. **Alternative sales channels:**
   - Transition from direct outreach → Community authority (answer writing questions on Reddit, Indie Hackers for 2 weeks)
   - Offer free 30-min content audit (lead magnet) → Convert via follow-up
   - Partner with marketing agencies (white-label referral partner → they sell, you write)

4. **Reduce sales friction:**
   - Simplify from 3 packages → 1 simple package ($800/mo, 6 articles + 1 strategy call)
   - Add trial offer: "$200 test project" (2 articles, no commitment) → upgrade to retainer
   - Payment flexibility: Monthly vs. quarterly discounts

### Fallback Plan (If pivot fails by Jun 15)
- **Sunset P2:** Reallocate 20 hrs/week to P1 + P3 instead
- **Pivot product:** Offer "Done-for-you Substack setup" ($2K one-time) instead of monthly retainer
- **Alternative:** Shift to "Ghostwriting for SaaS founders' books" (higher price, lower volume)

**Recovery path:** If P2 sunset, target $10K from P1 ($5K) + P3 ($5K), expect extended timeline.

## SCENARIO 3: P3 AFFILIATE NICHE SITES FAIL (0 traffic by Jun 15)

**Diagnosis:** SEO strategy not working (content quality, keyword targeting, or backlink strategy failing).

### Immediate Pivot Actions (May 15-31)
1. **Audit SEO performance:**
   - Check: Are articles indexed in Google? (Site: search)
   - Check: Are any keywords ranking (even page 3-10)?
   - Diagnosis: If not indexed → technical SEO issue (robots.txt, sitemap). Fix immediately (1 day).
   - Diagnosis: If indexed but no rankings → content quality or keyword too competitive. Revise strategy.

2. **Traffic acceleration alternatives:**
   - Paid ads experiment: $100/week Google Ads targeting "email marketing tools" keywords
   - Backlink building: Reach out to 20 SaaS blogs for guest posts (external links = SEO boost)
   - Reddit seeding: Post honest recommendations in r/Freelance, r/Entrepreneur (ethical, passive traffic)
   - Founder visibility: Cross-post best articles to Substack, Twitter (referral traffic)

3. **Content quality reset:**
   - Audit top 5 competitors: Are they deeper, longer, more detailed?
   - Revise strategy: Increase article length (2K → 3K words), add more affiliate links, improve visual design
   - Competitor research: Find unranked keywords competitors miss (lower competition angle)

4. **Alternative monetization (if organic fails):**
   - Run ads network (Google AdSense): Even 500 visitors/month = $50-100 revenue
   - Affiliate email funnel: Build email list on each site (5K subscribers possible) → Email sequence → affiliate sales
   - Product bundling: Sell "Email Marketing Toolkit" PDF for $29 (bundles free tools you review)

### Fallback Plan (If pivot fails by Jun 15)
- **Scale down to 1 niche site:** Kill the 2 lowest-traffic sites, double down on best-performing one
- **Sunset P3:** Reallocate 20 hrs/week to P1 + P2 instead
- **Passive income: Keep 1 site running (maintenance mode), hope for long-tail organic growth (Month 6-12 timeline)

**Recovery path:** If P3 scaled down to 1 niche, target $10K from P1 ($5K) + P2 ($5K), accept P3 as "bonus" if it lands.

## SCENARIO 4: ALL THREE STREAMS FAIL (0-500 revenue by May 31)

**Diagnosis:** Fundamental product-market fit issue OR execution/capacity problem.

### Emergency Board Meeting (May 31)
**Decision points:**
1. **Is it an execution problem?** (resources, time allocation, implementation fidelity)
   - If yes: Reallocate founder time (40-50 hrs/week intensive push, hire 1 contractor to help COO)
   - Timeline: 30-day intensive sprint June 1-30 to salvage recovery

2. **Is it a market-fit problem?** (nobody wants these products)
   - If yes: Pivot business model entirely (consider: YouTube automation, e-commerce, print-on-demand, freelance agencies)
   - Timeline: 2-week discovery sprint → launch new stream by Jun 15

3. **Is it a capital/cost problem?** (need money to pay for traffic/ads/operations)
   - If yes: Activate contingency funding sources (personal network loans, Stripe capital, creator loans)
   - Or reduce burn: Cut all non-essential costs, founder "sweat equity" only

### Contingency Stream Options (If pivoting required)
- **YouTube Automation:** Repurpose existing affiliate research → 2-3 YouTube channels (rank for long-tail queries)
- **Content Licensing:** License P3 article content to content agencies (bulk sales at $50-100 per article)
- **Freelance Marketplace:** Offer freelance services on Upwork (rapid revenue, but not scalable)
- **Consulting Calls:** Offer 1-on-1 founder coaching ($200-500/call, 5-10 calls/month = $1-5K revenue)

**Recovery target:** Emergency pivot → $2K revenue by Jun 30, $10K by Aug 31 (extended timeline).

## SCENARIO 5: ONE STREAM OVERPERFORMS, TWO UNDERPERFORM

**Example:** P1 hits $5K by May 31, but P2 + P3 stall at $200.

### Rebalance Strategy
1. **Winner acceleration:** Scale P1 spend (20 hrs/week → 30 hrs/week), maximize revenue before market saturation
2. **Loser consolidation:** Shut down underperforming streams, reallocate 10 hrs/week to P1 scaling
3. **Recovery math:** If P1 alone can hit $7-10K by Jun 30, accept that as "recovery achieved" and reassign P2+P3 to Month 6+ backlog

**New focus:** Single-stream mastery (focus founder energy on what's working vs. spreading thin).

## FOUNDER INTENSIVE MODE (If Needed)

If blended revenue <$2K by May 31, trigger **Founder Intensive** phase:
- **Time commitment:** 40-50 hrs/week (up from 15-20 hrs/week)
- **Focus:** Hands-on sales for P2, direct traffic acquisition for P1, content publishing for P3
- **Hiring:** Bring on 1 COO contractor to handle execution coordination ($1-2K/month)
- **Duration:** 4 weeks intensive (Jun 1-30) to hit $10K recovery or execute final pivot
- **Decision gate:** If intensive push still fails → Emergency contingency stream launch OR restructure recovery timeline to 6 months

---

## DECISION FRAMEWORK (Shutdown vs. Pivot vs. Scale)

**For each stream, ask:**
1. **Is there evidence of demand?** (1+ customer, 50+ interested prospects, or growing organic traffic)
   - **Yes:** Pivot or scale (don't shutdown)
   - **No:** Shutdown, reallocate to winning stream or new stream

2. **Is the bottleneck fixable?** (traffic, messaging, pricing, sales approach)
   - **Yes:** Allocate resources to fix (1-2 week experiment)
   - **No:** Shutdown, it's a product-market fit issue

3. **What's the opportunity cost?** (20 hrs/week on failing stream vs. scaling winner)
   - **If opportunity cost > potential upside:** Shutdown, reallocate
   - **If opportunity cost < potential upside:** Invest in pivot
