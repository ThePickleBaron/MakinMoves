## Key Findings Summary

### Finding 1: 90 Days Is Aspirational
- Industry data: 4-5 months for bootstrap revenue
- CFO model: "Late July-early August" = Aug 31
- Execution risk: 65% burnout probability by Jun 30
- **Verdict:** Plan optimistically, expect realistically

### Finding 2: Slog Phase Is 6-8 Weeks
- Week 4-5: Energy crashes 40-60%
- Week 6-8: Quality collapse if not mitigated
- Recovery founders: Longer slog due to emotional load
- **Verdict:** 30-day recovery buffer essential

### Finding 3: Revenue Timeline ≠ Effort Timeline
- P1: Effort Day 45, revenue Day 60-90
- P2: Effort Day 30, revenue Day 75-90
- P3: Effort Day 45, revenue Day 120-180 (SEO maturity)
- **Verdict:** Jun 30 is effort midpoint, Aug 31 is revenue endpoint

### Finding 4: Founder Energy Validates at Day 120
- Day 90: Exhausted, needs proof. Jun 30 shows $1.2K (not enough)
- Day 120: Has trajectory. Aug 31 shows $10K (validation)
- **Verdict:** Extended timeline improves psychological outcome

### Finding 5: Decision Gates Work Better at Day 120
- Day 30 gates: 30-40% data (too early)
- Day 60 gates: 60-70% data (mixed signals)
- Day 90 gates: 50-80% data (incomplete)
- **Day 39/75/120:** 60-90% data (clear decisions)
- **Verdict:** Gates move to Days 39/75/120

**Q: "Why extend to 120 days if CFO already forecasts Aug 31?"**
A: Exactly. We're aligning plan with reality, not pretending Jun 30 is achievable.

**Q: "Can't we just push harder for 90 days?"**
