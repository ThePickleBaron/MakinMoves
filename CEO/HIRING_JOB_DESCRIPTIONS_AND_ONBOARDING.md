## JOB 1: FREELANCE WRITER (P2 AFFILIATE BLOG)
**Posting Date:** April 19 (Day 20)
**Start Date:** April 24 (Day 25)
**Duration:** 4 months (May-August)

### JOB TITLE
**SEO/Freelance Content Writer — SaaS Automation & Digital Tools Niche**

### POSITION TYPE
Part-time contract, 4-10 hours/week

### SALARY
$1.6K-2.4K per month (~$400-600/week, $100-150/hour equivalent)

### LOCATION
Remote (async preferred, US timezone preferred but not required)

### ABOUT THE ROLE

We're a solopreneur-to-portfolio agency helping freelancers and digital creators scale with smart automation and AI tools. We're launching an affiliate blog + freelance writing service and need a **mid-level SEO copywriter** to:

1. **Write 2+ articles/week** (1K-1.5K words) for freelancer-focused affiliate site
   - Topics: Email marketing, project management, automation tools, time tracking
   - Style: Friendly, practical, SEO-optimized (target: "best [tool] for freelancers")
   - Format: Comparison articles, tutorials, tool reviews
   - Goal: Rank for high-intent keywords within 6 months

2. **Manage 2-3 client retainers** (freelance writing clients)
   - Communicate scope + deadlines with clients
   - Deliver 1-2 articles/week per client
   - Manage revisions (<2 rounds per article)
   - Track hours + invoice

3. **Coordinate with me (CEO)** on:
   - Weekly article planning (keywords + angles)
   - Client communication (introduction, scope confirmation)
   - Quality/SEO review (I provide feedback on drafts)
   - Email sequences (1-2 promotional emails for articles)

### MUST-HAVE QUALIFICATIONS
- 2+ years of freelance copywriting or content marketing experience
- Proven ability to write 2+ articles/week consistently
- Understanding of SEO (keyword research, on-page optimization, CTAs)
- Experience with SaaS/tech tools (software, automation, etc.)
- Reliable turnaround (3-7 day delivery on articles)
- Comfortable with email + message updates (async, not Slack/calls)

### NICE-TO-HAVE
- Experience with WordPress or content management
- Familiarity with affiliate marketing or e-commerce
- Background in freelance services or automation
- Experience with Zapier, Make, or no-code tools
- Portfolio with 5+ published articles

### WHAT YOU'LL GET
- Flexible schedule (4-10 hours/week, you choose)
- Clear scope (2 articles/week + 2 client accounts, no scope creep)
- Async communication (no meetings, email/Slack only)
- Regular feedback on quality
- Opportunity to scale to higher rates if successful
- Potential for long-term partnership (Month 9+)

### WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR
Not a marketing agency. Not a junior writer. Not AI-generated content.

We want a **mid-level freelancer who:**
- Can research a tool, interview users (via Reddit/Twitter), and write a 1.5K article in 4-5 hours
- Understands reader psychology ("What do freelancers actually care about?")
- Can balance SEO keywords with readable, helpful copy
- Works independently (minimal hand-holding)
- Cares about quality (not just word count)

### HOW TO APPLY
**Send us:** (in email body, not attachment)
1. Your name + timezone
2. Why you're interested in this role
3. Link to 2-3 portfolio pieces (published articles you're proud of)
4. Your typical turnaround time for a 1.5K article
5. Availability: Can you start April 24?

### SCREENING CRITERIA
- ✓ Portfolio shows 1.5K+ articles (not 500-word blog posts)
- ✓ Writing quality is clear, helpful, not generic
- ✓ SEO fundamentals evident (meta descriptions, headings, CTAs)
- ✓ Can commit to 2+ articles/week for 4+ months
- ✓ Timezone compatible (US preferred, but flex for good fit)

## JOB 2: SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGER / VA
**Posting Date:** May 14 (Day 45)
**Start Date:** May 19 (Day 50)
**Duration:** 4 months (June-August)

### JOB TITLE
**Social Media Manager / Virtual Assistant — Twitter & Email Marketing**

### POSITION TYPE
Part-time contract, 8-12 hours/week

### SALARY
$1.2K-1.5K per month (~$250-375/week, $20-30/hour equivalent for tasks)

### LOCATION
Remote (async, any timezone okay)

### ABOUT THE ROLE

We're scaling a digital education + services business. I (CEO/founder) need someone to manage **Twitter & Substack** so I can focus on product development and customer relationships.

You'll handle:

1. **Twitter Content (70% of time)**
   - Schedule 15-20 tweets/week from pre-written content (I provide ideas/themes)
   - Bulk-create thread templates monthly (3-5 threads about automation, freelancing, tools)
   - Respond to replies/mentions within 24 hours (batch daily)
   - Monitor trends in our niche (automation, freelancing, AI)
   - No content creation — just scheduling + engagement management

2. **Substack Email (20% of time)**
   - Format weekly newsletter in Substack
   - Schedule send time + optimize preview text
   - Track open rates + link clicks (report weekly)
   - Add images/formatting to raw article text (I provide copy)

3. **Analytics & Reporting (10% of time)**
   - Weekly email with Twitter metrics: followers gained, impressions, engagement rate
   - Identify which tweet types perform best
   - Flag trending topics for content ideas
   - Monthly report: 1-page summary of growth

### MUST-HAVE QUALIFICATIONS
- 1+ year social media experience (personal account, brand account, or VA role)
- Proficiency with Twitter/X platform
- Comfortable with Buffer, Later, or Substack scheduling tools
- Reliable responsiveness (reply to DMs/comments within 24 hours)
- Attention to detail (grammar, formatting, brand voice)
- Async communication (no meetings, email/Slack only)

### NICE-TO-HAVE
- Experience with email marketing (ConvertKit, Beehiiv, Substack)
- Analytics experience (Google Analytics, Twitter analytics)
- Copywriting skills (not required, but helpful)
- Background in tech/SaaS/freelancing (understands our niche)

### WHAT YOU'LL GET
- Flexible hours (8-12/week, you choose when)
- Clear task list (no vague "manage socials")
- Pre-written content (I provide tweets/themes, you schedule)
- Regular feedback on performance
- Professional relationship (no drama, no scope creep)

### WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR
Not a marketing agency. Not a content creator. Not someone who "grows TikTok."

We want a **organized VA who:**
- Can schedule tweets in bulk using Buffer/Later without error
- Responds to comments professionally and on-brand
- Tracks metrics and spots patterns
- Works asynchronously (I won't be in Slack all day)
- Understands that consistency matters more than virality

### HOW TO APPLY
**Send us:** (in email body)
1. Your name + timezone
2. Social media platforms you've managed
3. Link to a Twitter profile or social account you've managed
4. Example: "A month of tweets I'd schedule for a [your previous client/brand]"
5. Availability: Can you start May 19?

### SCREENING CRITERIA
- ✓ Has managed at least one social account professionally
- ✓ Twitter profile shows engagement (replies, community building)
- ✓ Can use Buffer/Later (or willing to learn in 1 day)
- ✓ Responds to application within 24 hours (shows responsiveness)
- ✓ Timezone allows batch daily responses (GMT-7 to GMT+5 preferred)

## JOB 3: EMAIL MARKETING DESIGNER / AUTOMATION SPECIALIST
**Posting Date:** June 8 (Day 70)
**Start Date:** June 15 (Day 75)
**Duration:** 2-3 months (July-September, optional)

### JOB TITLE
**Email Marketing Specialist — Automation & Funnel Design**

### POSITION TYPE
Part-time contract, 6-8 hours/week

### SALARY
$1.0K-1.2K per month (~$250-300/week, $35-50/hour equivalent)

### LOCATION
Remote (async, any timezone)

### ABOUT THE ROLE

We're building **automated email funnels** across three revenue streams (digital products, affiliate blog, freelance services). You'll design email sequences and set up automation so our customers get nurture emails automatically.

Tasks:

1. **Email Sequence Design (50% of time)**
   - Design P1→P2 cross-sell sequence (4-6 weeks, nurture digital product buyers into freelance service customers)
   - Design P1→P3 affiliate promotion sequence (2-3 weeks, promote affiliate tools to our email list)
   - Design P2 customer onboarding sequence (3-4 weeks, welcome + value delivery + upsell)
   - Each sequence: outline (3-5 email structure) + copy + timeline
   - Note: I provide raw copy/ideas, you structure + optimize

2. **Zapier / Automation Setup (30% of time)**
   - Build Zapier workflows: trigger (signup) → action (send email sequence)
   - Connect email platform (ConvertKit/Beehiiv) to our CRM
   - Test workflows (send test emails, verify automation)
   - Document workflows for future troubleshooting
   - Monitor automation performance (open rates, click rates)

3. **A/B Testing & Optimization (20% of time)**
   - Identify high-performer emails (which subject lines convert best?)
   - Recommend tests (subject line, send time, CTA copy)
   - Report monthly: conversion rates per sequence, revenue attribution
   - Suggest improvements

### MUST-HAVE QUALIFICATIONS
- 1+ year email marketing experience (ConvertKit, Mailchimp, Beehiiv, or similar)
- Proficiency with Zapier, Make, or similar automation tools
- Understanding of email funnel design (welcome sequences, nurture, upsell)
- Attention to detail (formatting, copywriting, brand voice)
- Comfortable with email analytics (open rate, click rate, conversion tracking)
- Async communication (no Zoom calls, email/Slack updates only)

### NICE-TO-HAVE
- Experience with UTM tracking + attribution
- HTML/CSS knowledge (not required, but helpful for email formatting)
- Familiarity with SaaS email best practices
- Experience with A/B testing and optimization
- Background in digital marketing or product

### WHAT YOU'LL GET
- Clear project scope (3 sequences + 2 automations + testing)
- Pre-written email copy (I provide raw material, you structure)
- Async workflow (no meetings, structured feedback)
- Opportunity to scale if sequences perform well
- Portfolio piece (you can reference the funnel results)

### WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR
Not a copywriter. Not a designer. Not a full-service marketer.

We want an **email automation specialist who:**
- Understands customer lifecycle (awareness → consideration → decision)
- Can set up Zapier workflows without hand-holding
- Spots conversion leaks in email funnels
- Tracks data (open rate, click rate, revenue per email)
- Communicates clearly about technical constraints

### HOW TO APPLY
**Send us:** (in email body)
1. Your name + timezone
2. Email platforms you've managed (ConvertKit, Beehiiv, etc.)
3. Example Zapier workflow you've built (screenshot + description)
4. Why you're interested in this role
5. Availability: Can you start June 15?

### SCREENING CRITERIA
- ✓ Portfolio shows 2+ email sequences they've designed
- ✓ Demonstrates Zapier/automation experience (workflow example)
- ✓ Email copy examples show persuasive funnel thinking
- ✓ Can articulate why X email sequence works (strategic thinking)
- ✓ Timezone allows async communication (GMT-7 to GMT+5 preferred)

## ONBOARDING CHECKLIST (FOR EACH HIRE)

### Week 1: Foundation
- [ ] Send onboarding doc (role overview, tools, expectations)
- [ ] Schedule 30-min async intro call (I send Loom video intro, they send response)
- [ ] Add to Slack/email group (async communication only)
- [ ] Provide access to tools (Gumroad, WordPress, Zapier, Buffer, etc.)
- [ ] First task: Light (2-3 hours max)

### Week 2: Ramp-Up
- [ ] 2-3 examples from previous work (show quality standard)
- [ ] First major task (but with heavy feedback)
- [ ] Weekly sync: 1 email check-in (5 min read, any blockers?)
- [ ] Feedback on first deliverables (be specific, positive, actionable)

### Week 3: Full Productivity
- [ ] Independent work (minimal input from me)
- [ ] Weekly check-in continues
- [ ] Feedback on quality + speed
- [ ] Any blockers resolved by Day 21

### Week 4+: Ongoing
- [ ] Bi-weekly sync (instead of weekly)
- [ ] Monthly performance review email (2 min read)
- [ ] Feedback on any issues
- [ ] Raise rate if exceeding expectations by Month 2
