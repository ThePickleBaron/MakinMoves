| Founder Brand | 8-9/10 | ✅ Keep building |
| Email List | 8-9/10 | ✅ Protect at all costs |
| Niche Positioning | 7/10 | ✅ Reinforce daily |

## DAILY DECISION CHECKLIST

**Before launching a product, publishing content, or making a major decision, ask:**

- [ ] **Does this reinforce founder brand?** (If no, reconsider.)
- [ ] **Does this integrate with P1/P2/P3?** (If no, how does it fit?)
- [ ] **Does this target a specific underdog?** (If generic, reposition.)
- [ ] **Is there warm traffic support?** (Email, Twitter, partnerships?)
- [ ] **Can this be automated?** (If yes, build workflow before launch.)
- [ ] **How defensible is this vs. competitors?** (Will they copy in 2 weeks or 2 months?)

**If you can't answer 4+ of these "yes," pause and rethink.**

## WHAT NOT TO DO

❌ **Don't position as "best" anything.** (Compete on authenticity, not rankings.)
❌ **Don't launch without warm traffic.** (Plan email/Twitter/partnerships first.)
❌ **Don't build for "everyone."** (Pick a specific underdog, go deep.)
❌ **Don't ignore cross-stream integration.** (P1→P2, P2→P3—make them work together.)
❌ **Don't neglect founder visibility.** (Brand is your biggest defensible advantage.)
