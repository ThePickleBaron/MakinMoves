   Verdict: REVISIT Month 4+. Not worth mid-launch pivot.

   Pros: Recurring revenue, community moat
   Cons: 5-10 hrs/week management, slow ramp (Month 4 before 50 members)
   Verdict: PHASE 2 option. Too slow for initial launch.

3. AGENCY (Done-for-You Services)
   Pros: $3K-5K/mo ceiling, recurring clients, best defensibility
   Cons: 8-12 week sales cycle (Month 3 first revenue), requires hiring
   Verdict: STRONG Phase 2 option. Sub-optimal for initial launch.

4. SaaS TOOL
   Pros: Passive revenue, $10K achievable, defensible
   Cons: 2-4 week build time, no revenue until Month 2-3, product-market risk
   Verdict: TOO RISKY for immediate launch. Better Month 4+ with revenue to fund.

5. HYPERSPECIALIZATION (SaaS Niche)
   Pros: 2-3x higher conversion, premium pricing
   Cons: Smaller TAM, niche saturation risk
   Verdict: EASY, LOW-RISK experiment. Test Week 1 (2 hours). Reversible.
