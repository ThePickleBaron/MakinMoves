## RECOMMENDED PATH: Canva Instagram Template Bundle (90 minutes)

**Why this:** Fastest creation, highest volume, immediate validation.

---

## TIMELINE & CHECKLIST

### PHASE 1: Research & Planning (15 minutes)

- [ ] Open Etsy.com
- [ ] Search one of these trending niches:
  - "Instagram templates for fitness coaches"
  - "Instagram templates for therapists"
  - "Pinterest templates for small business"
  - "Instagram templates for solopreneurs"
- [ ] Click "Best Selling" sort
- [ ] Note top 3 products:
  - Color scheme used
  - Number of templates in bundle (note: 15-50 is sweet spot)
  - Price ($25-39 is most common)
  - Keywords in title (copy these down)
- [ ] Example title structure: "[Quantity] Editable [Platform] Templates for [Specific Niche] | Canva"

**Save this to a notepad:**
```
Niche: [your choice]
Top competitor 1: [URL] - Price: $___
Top competitor 2: [URL] - Price: $___
Top competitor 3: [URL] - Price: $___
My title: [write it here]
My price: $[19-39]
Colors to use: [3 colors noted from competitors]
```

---

### PHASE 2: Create Templates in Canva (45 minutes)

**Step 1: Open Canva (5 min)**
- Go to canva.com
- Sign up free (or log in)
- Search: "[Your niche] template" (e.g., "fitness coach instagram template")
- Browse designs, pick 5 that you like

**Step 2: Customize base templates (25 min)**
- For each of 5 templates:
  - Click "Edit this template"
  - Change colors to your 3-color scheme
  - Change main text to fitness/niche-related copy:
    - "Book Your Free Consultation"
    - "New Client Special"
    - "Join My Challenge"
    - "Success Story"
    - "Services Offered"
  - Replace any images with free Canva stock images
  - Download as PNG
- **Total: 5 base templates, customized**

**Step 3: Create color variations (15 min)**
- Take your 5 templates
- Re-edit each one with different color variations:
  - Variation A: Light bg + dark text
  - Variation B: Dark bg + light text
  - Variation C: Accent color dominant
- Download each (you now have 15 templates)

**Pro tip:** Use Canva's "Brand kit" feature (Pro only) to apply colors to all at once = 2 min vs 15 min

---

### PHASE 3: Package & Create Etsy Listing (30 minutes)

**Step 1: Prepare files (5 min)**
- Create folder: "Instagram Templates - [Your Niche]"
- Move all 15 downloaded PNGs to this folder
- Create ZIP file (right-click → Compress)

**Step 2: Create Etsy account (5 min)** [If not already done]
- Go to Etsy.com
- Click "Sell on Etsy"
- Fill basics: email, password, name

**Step 3: Create first listing (15 min)**
- Click "Add a product"
- **Title:** (Use your researched template from Phase 1)
  - Example: "15 Editable Instagram Templates for Fitness Coaches | Canva + Etsy"
- **Price:** $29.99 (or match competitor -$5)
- **Category:** Digital Products > Digital Design Template
- **Files:** Upload your ZIP file with 15 templates
- **Description template:**
  ```
  Includes 15 ready-to-edit Instagram templates for [your niche]

  WHAT'S INCLUDED:
  - 5 promotional templates
  - 5 testimonial/success story templates
  - 5 service/offer templates

  Each template is fully editable in Canva (free or Pro)
  No design experience needed

  USE FOR:
  - Social media marketing
  - Promoting your services
  - Building social presence
  - Client testimonials

  BONUS: Comes as both PNG + editable Canva links (easy resizing)

  Questions? Message me!
  ```
- **Upload preview image:** Take 3 screenshot collages showing templates
  - Use Canva to make a nice collage: screenshot each template, put 4-6 in a grid
  - This is your cover image
- **Tags/SEO:** Use keywords from Phase 1 research
  - Examples: [niche] instagram templates, canva templates, editable instagram posts
- **Publish**
