EXSITUINNOVATIONS — COMPETITIVE MOAT SUMMARY
============================================

QUESTION: Why can't competitors just copy us?

SHORT ANSWER:
They can copy individual products, but they can't copy:
1. Jason's founder story (we're the "recovering entrepreneur" brand)
2. Our SEO rankings (takes 6-12 months to replicate)
3. Our email lists (competitors start at zero)
4. Our speed (we innovate faster)
5. Our retainer relationships (high switching cost)

---

MOAT STRENGTH BY STREAM:
========================

FOUNDER BRAND: 9/10 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Why defensible: Can't copy Jason's story
- Erosion timeline: Ongoing (if Jason stays visible)
- Effort: Weekly Twitter/email (maintenance light)
- Examples: "Buy templates from Jason the underdog"
- Verdict: STRONGEST moat, spans all streams

AFFILIATE MARKETING: 8/10 ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Why defensible: SEO rankings stick; email list compounds
- Erosion timeline: 9-12 months
- Effort: 1 article/week (moderate maintenance)
- Examples: "Our articles rank #1 before competitors' exist"
- Verdict: STRONG, longest-lasting moat

FREELANCE WRITING: 8/10 ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Why defensible: Personal relationships, retainer lock-in
- Erosion timeline: Ongoing (if Jason keeps clients happy)
- Effort: Client management (moderate)
- Examples: "Client switching cost = find new writer + rebuild trust"
- Verdict: STRONG, but limited to Jason's time capacity

DIGITAL PRODUCTS: 6/10 ⭐⭐⭐
- Why defensible: Speed + specificity + founder brand halo
- Erosion timeline: 6 months
- Effort: New product every 2 weeks (high)
- Examples: "They copy template, we launch 2 more in that time"
- Verdict: MODERATE, requires constant innovation

PRINT-ON-DEMAND: 5/10 ⭐⭐
- Why defensible: Portfolio size + niche focus (mostly not defensible)
- Erosion timeline: 4 months
- Effort: 2-3 designs/week (very high)
- Examples: "They copy 1 design, we have 49 others + new designs weekly"
- Verdict: WEAK, relies on volume + speed
