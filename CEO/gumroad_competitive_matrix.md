## Fastest Path to $1K Revenue (By Audience Stage)

### Cold Start (No Audience)
```
TIMELINE: 8-12 weeks

Week 1-2:   Build Twitter account, post daily on niche topic
Week 3-4:   Grow to 500 followers, create lead magnet (free template/guide)
Week 5-6:   Build email list to 300-500
Week 7-8:   Launch niche AI prompt library ($19.99)
Week 9-12:  Convert 50+ email subscribers + Twitter followers = $1K+
```

### Warm Start (1K-5K Audience)
```
TIMELINE: 4-8 weeks

Week 1-2:   Announce niche product, create opt-in for email list
Week 2-3:   Launch niche Notion template ($29) to warm audience
Week 3-4:   Reach 500+ email subscribers
Week 5:     Add second product (complementary, $19.99) = bundle
Week 6-8:   Achieve $1K-$3K combined revenue
```

### Hot Start (5K+ Audience)
```
TIMELINE: 2-4 weeks

Week 1:     Announce product to audience + email list
Week 2:     Launch Notion template/course ($29-$99)
Week 3-4:   Hit $1K+ from warm launch
            (30+ sales @ $30-40 = $1K from 2-3% conversion)
```

## Key Metrics to Track

- **Conversion Rate:** Target 2-5% for cold traffic, 10-20% for warm audience
- **Average Sale:** Target $20-$50 per transaction
- **Customer Acquisition Cost:** $0 (organic/owned audience) to $2-5 (ads)
- **Email List Value:** $1-5 per subscriber over lifetime
- **Product Velocity:** 50+ sales/month = sustainable; 200+/month = scalable
- **Launch Size:** 100 sales in first week = strong signal; 10-20 = weak signal
