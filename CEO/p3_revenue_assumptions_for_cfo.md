## KEY METRICS TO MONITOR (CFO)

**Metric 1: Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)**
- Formula: (Affiliate commission paid out) / (Number of signups)
- Target: $0 (all revenue is passive affiliate commission, no direct costs)
- Monitor: Monthly, by niche

**Metric 2: Affiliate Commission Mix**
- Track: % of revenue from ConvertKit vs. Jasper vs. Notion, etc.
- Why: High-commission programs (30%+) vs. low-commission (5%) changes MRR
- Monitor: Quarterly, adjust content strategy accordingly

**Metric 3: Traffic Quality Score**
- Formula: (Affiliate signups) / (Total visitors)
- Target: 2-4% (decent conversion)
- Monitor: Monthly per niche
- Action: If <1%, improve article quality or affiliate placement

**Metric 4: Email List Growth Rate**
- Target: 50-100+ new subscribers/month by Month 3
- Why: Email = owned audience, hedge against Google algorithm changes
- Monitor: Weekly

**Metric 5: SEO Keyword Rankings**
- Track: # of keywords ranking Page 1, Page 2, Page 3+
- Target: 8-12 keywords on Page 1 by Month 6
- Monitor: Weekly using Google Search Console (free)

## RISK FACTORS & MITIGATION

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Ranking takes 14+ weeks** | Revenue delayed by 4-8 weeks | Medium | Write 12+ articles upfront (faster indexing) |
| **Competitors copy our angle** | Margin compression over time | Low | Build community moat, email list, case studies |
| **Affiliate program commission cuts** | Revenue drops 20-30% | Medium | Diversify to 5+ programs per niche, negotiate higher rates |
| **Algorithm penalty** | Traffic drops to zero | Very Low | Focus on quality, no black-hat SEO, natural links |
| **Topic saturation** | CPA increases, conversion drops | Medium | Vertical specialization (design, copywriting, etc.) |
| **Market volatility** (AI tools change fast) | Content becomes outdated | High | Monthly updates, rapid response to changes |

## DECISION POINTS FOR CFO

1. **Do we invest in paid traffic** (Google Ads, Pinterest) to accelerate Month 1-3?
   - CFO Recommendation: No. Organic is free; establish authority first, then paid (Month 4+)

2. **Do we hire a dedicated content writer** for P3 (full-time)?
   - CFO Recommendation: No. Use P2 freelance writers (already budgeted). If Month 6 revenue exceeds $15K/mo, hire dedicated writer.

3. **Do we diversify affiliate networks** (ShareASale, Impact, CJ) or focus on direct affiliate programs?
   - CFO Recommendation: Direct affiliate programs (ConvertKit, Jasper) have better commissions. Use networks as secondary tier.

4. **What's the revenue threshold to hire a community manager?**
   - CFO Recommendation: $5K/mo per niche. At that point, building community moat becomes more valuable than content volume.
