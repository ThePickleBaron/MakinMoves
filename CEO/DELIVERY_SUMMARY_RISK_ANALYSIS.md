## CRITICAL PROBLEMS ADDRESSED

### 1. P1 (Digital Products) Failures
**Risk:** Gumroad account ban / wrong niche / wrong pricing
**Contingency:** 2 backup platforms (SendOwl, Podia) + niche pivot plan + A/B test lower price
**Timeline to recover:** 48 hours (platform) to 1 week (niche pivot)
**Revenue at risk:** $3K-7K/month

### 2. P2 (Affiliate Blog) Failures
**Risk:** Affiliate networks reject / email spam / no traffic / low email signup
**Contingency:** 5 backup networks + Substack email platform + manual traffic seeding
**Timeline to recover:** 1-3 days (network) to 1 week (email)
**Revenue at risk:** $100-500/month

### 3. P3 (PoD + N8N) Failures
**Risk:** Redbubble rejections / design quality poor / n8n automation breaks
**Contingency:** Printful backup platform + quality QA + manual data import
**Timeline to recover:** 24 hours (platform) to 2 weeks (quality)
**Revenue at risk:** $200-600/month

### 4. Founder Burnout Risk
**Risk:** Jason working 60+ hours/week → quality tanks, deadlines miss
**Contingency:** Descope P2 OR hire writer OR hire designer (descope is easiest)
**Timeline to activate:** Immediate (same day detection)
**Revenue at risk:** -30% to -80% if not addressed

### 5. Payment Processing Failures
**Risk:** Stripe account suspended / payment processing blocked
**Contingency:** Square + 2Checkout accounts pre-approved + ready to activate
**Timeline to recover:** 2 hours to switch
**Revenue at risk:** -100% collection (temporary, 24-48 hour gap)

### 6. Cross-Stream Market Collapse
**Risk:** All 3 streams fail simultaneously (wrong niche selected)
**Contingency:** 2 backup niches researched + lite product specs ready
**Timeline to pivot:** 1 week (rewrite content + relaunch)
**Revenue at risk:** -100% (but can reset with new niche)

## DEPENDENCY INSIGHTS

### What Breaks Everything
1. **Founder burnout** (60+ hours/week unsustainable)
   - Causes: -80% revenue if not fixed by Week 4
   - Solution: Descope P2 OR hire within 1 week

2. **Wrong niche selection** (market doesn't want what you're selling)
   - Causes: -100% revenue across all 3 streams
   - Solution: Pre-identify 2 backup niches, pivot by Week 5

3. **Payment processor failure** (can't collect money)
   - Causes: -100% cash flow temporarily
   - Solution: Pre-build backup processors (4-hour switch)

### What's Resilient
1. **Platform-specific failures** (Gumroad banned, Stripe suspended)
   - Impact: -30% revenue (platform-specific, not fatal)
   - Solution: Backup platforms ready (48-hour switch)
   - Resilience: HIGH (multiple platforms per stream)

2. **Email/content delivery issues** (spam, low signup)
   - Impact: -12% to -50% revenue (P2 specific)
   - Solution: Platform switch or CTA improvement (1-3 days)
   - Resilience: MEDIUM (easy to fix or switch)

3. **Content quality issues** (low ATC, poor designs)
   - Impact: -30% to -50% revenue (but fixable)
   - Solution: A/B test, hire designer, improve QA (2-3 weeks)
   - Resilience: MEDIUM (take time to fix but solvable)
