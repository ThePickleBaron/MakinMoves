# One-Page Summary: Apr 1-8 UTM Test Validity

---

## THE QUESTION
**Is the planned Apr 1-8 UTM attribution test (email vs. Twitter) statistically valid for deciding channel priority?**

## THE ANSWER
**No.** Test has 6 critical flaws and will likely produce wrong channel decision (50% error rate).

---

## THE 6 FLAWS

1. **Sample Size (N=75)** — Need N=200+ for statistical significance
2. **Launch Halo** — Apr 1 spike dominates signal, not representative
3. **UTM Blind Spot** — 40% of revenue unattributed (organic/direct)
4. **Effort Confound** — Can't separate channel quality from effort level
5. **Missing Sustainability** — Doesn't measure list/follower growth
6. **Product Quality** — Assumes product is good (unknown)

---

## WHAT Apr 1-8 WILL SHOW (LIKELY)
```
Email: 30-40 visitors, 1-3 sales
Twitter: 15-25 visitors, 0-1 sales
→ Conclusion: "Email is 2-3x better"
```

## WHAT IT ACTUALLY MEANS
"Email worked better during high-effort launch week" (unreliable)
