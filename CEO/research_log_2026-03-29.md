## Key Findings

### Market Size & Structure
- **Total Annual Revenue:** $200M+ across 146K+ products
- **NOT Oversaturated:** Market is under-segmented (specific niches underserved 2-10x)
- **Revenue Varies by Niche:** $2.1K (Photography) to $60.8K (Software) average per product

### Top Opportunity Categories
1. **Writing & Publishing:** 226 products | $15,750 avg/product (BEST)
2. **Fitness & Health:** 379 products | $11,046 avg/product (SECOND)
3. **Affinity Software:** 121 products | ~$8K-$12K avg/product (EMERGING)
4. **Business & Money:** $15.4M market | $10,130 avg/product

### Fastest Entry Products
1. **AI Prompt Library:** 3-5 days build | $14.99-$24.99 price
2. **Niche Notion Template:** 1-2 weeks build | $24.99-$39.99 price
3. **eBook:** 1 week build | $19.99-$29.99 price

### Pricing Reality
- Sweet spot: $20-$49 (top 1% sellers use this range)
- Best conversion: $24.99-$39.99 for templates
- Avoid: Generic $5 products (volume play, low margins)

### Audience Building (Critical Success Factor)
- 64% of successful creators use Twitter as primary channel
- Email list converts at 5-15%
- Timeline to first $1K: 2-4 weeks (warm audience) vs. 8-12 weeks (cold start)
