## RED FLAGS & ESCALATION

**If any of these happen, escalate to CEO immediately:**

1. **No articles ranking by Week 8** — May indicate SEO issue (technical or content quality)
2. **Zero affiliate clicks by Week 6** — May indicate affiliate link placement issue or traffic quality issue
3. **Competitor launches exact same niche** — May need positioning pivot
4. **Affiliate program shuts down or cuts commission** — May need program substitution
5. **Traffic drops >30% week-to-week** — May indicate algorithm penalty or technical issue
