# 90-Day Vision & Decision Gates (Mar 29 - Jun 30)

**Objective:** Execute Trinity revenue recovery ($10K cumulative by Month 3) with clear go/no-go decision points.

---

## MONTH 1 (Apr 1-30): Launch & Foundation

### Week 1 (Apr 1-5): P1 Gumroad + Content Launch
- **P1:** Product suite live, 3 traffic channels activated (email seeding, Twitter launch, Medium cross-post)
- **P2:** Substack/Medium foundation (Week 1 content calendar published)
- **P3:** Email Marketing niche site live (5 pillar articles published)
- **Metrics target:** 50+ external visitors to P1 by Apr 5, Substack >20 subscribers

### Week 2-3 (Apr 6-19): Sales Funnel & SEO Crawl
- **P1:** Run traffic experiments, measure conversion by channel (email, Twitter, Medium)
- **P2:** First outbound emails to 20 target clients (SaaS marketing decision-makers)
- **P3:** Freelance Tools niche site live, AI Writing Tools niche site live (15 total P3 articles)
- **Metrics target:** P1 $200-500 revenue, P2 3+ qualified leads, P3 indexed in Google

### Week 4 (Apr 20-30): Optimize & Close First Customers
- **P1:** Scale top-performing traffic source (email or Twitter), A/B test product messaging
- **P2:** Close first customer (1 Growth Articles or Blog Stack package)
- **P3:** First affiliate conversions appearing (target 2-5)
- **Metrics target:** P1 $500+, P2 $600+, P3 $100+

**🚨 DECISION GATE (Apr 30):**
- **P1:** Revenue ≥ $500 AND external traffic > 200 visitors. **No:** Pivot traffic strategy or sunset.
- **P2:** 3+ qualified leads in pipeline AND first customer interested. **No:** Pivot to lower-touch service or shutdown.
- **P3:** All 3 sites indexed in Google AND >50 organic visitors cumulative. **No:** Reallocate content effort to P1/P2.

---

## MONTH 2 (May 1-31): Scale & Profitability

### Week 5-6 (May 1-12): Double Down on Winners
- **P1:** Scale highest-converting traffic channel (likely email), increase frequency to 2x/week pre-launch messaging
- **P2:** Target 2 new customers (aim for 1 Blog Stack $1,200/mo package)
- **P3:** Publish 16 articles (sites generating >500 organic visits cumulative)
- **Metrics target:** P1 $1.5K cumulative, P2 $1.2K-1.8K MRR potential, P3 $200-400 revenue

### Week 7-8 (May 13-26): Consolidation & Writer Backup Hire
- **P1:** Optimize product based on customer feedback (bundle offers, pricing tests)
- **P2:** Hire backup writer (critical by end of Week 8 for Month 3 scaling — $1.5K-2K/mo)
- **P3:** Launch affiliate partnerships (ConvertKit, Jasper, Beehiiv direct API integrations for tracking)
- **Metrics target:** Establish P2 operations with 2-writer capacity, P3 affiliate links live

### Week 9 (May 27-31): Final May Push
- **P1:** Prepare new product bundle (3-pack deal), measure lifetime customer value
- **P2:** Close 3rd customer, establish standard sales cycle (8-12 weeks per sales best practices)
- **P3:** Weekly affiliate revenue tracking live (dashboard in Notion/Sheets)
- **Metrics target:** P1 $2K+, P2 $2.4K-3.6K MRR potential, P3 $500+ cumulative

**🚨 DECISION GATE (May 31):**
- **Blended revenue target:** $3K+ cumulative (May cumulative, not monthly). **No:** Emergency pivot — reallocate capital/time to 1 stream.
- **P1 health:** MRR sustainable ≥ $500. **No:** Sunset or transition to seasonal/promotional model.
- **P2 health:** 2+ active customers, writer capacity hired. **No:** Reduce scope to single-writer max capacity.
- **P3 health:** Traffic >1K organic/week across sites. **No:** Reallocate to content marketing for P1/P2.

---

## MONTH 3 (Jun 1-30): Recovery Target

### Week 10-13 (Jun 1-30): Full Sprint to Recovery
- **P1:** Target $3K+ revenue (sustain $500-1K/mo run rate, possible one-time promotions)
- **P2:** 2-3 active customers generating $2K-3.6K MRR potential ($2.4K expected with writer backup)
- **P3:** Affiliate conversions generating $1.5K-2K+ revenue
- **P3 secondary:** Publish 12 more articles (36 total by end of Month 3), target organic ranking improvements
- **Metrics target:** $10K cumulative recovery ($3K P1 + $3K P2 + $2K P3 minimum)

**Recovery gate:** Achieve $10K cumulative revenue. If shortfall:
- P1 underperforms: Extend P3 timeline, emergency P2 customer acquisition campaign
- P2 underperforms: Aggressive P1 seasonal offer, scale P3 affiliate commission per sale
- P3 underperforms: Accept Month 4-6 lag, focus capital on P1+P2

---

## DECISION FRAMEWORK

### Go/No-Go Criteria
Each gate is binary. If a stream misses its metric:
- **P1:** Organic revenue problem = traffic strategy failure → pivot or sunset
- **P2:** Sales problem = demand validation failure → reduce scope or shutdown
- **P3:** SEO problem = content quality/quantity failure → reallocate effort or sunset

### Capital Allocation Rules
- If 1 stream fails Gate 1 (Apr 30): reallocate 20 hrs/week from it to winning streams
- If 2 streams fail Gate 2 (May 31): focus 100% on highest-velocity stream
- If blended revenue <$3K by May 31: emergency cost-cutting + founder intensive work (40+ hrs/week)

### Shutdown Triggers
- **P1:** 0 revenue by May 15 (despite traffic activation)
- **P2:** 0 qualified leads by May 31
- **P3:** 0 traffic by May 31

---

## CONTINGENCY ESCALATION

**If $10K recovery NOT achieved by Jun 30:**
- Month 4-6 extension phase (moderate success scenario)
- OR emergency diversification (add 1 new stream: YouTube automation, print-on-demand, etc.)
- OR full-time founder intensive push (40-50 hrs/week vs. current 15-20 hrs/week assumption)

---

**Decision owner:** CEO
**Review cadence:** Weekly progress against decision gates
**Escalation:** If any stream misses 2-week momentum checkpoint, emergency board meeting required
