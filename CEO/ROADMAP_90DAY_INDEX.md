## Risk Mitigation Summary

### P1 (Digital Products) Risks
- **Niche saturation:** Have 2–3 backup niches ready
- **Conversion low:** A/B test copy/thumbnail by Week 3
- **Traffic insufficient:** Expand seeding (Reddit, email, Twitter) by Week 4

### P2 (Affiliate Marketing) Risks
- **SEO slower:** Normal (3–5 week indexing); monitor GSC
- **Email signups low:** Improve CTA, test lead magnets by Week 3
- **Network rejections:** Apply to 5–6 platforms; fallback ready

### P3 (Print-on-Demand) Risks
- **Design quality poor:** Use Canva templates, benchmark Etsy by Week 2
- **Moderation delays:** Test safe designs first; Teespring fallback
- **Merch rejected:** Focus Redbubble; plan Printful or Teespring by Week 4

### Macro Risks
- **User unavailable:** Hire contractor, pause P2/P3
- **Platform rejections:** Activate fallbacks within 24h
- **Market shift:** Pivot to new niche (1-week sprint, Week 4–5)

- CEO: Review standups, identify decisions needed

### Decision Days (15–60 min)
- CEO: Review gate metrics + scenarios
- CFO: Present metrics snapshot
- CEO: Make decision (GO / CONDITIONAL / NO-GO)
- All: Document decision to `board/decisions/`

### 2-Hour Board Reviews (Every Monday)
- Review all standups from past week
- Escalate blockers
- Approve decisions
- Adjust roadmap if needed
