## CRITICAL FAILURE SCENARIOS COVERED

### P1 (Digital Products on Gumroad)
- Gumroad account banned → Backup: SendOwl (4 hours to switch)
- Wrong niche → Backup: 2 alternative niches ready (1 week to pivot)
- Pricing wrong → Fix: A/B test $14.99 vs $24.99 (1 week test)

### P2 (Affiliate Blog)
- Affiliate networks reject → Backup: 5 alternative networks ready (1 day to activate)
- Email spam issues → Backup: Substack platform (3 days to switch)
- No traffic/list growth → Fix: Improve CTA + lead magnet (1 week)

### P3 (PoD + N8N)
- Redbubble rejects designs → Backup: Printful platform (24 hours to switch)
- Quality collapses → Fix: QA process + design templates (2 weeks)
- N8N workflow breaks → Fallback: Manual CSV import (same day)

### Cross-Stream
- Founder burnout (60+ hrs/week) → Fix: Descope P2 or hire writer (immediate)
- Payment processor suspended → Backup: Square account (2 hours to switch)
- All 3 streams fail → Pivot: New niche (1 week to relaunch)
