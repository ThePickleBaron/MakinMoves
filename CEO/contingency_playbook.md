## SCENARIO 1: P1 GUMROAD UNDERPERFORMS

### Trigger Conditions (Decision Required)

**Option A: Likely (50% probability)**
- Day 30: <$500 revenue
- Symptoms:
  - <50 external visits
  - 0-2 Gumroad sales
  - No email pre-launch audience activated
  - Twitter mentions <5

**Option B: Possible (20% probability)**
- Day 60: <$1.5K cumulative revenue
- Symptoms:
  - <100 external visits Week 1-4
  - <5 total sales by Day 60
  - External traffic declining (not growing)

---

### Decision: Kill P1 External, Reduce to Internal Only

**Rationale:**
- Gumroad organic discovery is <5% of revenue
- Without external traffic, P1 is not viable
- Jason's time better spent on P2 (active sales) or P3 (passive SEO)
- Keep products live (passive revenue continues), stop active marketing

**Implementation (Day 30 or Day 60):**

1. **Pause marketing (48 hours)**
   - [ ] Stop email campaigns to P1
   - [ ] Delete scheduled Twitter posts (P1)
   - [ ] Stop Reddit posting (P1)
   - [ ] Stop Medium publishing (P1)

2. **Maintain products (passive mode)**
   - [ ] Keep 5-10 best templates live on Gumroad
   - [ ] Keep minimal product descriptions updated
   - [ ] Monitor Gumroad messages (respond to organic inquiries)
   - [ ] Expected revenue: $0-200/month (organic only)

3. **Reallocate Jason (40 hours/week)**
   - [ ] 50% to P2 sales (aggressive outbound)
   - [ ] 40% to P3 content (4-5 articles/week)
   - [ ] 10% to board/planning

4. **Update forecast**
   - [ ] Remove P1 from Day 90 recovery estimate
   - [ ] Adjust recovery timeline to Month 8-9 (P2+P3 only)
   - [ ] New target: $8K-12K by Day 90 (from P2+P3)

**Success criteria after pivot:**
- P2 accelerates to 5+ clients by Day 90 (aggressive sales focus)
- P3 hits $1.5K-2K revenue by Day 90 (48-50 articles published)
- Combined (P2+P3) hits $6K-10K by Day 90 (maintains recovery goal)

---

### Alternative: P1 Salvage Plan (If Worth Trying)

**Condition:** If Jason has some evidence of external traffic (email list 100+, Twitter 1K+, Medium followers 200+)

**Actions (Week 2 intensification):**
1. Launch 2x email campaign sequence (existing audience only)
2. Create 5 new templates (tap into feedback from early customers)
3. Expand Twitter posting to daily (3-5 tweets/day about templates)
4. Test 1 niche Reddit community (daily participation)

**Success bar:** Revenue doubles from baseline (e.g., $200→$400)

**If revenue still flat Week 4:** Proceed with kill decision (stop P1 marketing)

## SCENARIO 3: P2 FREELANCE SERVICES STALLS

### Trigger Conditions

**Option A: Likely stall (30% probability)**
- Day 60: <2 clients signed
- Symptoms:
  - <5 qualified prospects in funnel
  - Close rate <10%
  - Sales cycles 12+ weeks (vs. expected 8-10 weeks)
  - Rejection rate >80%

**Option B: Service quality issues (20% probability)**
- Day 60: 3+ clients signed but high churn
- Symptoms:
  - 1+ client cancellation in first 30 days
  - Client satisfaction <7/10
  - Jason reporting >50 hrs/week on client work
  - Quality slipping on deliverables

---

### Decision: Pivot to Premium High-Value Model

**Rationale:**
- Enterprise sales cycles are longer than expected (8-12 weeks baseline)
- Jason can't manage 20 clients solo (burnout + quality risk)
- Pivot to 2-3 high-value clients ($2K-3K/month each) instead of 5-12 medium clients
- Smaller portfolio = higher service quality + sustainability

**Implementation (Day 60 or upon detection):**

1. **Sales repositioning (Week 1)**
   - [ ] Stop spray-and-pray outreach (10+ emails/day)
   - [ ] Focus on 20 warm prospects (warm introductions, referrals, inbound)
   - [ ] Increase proposal value: $1.2K→$2K-3K per client
   - [ ] Reduce sales cycle focus: target 3-6 month contracts (not 1-2 month)

2. **Service model redesign (Week 1-2)**
   - [ ] Create premium tier: "Done-With-You" ($3K/month, 1 on 1 coaching + content)
   - [ ] Create standard tier: "Done-For-You" ($1.5K/month, content only)
   - [ ] Minimum contract: 3 months (not 1-2 months)
   - [ ] Add success metrics to contracts (retention + referrals)

3. **Client acquisition target (Week 2+)**
   - [ ] Goal: 3 premium clients ($2K-3K each) by Day 90 = $6K-9K MRR
   - [ ] Close rate target: 1 out of 5 proposals (20%, vs. current <10%)
   - [ ] Sales effort: 10 high-touch calls/week (vs. 50+ emails/week)

4. **Delivery optimization (Week 2)**
   - [ ] Hire freelance writer ($1.5K-2K/month) to handle delivery
   - [ ] Jason focuses on sales + client strategy
   - [ ] Writer handles content creation + basic editing
   - [ ] Reduce Jason's delivery hours from 40+ to <15/week

**Success criteria after pivot:**
- P2 revenue: $4K-6K MRR by Day 90 (vs. $2K-4K base case)
- Clients: 2-3 premium clients (vs. 5-8 medium clients base case)
- Jason hours: <30/week (vs. 40+ base case)
- Client satisfaction: 8/10+ (vs. 7/10 baseline)
- Profit margin: 50%+ (vs. 40% baseline with writer)

**Worst case:** If <1 client by Day 90, kill P2 entirely, reallocate to P1/P3

## SCENARIO 7: JASON HEALTH EMERGENCY (BURNOUT/ILLNESS)

### Trigger Conditions

**Option A: Burnout warning (48-72 hour decision)**
- Jason reports: >50 hrs/week, missing deadlines, quality slipping
- Physical signs: Exhaustion, illness, stress
- Decision: PAUSE execution, reduce to maintenance mode

**Option B: Acute illness (24-hour decision)**
- Jason unable to work 1+ weeks
- Decision: IMMEDIATE PAUSE, activate maintenance mode

---

### Decision: PAUSE All Streams, Reduce to Maintenance

**Rationale:**
- Jason's health is non-negotiable
- All streams are sustainable passively (products sell, content ranks, affiliate converts)
- Can maintain 60-70% revenue in minimal-effort mode
- 2-4 week pause acceptable (recover, then resume)

**Implementation (24-48 hours):**

1. **Activate maintenance mode (24 hours)**
   - [ ] **P1:** Stop new product creation, keep 5-10 templates live (0 marketing effort)
   - [ ] **P2:** Pause sales outreach, service existing 2-3 clients only (minimal delivery)
   - [ ] **P3:** Pause content creation, affiliate sites continue earning passively (0 effort)

2. **Expected revenue in pause mode (weeks 1-4)**
   - [ ] P1 passive: $0-200 (Gumroad organic)
   - [ ] P2 existing clients: $2K-3K (if 2-3 clients active, maintenance delivery)
   - [ ] P3 passive: $200-500 (affiliate conversion on existing content)
   - [ ] **Total: $2.2K-3.7K/month** (60-70% of run-rate)

3. **Pause protocol (Week 1)**
   - [ ] Notify all active clients: "Temporary pause for health, resuming in X weeks"
   - [ ] Set auto-responder on email (reduce expectations)
   - [ ] Pause all paid subscriptions (save ~$500/month)
   - [ ] Stop hiring/resource escalation

4. **Recovery protocol (Week 3-4)**
   - [ ] Jason assesses readiness to resume
   - [ ] If ready: Gradually resume with P2 (highest ROI) first, then P3, then P1
   - [ ] If not ready: Extend pause 2 more weeks, no guilt

**Sustainability during pause:**
- Can sustain 4-6 weeks in maintenance mode without revenue impact (passive earnings cover basics)
- If pause extends 8+ weeks: May need external support (hire writer for P3, VP sales for P2)

**Resume plan (post-pause):**
- Week 1-2: Resume P2 sales (slow ramp, 5-10 leads/week)
- Week 2-3: Resume P3 content (1-2 articles/week, slow ramp)
- Week 3+: Resume P1 marketing (limited, test market)
- Full execution by Week 4-5

## SCENARIO 8: RECOVERY MISS (Day 90: <$8K)

### Trigger Conditions

**Option A: Conservative miss (25% probability)**
- Day 90: $6K-8K revenue
- Symptoms:
  - All 3 streams executed but below target
  - P1: $2K-3K (traffic weak)
  - P2: $2K-3K (slow sales cycle)
  - P3: $1K-2K (early SEO inflection)

**Option B: Major miss (15% probability)**
- Day 90: <$6K revenue
- Symptoms:
  - 1-2 streams failed (0 revenue)
  - Major pivot or execution error
  - Timeline needs reset to Month 9-10

---

### Decision: Extend Timeline, Cut Expenses, Double Effort

**Rationale:**
- $10K recovery is aggressive (not guaranteed)
- $6K-8K is still progress (justifies continued effort)
- Extend to Month 7-8 (30-60 more days)
- Cut expenses to extend runway
- Double effort on winning streams

**Implementation (Day 90, upon miss):**

1. **Financial reset (24 hours)**
   - [ ] CFO: Trim burn rate by 30-50% ($200-400/month cuts)
   - [ ] Pause all non-essential subscriptions
   - [ ] Reduce paid writer budget (Jason does more content)
   - [ ] Extend cash runway to Month 9-10

2. **Strategic reallocation (48 hours)**
   - [ ] Kill losing stream (0 revenue by Day 90)
   - [ ] Double down on 2 winning streams
   - [ ] Example: If P3=0, kill P3, focus 100% on P1+P2
   - [ ] New goal: $10K by Day 120 (Month 4, not Month 3)

3. **Effort doubling (Day 91+)**
   - [ ] Increase Jason hours to 50-60/week (from 40)
   - [ ] Accelerate marketing/sales on winning streams
   - [ ] Test paid ads ($100-200/week) on winner
   - [ ] Hire freelancer for losing stream (if salvageable)

4. **Communication reset (24 hours)**
   - [ ] CEO writes clear recovery plan: "We hit $6K by Day 90, aiming for $10K by Day 120"
   - [ ] Reframe timeline: "Month 7-8 recovery is normal for these streams"
   - [ ] Celebrate progress: "$6K passive revenue is stable foundation"

**Success criteria for extended timeline:**
- By Day 120 (Month 4): Hit $10K-12K recovery target
- By Day 150 (Month 5): Hit $12K-15K (acceleration beyond recovery)

**Contingency if Day 120 also missed:**
- Fundamental business model error
- Recommend pivot to completely different approach (e.g., agency services, not templates)
- Or seek external capital (investor, loan) to extend runway

## FINAL NOTE
