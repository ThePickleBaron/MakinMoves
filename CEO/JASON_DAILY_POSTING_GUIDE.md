
```
Sunday: Write + schedule 5 posts on Buffer ($15/mo)

Friday evening: Write + schedule Substack + write Monday posts

Total time: ~3.5 hours/week
```

## WHEN YOU GET REPLIES

### Scenario 1: Someone Asks a Question
**Response:**
```
Great question! [Answer it]
```

### Scenario 2: Someone Retweets or Likes
**Response:**
```
Thanks for the share! [Personal comment if relevant]
```

### Scenario 3: Troll or Negative Comment
**Response:**
Option A: Ignore (most likely)
Option B: Reply positively ("I appreciate the feedback, here's my take...")
Option C: Block (if abusive)

**Rule:** Don't engage in arguments. You're building a community, not winning debates.

### Scenario 4: Someone Asks to Collaborate
**Response:**
```
Interesting! Let's chat. DM me and we can connect.
```

## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### ❌ Don't: Post and disappear
✅ Do: Post, then spend 5 min replying to comments

### ❌ Don't: Only talk about your products
✅ Do: 80% education/behind-the-scenes, 20% product

### ❌ Don't: Use salesy language
✅ Do: Use casual, conversational language

### ❌ Don't: Share fake numbers
✅ Do: Share real numbers (even if small)

### ❌ Don't: Post once, expect followers
✅ Do: Post consistently for 4+ weeks, then expect growth

### ❌ Don't: Ignore feedback
✅ Do: Ask for feedback, implement it quickly

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### "No one's engaging with my posts"
**Solution:**
- Make sure you're replying to comments (within 2 hours)
- Post more consistently (daily > 3x/week)
- Use more questions (people reply to questions)
- Engage with others' posts first (follow/retweet/comment on others)

### "Not getting many followers"
**Solution:**
- Week 1-2 is slow. Expected.
- Make sure posts are about your actual journey (not generic advice)
- Share real numbers (people follow authenticity)
- Use hashtags: #BuildInPublic #Bootstrapped #IndieHacker

### "Revenue not correlating to followers"
**Solution:**
- Check your landing pages (is copy good?)
- Check conversion rate (2% is healthy)
- Focus on email (email converts better than followers)
- Ask followers directly: "What would make you buy?"

### "Can't keep up with posting"
**Solution:**
- Pre-write 5 posts on Sunday (batch work)
- Use Buffer ($15/mo) to schedule automatically
- Post 1x/day instead of 3x/day (quality over quantity)
- It's OK to miss a day. Consistency > perfection.
