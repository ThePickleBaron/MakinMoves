## Key Findings (Summary)

### Finding 1: SEO Timeline is 50-75% Too Aggressive
**CEO assumption:** Month 3-4 page 1 rankings
**Industry reality:** 6-12 months for competitive keywords
**Impact:** Month 6 traffic drops from 10,000 to 4,000 visitors

### Finding 2: Conversion Rate Baseline is Optimistic
**CEO assumption:** 2-3% cold organic traffic
**Industry reality:** 1-2% cold organic traffic
**Impact:** 50% fewer affiliate signups at same traffic level

### Finding 3: Commission Risk is Manageable
**Status:** No imminent cuts to our programs (ConvertKit, Beehiiv, Jasper, Monday.com)
**Mitigation:** Diversified across 4-5 programs per niche
**Impact:** 1 platform cut = 5-10% revenue loss, not catastrophic

### Finding 4: Content Quality Requires In-House Effort
**CEO assumption:** P2 freelancers at $150/article can produce 6/week at SEO quality
**Reality:** 3-4 articles/week at publishable quality is reasonable; 6/week requires founder time
**Impact:** Founder/COO must commit 6-10 hours/week OR accept slower timeline
