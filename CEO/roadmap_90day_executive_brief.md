## Risk Mitigation

### P1 Risks & Mitigations
**Risk 1:** Niche saturated (low conversion <0.1%)
- **Mitigation:** Have 2–3 alternative niches ready. If <1 sale by Week 3, pivot by Week 5.

**Risk 2:** Price wrong ($24.99 too high)
- **Mitigation:** A/B test pricing ($14.99 vs $24.99) by Week 6. Monitor ATC rate.

**Risk 3:** Traffic insufficient (<50 views/week)
- **Mitigation:** Expand seeding to Reddit communities, email list, Twitter. Scale by Week 4.

### P2 Risks & Mitigations
**Risk 1:** SEO slower than expected (0 impressions by Week 3)
- **Mitigation:** Normal (GSC needs 3–5 days to index). Confirm GSC setup, check keywords. Don't panic.

**Risk 2:** Email signup rate <1% (list not growing)
- **Mitigation:** Improve CTA, test lead magnets (free checklist, template), add popup. By Week 3.

**Risk 3:** Affiliate networks reject (no revenue stream)
- **Mitigation:** Apply to 5–6 networks (Amazon, ShareASale, CJ, Mediavine, Impact). Fallback ready.

### P3 Risks & Mitigations
**Risk 1:** Design quality poor (0–1 clicks)
- **Mitigation:** Use Canva templates as baseline, benchmark against Etsy bestsellers. Test new niche Week 3.

**Risk 2:** Redbubble moderation delays (designs not live)
- **Mitigation:** Prepare 5 safe designs first (no copyright/brand issues). Have Teespring fallback.

**Risk 3:** Merch by Amazon rejected (no second platform)
- **Mitigation:** Focus Redbubble initially. Plan Printful or Teespring fallback. Apply to multiple platforms.
