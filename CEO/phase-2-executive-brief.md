## THE BET
Move from **launch** to **growth through specialization**. Rather than 3 equal streams, identify early winners by Day 21 and pour 50%+ of effort into whatever is working. Expected outcome: **$200–$600 cumulative revenue by Day 60**, establishing repeatable patterns for Phase 3 maintenance.

---

## THREE KEY DECISIONS YOU'LL MAKE

### 1. Which Stream Gets Extra Attention? (Decision Date: Day 24)
**If Digital Products >$5 cumulative by Day 21:** Accelerate (create 2–3 more templates)
**If PoD >$10 cumulative by Day 21:** Focus on volume (create 20+ designs)
**If both >$5:** Split focus 50/50; let Affiliate run on autopilot

**If all <$5 combined:** Execute emergency niche pivot (3-day test of new niche)

### 2. Should We Launch SaaS in Phase 2? (Decision Date: Day 35)
**Gate:** Digital Products must hit $10/week revenue first
**If YES (revenue $10+/week):** Allocate 5 hours for market research; no build yet
**If NO:** Defer to Phase 3; double down on profitable streams instead

### 3. Is Phase 2 Working? (Decision Date: Day 60)
**Success threshold:** $200+ cumulative, 2+ streams with revenue, 200+ email subscribers
**If YES:** Approve Phase 3 transition (15-min/week maintenance mode)
**If NO ($100–$200 range):** Extend Phase 2 4 weeks; increase marketing spend $50
**If FAILED ($0 revenue):** Reset strategy; investigate Phase 1 assumptions
