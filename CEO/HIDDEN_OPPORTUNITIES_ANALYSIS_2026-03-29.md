### ❌ REJECT (Distraction from Core Mission)

#### **OPPORTUNITY #2: Template Reselling (Dropshipping)**

**Opportunity:** Resell other people's Notion templates, Zapier workflows, GPT prompts at 20–30% markup.

**Model:**
- Find 10–20 templates on Gumroad, Creative Fabrica, or Etsy
- Resell on your own Gumroad at 20–30% markup
- Example: Buy template for $15 → Resell for $20 = $5 margin per sale

**Why it scores LOW:**

1. **Margin is terrible:** 20–30% on low-cost products = $2–5 per sale minimum
   - Need 100+ sales/month to make $200–500
   - That's 3–4 sales/day: requires 10–15 hours/week marketing
   - Competes directly with P1 (own products, 100% margin)

2. **White-label risk (CFO found critical issue):**
   - CFO's agent research (March 29 22:35): White-label templates trigger Gumroad shadowban (silent algorithm throttling)
   - Conversion rates 4x lower than assumed (0.5–2%, not 2–5%)
   - 80% of resold digital products fail
   - **You just pivoted P1 AWAY from white-label** (Creator Toolkit instead) because of this risk

3. **Brand dilution:**
   - Selling other people's mediocre templates kills your authority
   - Customers compare your resold templates to your Creator Toolkit
   - Negative: "Why is he selling someone else's template when he has his own?"

4. **Time cost is hidden:** Management burden
   - Customer support on products you don't understand
   - Refunds/chargebacks on products you don't control quality of
   - Template updates/changes from original creator

**Decision:** ❌ **REJECT** (conflicts with P1 pivot, low margin, execution burden, white-label risk)

#### **OPPORTUNITY #7: Job Board/Marketplace**

**Opportunity:** Launch marketplace connecting freelancer audience with gig jobs at 10–20% commission.

**Model:**
- Platform: Airtable form (free) or custom ($2K+ to build)
- Jobs: Collect from freelancer forums, subreddits, newsletters
- Commission: 10–20% per completed job
- Target: 50–100 jobs posted/month by Month 6

**Why it scores LOW:**

1. **Network effect required, not available yet:**
   - Marketplaces need both supply (freelancers) and demand (job posters)
   - You have neither on Day 1
   - Chicken-egg: No jobs → freelancers leave, no freelancers → posters won't post
   - **Realistic launch:** Month 4+ (after you have 1K+ audience)

2. **Low commission creates revenue drag:**
   - Marketplace revenue from $2K job: $200–400 commission (after payment processing)
   - Your email list value: 1 customer = $20–50 LTV (P1/P2/P3)
   - **Better use of your audience:** Sell them your products (P1/P2/P3), not host marketplace

3. **Moderation and legal:**
   - Job disputes, payment issues, chargebacks
   - Liability: Are you responsible if freelancer doesn't deliver?
   - Legal: Do you need terms of service, escrow accounts, insurance?

4. **Platform risk:**
   - Building on Airtable is limiting (can't brand or scale)
   - Building custom ($2K+) competes with CTMO infrastructure budget
   - Free alternatives (Taskrabbit, Fiverr, Upwork) already dominate

**Decision:** ❌ **REJECT** (network effect not available, low revenue vs. P1/P2/P3, legal/operations risk). Save this for Year 2 if you have $100K+ revenue and want to build ecosystem.
