## Model Overview

### Trinity Model (Current/Baseline)
- **P1:** Digital Products (Gumroad) | Launch: March 31
- **P2:** Print-on-Demand (Redbubble) | Launch: March 31
- **P3:** Affiliate Marketing | Launch: April 1
- **Decision:** No P2 consulting test; P1 + P2 (PoD) + P3 parallel execution

### Hybrid Model (Proposed Alternative)
- **P1:** Digital Products (Gumroad) | Launch: March 31
- **P2:** Consulting Test (May 1–15) → Success: Continue consulting | Fail: Pivot to Freelance Writing
- **P3:** Affiliate Marketing | Launch: April 1
- **Decision:** Test consulting viability. Fallback to freelance if test fails.

**Key Difference:** Trinity replaces P2 with PoD (passive, low-margin). Hybrid tests consulting (active, high-margin) with fallback to freelance.

## Risk-Return Analysis

### Trinity Model (P1+PoD+P3)
**Upside:**
- ✅ Low execution risk (all 3 streams proven, no new test)
- ✅ Balanced portfolio (no single dominates)
- ✅ Passive PoD provides steady baseline
- ✅ No decision gate complexity (set it and forget it)

**Downside:**
- ❌ Lower revenue ceiling ($12K–$20K by Aug 31)
- ❌ PoD grows slowly (passive, not high-margin)
- ❌ Requires strong product design (limited by design ability)
- ❌ Recovery timeline July 25–Aug 10 (tighter cash runway)

**Profile:** Conservative, low-risk, baseline recovery path

---

### Hybrid Model (P1+P2 Consulting+P3)
**Upside:**
- ✅ High revenue ceiling if consulting succeeds ($42K–$45K by Aug 31)
- ✅ Consulting has 60–70% margins (vs. PoD 40%)
- ✅ Accelerates recovery by 2 weeks (June 15 vs. July 25)
- ✅ Scaling potential in Phase 2 ($15K–$50K/mo with consulting ramp)

**Downside:**
- ❌ Consulting test is execution risk (60% failure rate)
- ❌ If consulting fails, falls back to freelance (no upside, same as baseline)
- ❌ May 15 decision gate adds complexity
- ❌ May 1–15 consulting test = 20 hours of effort (CEO/COO time allocation)

**Profile:** Higher risk/reward. Optionality-based. Accelerates recovery if successful.

## Board Recommendation

### HYBRID MODEL PREFERRED

**Rationale:**

1. **Negligible Downside:** Consulting failure = no revenue loss. Fallback to freelance preserves July 25 timeline.

2. **Substantial Upside:** Consulting success = +$25K–$32K revenue by Aug 31 (2.5x Trinity baseline).

3. **Accelerated Recovery:** If consulting wins, $10K milestone hits by June 15 (vs. July 25 Trinity). Matters for cash runway.

4. **Phase 2 Optionality:** Consulting creates scaling path to $50K+/mo in Phase 2 (PoD capped at $1K–$2K/mo).

5. **Risk is Manageable:** May 15 decision gate + freelance fallback = execution risk is contained and well-understood.

### Implementation Approach

**Step 1: Approve Hybrid Model** (CEO sign-off)

**Step 2: Run Trinity Baseline in Parallel (Low Effort)**
- Prepare PoD designs as contingency
- If P2 consulting fails, PoD becomes fallback to freelance (optional upside)

**Step 3: Execute Consulting Test (May 1–15)**
- 20 hours CEO/COO warm outreach
- 3–4 discovery calls
- Target: ≥1 deal closed or ≥3 qualified leads

**Step 4: Decision Gate (May 15)**
- ✅ Consult success: Continue ramp (May 16+)
- ❌ Consult fail: Activate freelance (May 16+, zero setup cost)

**Step 5: Monitor Milestones** (Every 2 weeks via decision gate tracker)
