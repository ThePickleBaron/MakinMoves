## Consulting Test Details (May 1–15)

**Objective:** Validate $3K–$5K/mo consulting pipeline before committing to full service

**Test Approach:**
- 2-week sprint (May 1–15)
- Warm outreach to 20–30 warm network contacts
- 3–4 discovery calls
- Target: 1–2 paying customers ($2K–$5K each)

**Success Criteria:**
- ≥1 deal closed: CONTINUE consulting (proceed to May 16 ramp)
- ≥3 qualified leads: CONTINUE (high probability of May 16 closes)
- 0 deals, <3 leads: PIVOT to freelance writing (May 16+)

**Time Investment:** 15–20 hours (CEO/COO focused outreach + sales calls)

**Fallback Cost:** Zero. Freelance writing is baseline model already planned (zero re-setup cost).
