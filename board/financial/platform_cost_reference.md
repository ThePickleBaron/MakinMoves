## 1. GUMROAD

**Overview:** Creator-to-fan distribution platform. Best for: ebooks, templates, courses, digital products.

### Costs
| Item | Amount | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Account creation | $0 | Free forever |
| Monthly subscription | $0 | Free tier sufficient |
| Product listing | $0 | Unlimited products |
| Hosting | $0 | All files hosted by Gumroad |

### Fees
| Transaction Type | Fee | Notes |
|-----------------|-----|-------|
| Direct sales (your link) | 10% + $0.50 | Lowest fee tier |
| Discover marketplace | 30% | If you list in Gumroad's shop |
| Payment processor | 2.9% + $0.30 | Stripe/PayPal (included in above) |
| Refunds | No refund of platform fee | If refunded, you keep the 10% loss |

### Example: $20 Ebook Sale
```
Gross revenue:           $20.00
Gumroad fee (10%):       -$2.00
Processing fee (2.9%):   -$0.58
NET TO YOU:              $17.42 (87% margin)
```

### Payout
- Weekly payouts (Fridays)
- Stripe direct deposit (US) = free
- PayPal (international) = 2-3% fee

**Source:** [Gumroad Pricing](https://gumroad.com/pricing)

## 2. ETSY

**Overview:** Marketplace for handmade, vintage, and craft items. Best for: digital art, printables, templates, designs.

### Costs
| Item | Amount | Frequency | Notes |
|------|--------|-----------|-------|
| Shop setup | $15-20 (one-time) | One-time | May vary by location |
| Listing fee | $0.20 | Per listing | Listings expire after 4 months |
| Listing renewal | $0.20 per renewal | Monthly (as needed) | Must renew to keep listed |

### Fees (On Each Sale)
| Fee Type | Amount | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Transaction fee | 6.5% of order total | Includes item + shipping + gift wrap |
| Payment processing | $0.20 per transaction | Built-in (Etsy Payments only) |
| Shipping label | $0 (if digital) | N/A for digital products |

### Example: $25 Digital Product Sale
```
Gross revenue:           $25.00
Etsy transaction (6.5%): -$1.625
Payment fee:             -$0.20
NET TO YOU:              $23.175 (93% margin for digital products)
```

### 250-Listing Startup Cost
```
Shop setup:              $20
Listings (250 × $0.20):  $50
Total initial investment: $70

Cost per listing:        $0.28 ($20 setup / 250 + $0.20 listing)
Break-even:             ~3-5 sales to cover costs
```

### Payout
- Direct deposit (2-3 business days)
- Weekly payouts available

**Source:** [Etsy Fees & Pricing](https://www.etsy.com/legal/fees/)

## 3. REDBUBBLE

**Overview:** Print-on-demand marketplace. Best for: t-shirts, hoodies, mugs, stickers, artwork.

### Costs
| Item | Amount | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Account creation | $0 | Free |
| Product upload | $0 | Unlimited designs |
| Store hosting | $0 | Included |
| Monthly fees | $0 | Free forever |

### Fee Structure (2026 Update)
| Account Tier | Condition | Account Fee | Revenue Share |
|-------------|-----------|------------|---------------|
| **Standard** | < 5 sales/month | None initially | 20-40% (varies by product) |
| **Premium** | Established artists | Up to $150/month cap | 40-60% (higher margin) |
| **Pro** | Top sellers + Ambassadors | $0 (exempt) | 60-80% (best rates) |

**Key:** Only pay account fees AFTER first sale. No upfront cost.

### Example: $20 T-Shirt Sale
```
Sale price:              $20.00
Redbubble fee (50%):     -$10.00
NET TO YOU:              $10.00 (50% margin - Standard tier)

Sale price:              $20.00
Redbubble fee (30%):     -$6.00
NET TO YOU:              $14.00 (70% margin - Pro tier)
```

### Payout
- Monthly direct deposits
- No minimum threshold (pay from $1)
- Processing time: 1-3 business days

**Note:** Redbubble uploads products to their print partners (Printful, Merch by Amazon, etc.). You never handle inventory.

**Source:** [Redbubble Account Fees & Earnings](https://help.redbubble.com/hc/en-us/articles/4412593541908-What-are-Redbubble-s-account-fees)

## 4. MEDIUM (Medium Partner Program)

**Overview:** Publishing and content monetization platform. Best for: blog posts, articles, thought leadership.

### Costs
| Item | Amount | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Account creation | $0 | Free |
| Publishing articles | $0 | Unlimited |
| Platform fee | $0 | Free |
| Monthly subscription | Optional | $5/month to join Member program (you don't need to pay) |

### Revenue Share
| Source | Revenue Split | Notes |
|--------|---------------|-------|
| Member reads | 50% to you | Split with other writers based on reading time |
| External traffic bonus | +5% boost | Reads from social media, newsletters, etc. get +5% |
| CPM equivalent | $2-5 per 1000 reads | Typical range for Medium |

### Example: 1,000 Member Reads
```
Medium member revenue pool: Varies (depends on total member spending)
Typical:                   $5-15 per 1,000 reads
Your share (50%):          $2.50-7.50 per 1,000 reads
Bonus (5% external):       +$0.125-0.375 if from external link
TOTAL:                     $2.625-7.875 (conservative: $3-5)
```

### Payout
- Monthly direct deposit (via Stripe)
- Minimum: No minimum
- Processing: 1-3 business days

**Key Insight:** Medium is the slowest path to revenue (30-60 days for first payment) but has longest tail. One article can earn for years.

**Source:** [Medium Partner Program](https://medium.com/partner-program)

## 5. WORDPRESS + DOMAIN + HOSTING

**Overview:** Self-hosted blog/site for content and affiliate marketing.

### Domain Cost
| Item | Cost | Frequency | Typical Price |
|------|------|-----------|---------------|
| .com domain | $10-15 | Annual | Registrars: Namecheap, GoDaddy |

## 6. STRIPE (Payment Processing)

**Overview:** Payment processor. Works with most platforms automatically. You need it for: Direct checkout, subscription payments.

### Costs
| Item | Amount | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Account setup | $0 | Free, instant |
| Monthly fee | $0 | No monthly subscription |
| Transaction fee | 2.9% + $0.30 | Per successful payment |
| Refunds | Included in fee | You eat the fee even if refunded |
| Chargebacks | $15 | Per disputed charge (win or lose) |

### Additional Fees
| Fee Type | Amount | When |
|----------|--------|------|
| International card | +1.5% | Customer's card issued outside US |
| Currency conversion | +1% | Multi-currency transactions |
| ACH bank transfer | $1 | Per ACH withdrawal |
| Wire transfer | $0 (direct deposit) | Standard payout method |

### Example: $50 Sale
```
Customer pays:           $50.00
Stripe fee (2.9%):       -$1.45
Processing fee ($0.30):  -$0.30
NET TO YOU:              $48.25 (96.5% margin)
```

### Payout
- Daily or weekly automatic deposits
- Free bank transfers (US)
- 1-2 business day processing

**Note:** Gumroad and most platforms handle Stripe integration. You don't set up Stripe separately unless building custom checkout.

**Source:** [Stripe Pricing](https://stripe.com/pricing)
