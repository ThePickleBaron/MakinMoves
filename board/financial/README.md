## Decision Triggers (When to Act)

### Scale Up (Spend Reserve)
- When: Monthly revenue > $500
- Action: Invest $200-300 in paid traffic testing
- Expected ROI: 3:1+ (spend $100 to earn $300+)

### Upgrade Hosting
- When: Free hosting becoming slow/unstable OR traffic > 10,000/month
- Action: Upgrade WordPress to paid tier ($5-50/month)
- Cost: 1-3% of projected revenue

### Upgrade n8n Automation
- When: Workflows exceed 200 executions/month (free tier limit)
- Action: Self-host on Railway (free) OR upgrade to n8n Cloud ($25/month)
- Cost: 3-5% of projected revenue

### Pivot Platform
- When: Any platform producing <$50/month by Day 60
- Action: Replace with competitor platform (usually free switch)
- Cost: 0-5 hours setup time

### Investigate/Optimize
- When: Revenue 50% below target on Day 21
- Action: Diagnose slowdown (product quality? price? traffic?)
- Decision: Increase content output 2-3x or reduce price 10-15%

## Common Questions

**Q: Do all 5 platforms need to launch on Day 1?**
A: Ideal yes (spreads risk), but not required. Can launch Gumroad + Etsy first, add others in Days 2-5.

**Q: What if we want to launch with just 2 platforms?**
A: Faster launch, but riskier. If one platform underperforms, single point of failure. Recommend all 5 within 1 week.

**Q: Can we skip expensive platforms (like Etsy)?**
A: Yes, but it costs revenue. Etsy has different audience. Recommend: start free (Gumroad, Redbubble, Medium), add Etsy when cash-positive.

**Q: Should we run paid ads immediately?**
A: No. Wait until Day 30+ when organic revenue validates product-market fit. Then run paid ads (budget $100-200) and measure ROI.

**Q: What if we need to refund a customer?**
A: Platform fees are not refunded. If $20 sale refunded, you lose $20 gross but still pay $2 fee. This is built into margins.

**Q: How do we handle taxes on platform payments?**
A: All platforms provide 1099 documentation. At current volume (<$1,000/month), likely not required to file, but track carefully. Consult accountant if revenue exceeds $600/month.

**Q: Can we change pricing mid-month?**
A: Yes, all platforms allow price changes. Recommend testing: 2 price points for 1 week each, then commit to winner.

**Q: What's the difference between PoD (Print-on-Demand) and digital products?**
A: PoD (Redbubble, Merch by Amazon): Customer pays, platform manufactures. You earn 20-50% margin. Digital (Gumroad, Etsy): Instant delivery, no manufacturing cost, you earn 87-93% margin. Digital has better margins but PoD has wider audience.
