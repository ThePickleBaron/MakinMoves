## Contingency Playbook

### If P1 Digital Underperforms (<5 sales by April 30)
- Investigate: Product quality, market demand, Gumroad discoverability
- Pivot: Reduce P1 focus, accelerate P3 or test P2 consulting earlier
- Timeline impact: 2–3 week delay if not mitigated

### If P3 Affiliate Stalls (<4 keywords by May 31)
- Investigate: Content quality, SEO strategy, competitive landscape
- Activate: Option A (paid traffic), Option B (add niche #4), Option C (pivot resources to P1)
- Timeline impact: 1–2 week delay if activated by June 15

### If P2 Consulting Gets 0 Sales by May 10
- Decision: Kill test immediately (don't wait for May 15)
- Activate: Freelance fallback (May 16)
- Timeline impact: No impact (same as baseline)

### If Cumulative Revenue <$1.5K by May 31
- Escalate: CEO review needed
- Investigate: Are all 3 streams executing?
- Contingency: Shift resources to fastest-growing stream, reduce support overhead

## Assumptions & Risk Factors

### Key Assumptions
1. **P1 Digital:** Notion templates $30–35/sale, 2–15 sales/month by April–August
2. **P2 Consulting:** $3K–$5K deals, 2–4 clients ramping by June–August
3. **P2 Freelance (Fallback):** $1.5K–$2.5K/month stable baseline (proven)
4. **P3 Affiliate:** 8–12 keywords ranking by June, 1K+ organic visitors/mo, 2–4% conversion rate
5. **Fixed Costs:** $41/mo (hosting, email, domain)
6. **Commission Rates:** 10% Gumroad, 20–30% affiliate, 0–2% payment processing

### Major Risk Factors
| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| P1 sells <5 units by April 30 | 15% | Have PoD designs ready as backup |
| P2 consulting gets 0 sales by May 10 | 30% | Kill test early, activate freelance immediately |
