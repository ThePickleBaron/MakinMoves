## Quick Calculator: How Many Sales to Break Even?

### By Platform (Individual)

**Gumroad: Average Product Price $15**
```
Fee: 10% + $0.50 per sale
Net per sale: $15.00 - $1.50 - $0.50 = $13.00

To break even $82 setup cost:
Break-even sales: 82 / 13 = 6.3 sales
TIMELINE: 1-2 weeks (if 3-4 sales/week)

To hit $500/month revenue:
Target sales: 500 / 13 = 38.5 sales/month
Timeline: 2-3 weeks at consistent 10/day
```

**Etsy: Average Product Price $20 (digital)**
```
Fee: 6.5% + $0.20 per sale
Setup cost: $20 (shop) + $50 (listings) = $70

Net per sale: $20.00 - $1.30 - $0.20 = $18.50

To break even $70 setup:
Break-even sales: 70 / 18.50 = 3.8 sales
TIMELINE: 3-5 days (if 1 sale/day)

To hit $500/month revenue:
Target sales: 500 / 18.50 = 27 sales/month
Timeline: 1 month at consistent 1 sale/day
```

**Redbubble: Average Print Sale $25 (Standard tier, 50% commission)**
```
Fee: 50% (Standard tier)
Net per sale: $25.00 × 0.50 = $12.50
Setup cost: $0

To break even (any revenue is profit):
No setup cost to recover
Timeline: First sale = profit

To hit $500/month revenue:
Target sales: 500 / 12.50 = 40 sales/month
Timeline: 1-2 sales/day (achievable with 5-10 designs)
```

**Medium: Typical Article Revenue $3-5/month**
```
Monthly reading payout: Varies, but typical = $3-5/month per article

To break even $0 setup:
No cost to recover
Timeline: First article published

To hit $100/month revenue (from Medium alone):
Need: 20-30 articles earning $4-5/month each
Timeline: 2-3 months publishing 1-2 articles/week

REALITY: Medium is slow path but has longest tail earnings
```

**WordPress + Affiliate: Average Affiliate Commission $2-5/sale**
```
Setup cost: $12 (domain)
Fee: 20-30% commission (varies by affiliate program)

Example: Promoting $50 product at 25% commission
Net per affiliate sale: $50 × 0.25 = $12.50

To break even $12 setup:
Break-even sales: 12 / 12.50 = 0.96 sales
TIMELINE: 1 affiliate sale = break even

To hit $500/month revenue:
Target sales: 500 / 12.50 = 40 affiliate clicks converting
Timeline: 40 conversions = 400-500 clicks (8-10% conversion rate)
At 100 visitors/month: 3-5 months to build traffic
```

## Appendix: Fee Formula Reference

**For any platform, calculate NET per sale:**

```
NET = (Price - Platform Fee - Processing Fee)

Examples:

Gumroad ($20 product):
NET = 20 - (20 × 0.10) - 0.50
NET = 20 - 2 - 0.50 = $17.50

Etsy ($25 product):
NET = 25 - (25 × 0.065) - 0.20
NET = 25 - 1.625 - 0.20 = $23.175

Redbubble ($30 print):
NET = 30 - (30 × 0.50)
NET = 30 - 15 = $15.00

Affiliate ($100 product at 25% commission):
NET = 100 × 0.25
NET = $25.00
```
