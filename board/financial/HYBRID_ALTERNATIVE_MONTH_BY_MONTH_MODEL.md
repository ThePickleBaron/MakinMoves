## Decision Gates & Success Criteria

### Gate 1: P2 Consulting Test Launch (May 1)
**Precondition:** P1 + P3 both validated in April (both trending positive)
**Test Duration:** May 1–15 (2 weeks)

**Success Criteria:**
- [ ] 2+ qualified leads by May 10
- [ ] 1+ closed deal by May 15 (even if $1.5K–$2K pilot)
- [ ] Customer feedback positive (NPS > 7, clear use case validation)

**Decision Rule:**
- **≥2 closed deals by May 15:** CONTINUE consulting (ramp to 3–4 clients by June)
- **1 deal closed + strong pipeline:** CONTINUE (consulting + freelance hybrid May 16–30)
- **0 deals closed OR <2 leads:** PIVOT to freelance writing (proven baseline)

**Fallback Trigger:** If 0 leads by May 10, stop consulting test immediately (by May 12), pivot to freelance to reclaim lost time in May.

---

### Gate 2: P3 SEO Milestone (June 1)
**Metric:** 8+ keywords ranking Page 1 Google, 1K+ organic visitors/mo across niches

**Success Criteria:**
- [ ] Email niche: 3+ keywords ranking, 500+ visitors/mo
- [ ] Freelance tools niche: 2+ keywords ranking, 300+ visitors/mo
- [ ] AI writing niche: 3+ keywords ranking, 700+ visitors/mo
- [ ] Total affiliate revenue: $600–1K/mo

**Decision Rule:**
- **On track (8+ keywords, 1K+ visitors):** PROCEED to Phase 2 (add niche #4, expand content)
- **Lagging (4–6 keywords, 500 visitors):** CONTINUE but adjust content strategy
- **Failed (<4 keywords, <300 visitors):** REVIEW SEO execution, consider paid traffic acceleration (Google Ads)

**Fallback Trigger:** If <300 organic visitors/mo by June 1, reallocate resources (speed up P1 or P2 instead).
