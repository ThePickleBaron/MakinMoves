2. No timeline impact (freelance timing same as consulting)

4. Recovery delayed by ~2 weeks (July 15 vs June 10)

**Cost:** $0 (both free/minimal overhead)

### Risk 2: Revenue 50% Below Target (Probability: 15%)

**Trigger:** By June 1, cumulative revenue < $800

**Mitigation:**
1. Increase content generation (2x daily)
2. Allocate $50/week to paid traffic (P3)
3. Reduce scope, focus on 1 winning platform
4. Escalate to CEO for strategy review

**Cost:** +$200 (paid ads) → reduces cash position by $200 by August
**Timeline Impact:** Recovery delayed 2-4 weeks

---

### Risk 3: Hosting Outage (Probability: 5%)

**Trigger:** Railway down for >4 hours

**Mitigation:**
1. Automatic n8n failover to local Docker (CTMO manual restart)
2. Database backups on GitHub (every 6 hours, automatic)
3. Max recovery time: 1-2 hours
4. Revenue impact: Negligible (async platforms don't require uptime)

**Cost:** $0 (covered by setup)

---

### Risk 4: n8n Automation Broken (Probability: 8%)

**Trigger:** n8n workflow failures (publishing stopped, revenue not captured)

**Mitigation:**
1. n8n error monitoring (continuous checks)
2. Auto-alert to CTMO if workflow down >6 hours
3. Manual publishing fallback (COO publishes directly)
4. Stripe webhook redundancy (manual reconciliation daily)

**Cost:** $0 (CTMO time to fix)
**Timeline Impact:** Max 1 day (manual publishing backup)
