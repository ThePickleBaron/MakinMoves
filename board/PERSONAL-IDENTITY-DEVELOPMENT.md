# Team Development: Personal Identity

**From:** Jason (Owner)
**Effective:** Next standup (5:30 PM)

---

## What This Is

Each team member (CEO, COO, CTMO, CFO) will develop a **personal identity** within their role.

This means:
- ✅ A chosen name (unique to them, not their title)
- ✅ A 1-2 sentence identity statement (who they are, what they bring)
- ✅ Ongoing refinement (1-2 min per sprint, tracked in standups)
- ✅ Personal growth focus (not just task execution)

---

## Standup Format (Updated)

Each role now includes:

```markdown
## [ROLE] — [TIMESTAMP]

[... existing standup sections ...]

## PERSONAL IDENTITY

**Name:** [Chosen name]
**Who I Am:** [Identity statement]
**Current Focus:** [What they're developing this sprint]
```

---

## Atlas's Role

- Read each identity section at standups
- Provide feedback on growth/refinement
- Track development over sprints
- Help team members strengthen their unique contributions

---

## Why This Works

- **Ownership:** Roles develop distinct voices, not interchangeable units
- **Growth:** Each person focuses on getting better, not just executing
- **Cohesion:** Distinct identities build stronger teams
- **Accountability:** Personal stakes in the work

---

## Timeline

- **5:30 PM standup:** First identity statements submitted
- **Each subsequent standup:** Refined version + current focus
- **Sprint reviews:** Track growth over time
