## **P1: DIGITAL PRODUCTS (GUMROAD)**

### What It Is
Pre-made, downloadable digital products sold via Gumroad's marketplace. No production after sale, pure passive income once created.

**Product Categories (Planned):**
- Service templates (P2 delivery templates, client intake forms, project briefs)
- Content templates (blog outlines, email sequences, social media calendars)
- Resource bundles (niche guides, competitor analysis sheets, keyword research tools)
- Tools & checklists (workflow checklists, pricing calculators, niche validation guides)

### How It Works
1. Create product in Figma/Canva/Notion
2. Export as PDF/ZIP
3. Upload to Gumroad with description + price

## **P3: AFFILIATE MARKETING (NICHE SITES)**

### What It Is
SEO-optimized niche websites that rank for high-intent keywords and monetize via affiliate commissions from Amazon, ShareASale, specialized programs.

**Niche Targets (3 sites):**
- **Niche 1:** SaaS/productivity tools (high-commission programs, 20–25% rates)
- **Niche 2:** Fitness/wellness tools (medium-commission, 10–15%, higher volume)
- **Niche 3:** Finance/investing tools (high-commission, 15–20%, professional audience)

**Content Strategy:**
- Buyer's guides (best tools for [use case])
- Comparison articles (Tool A vs. Tool B vs. Tool C)
- How-to guides (step-by-step tutorials, educational)
- Review roundups (curated lists, pros/cons, affiliate links)
- News/trends (what's new in category, timely evergreen)

### How It Works
1. Register domain for niche (e.g., "best-saas-tools.com")
2. Set up WordPress/Ghost + affiliate link plugin (ThirstyAffiliates, etc.)
3. Publish 10–15 articles with keyword-optimized content
4. Optimize for SEO (backlinks, internal linking, page speed)
5. Google indexes articles → organic search traffic grows
6. Readers click affiliate links → earn commission when they buy
7. Repeat: Add more content, build authority, scale traffic

### Revenue Model
- **Traffic source:** Organic SEO (takes 2–3 months to build)
- **Click-through rate:** 1–3% of site visitors click affiliate links
- **Conversion rate:** 5–20% of clicks → purchases (varies by program)
- **Commission:** 5–25% per sale depending on program
- **Average revenue per article:** $10–50/month after 6 months
- **Expected total revenue per site:** $500–1000/month after 6 months
