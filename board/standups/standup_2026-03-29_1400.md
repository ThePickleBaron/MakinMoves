### Critical Blockers

**BLOCKER #1: Zapier Free Tier**
- Published: 100 tasks/month free
- Reality: Exhausts in 1-2 weeks with real workflows
- Our need: 10-15 workflows × 2-3 steps = 50-180 tasks/week
- Decision needed: Upgrade to Zapier Professional ($29.99/mo) OR switch to n8n (free)
- **Recommendation:** n8n (self-hosted in Docker). Saves $360/year, aligns with containerized stack.

**BLOCKER #2: Bluehost Shared Hosting Throttle**
- Published: Unlimited traffic
- Reality: Throttles at 25k-50k combined monthly visitors (25 simultaneous processes limit)

- Decision needed: VPS upgrade trigger point
- **Recommendation:** Upgrade at 40k/mo (balances cost savings vs. performance risk)
- **Timeline:** Month 4 (after shared hosting has proven cost savings M1-3)
- **Cost:** +$30-50/mo
