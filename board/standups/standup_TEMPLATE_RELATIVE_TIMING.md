# STANDUP — Relative Timing Model — TEMPLATE

**Model:** Async Offset (Relative timing based on CEO filing, not wall clocks)
**Cycle:** CEO files → CTMO window 2 min later → CFO window 17 min later → COO window 32 min later

---

## HOW TO CALCULATE YOUR WINDOW

1. **Read the standup file** at your scheduled gap time
2. **Find the most recent timestamp comment** from your predecessor
3. **Add 2 minutes** to that timestamp (that's when your window starts)
4. **Add 13 minutes** to that (that's when your window ends)

**Example:**
```
File shows: **7:29 PM CTMO SECTION UPDATED — Nexus**

CFO's window calculation:
  7:29 PM + 2 min = 7:31 PM (window start)
  7:31 PM + 13 min = 7:44 PM (window end)

CFO window: 7:31–7:44 PM
```
