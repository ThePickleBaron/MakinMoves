## DECISION SUMMARY

## APPROVED DECISION GATES

### GATE 1: Apr 30 (Month 1 Success Validation)
**Criteria:**
- ✅ **P1 Gumroad:** Revenue ≥ $500 AND external traffic > 200 visitors
- ✅ **P2 Freelance Writing:** 3+ qualified leads in pipeline AND first customer interested
- ✅ **P3 Affiliate Sites:** All 3 sites indexed in Google AND >50 organic visitors cumulative

**If criteria NOT met:**
- P1 misses: Pivot traffic strategy or sunset (enter Contingency Scenario 1)
- P2 misses: Pivot offer positioning or shutdown (enter Contingency Scenario 2)
- P3 misses: Reallocate content effort to P1/P2 (enter Contingency Scenario 3)

**Decision authority:** CEO (in consultation with COO/CFO)

---

### GATE 2: May 31 (Month 2 Profitability Validation)
**Criteria:**
- ✅ **Blended revenue target:** $3K+ cumulative (May month-end)
- ✅ **P1 health:** MRR sustainable ≥ $500 (run rate maintained)
- ✅ **P2 health:** 2+ active customers, writer backup hired/trained
- ✅ **P3 health:** Traffic >1K organic/week across sites

**If criteria NOT met:**
- Blended revenue <$3K: Emergency pivot — reallocate capital/time to 1 stream (Contingency Scenario 4)
- P1 health poor: Sunset or transition to seasonal model
- P2 health poor: Reduce scope to 1-writer max capacity or shutdown
- P3 health poor: Reallocate to content marketing for P1/P2

**Decision authority:** CEO + CFO (requires financial validation)

---

### GATE 3: Jun 30 (Recovery Target Validation)
**Criteria:**
- ✅ **Blended revenue:** $10K+ cumulative (recovery achieved)
- ✅ **Sustainability check:** At least 1-2 streams on positive unit economics
- ✅ **Month 4-6 runway:** Clear path to $15K+ by Aug 31

**If criteria NOT met:**
- Recovery shortfall: Activate contingency diversification (new stream launch) OR extend timeline to 6 months
- Unit economics poor: Prepare for deeper cost cuts or founder intensive mode (40-50 hrs/week)

**Decision authority:** CEO + Board Chair (CHAIR, Jason)

## APPROVED CONTINGENCY ACTIONS

**The CEO is authorized to execute contingency pivot actions without further CEO approval, as follows:**

### Contingency 1: P1 Fails (Revenue <$100 by May 15)
**Authorized actions (CEO may execute with COO):**
- Test paid ads ($500 budget, 2-week test)
- Reposition product messaging (3 new angles)
- Explore ProductHunt launch or affiliate bootstrap
- If pivot fails by Jun 15: Sunset P1, reallocate to P2+P3

### Contingency 2: P2 Fails (Zero Qualified Leads by May 15)
**Authorized actions (CEO + COO may execute):**
- Market validation survey (50 cold emails, 5 message variations)
- Reposition service (pricing, scope, delivery model)
- Switch to community authority + partnership channels
- If pivot fails by Jun 15: Sunset P2, reallocate to P1+P3 or new stream

### Contingency 3: P3 Fails (Zero Traffic by Jun 15)
**Authorized actions (CEO + COO may execute):**
- SEO audit + content quality revision
- Backlink building sprint (20 guest post outreaches)
- Paid ads test ($100/week, 2-week test)
- Consolidate to 1 niche site (kill 2 underperformers)
- If pivot fails by Jun 30: Sunset P3, reallocate or extend timeline

### Contingency 4: All Streams Fail (Revenue <$500 by May 31)
**Authorized escalation (requires CHAIR + CEO decision):**
- Emergency board meeting (May 31)
- Founder intensive mode trigger (40-50 hrs/week push)
- Activate new stream (YouTube automation, consulting, etc.)
- Consider external funding or strategic pivot
