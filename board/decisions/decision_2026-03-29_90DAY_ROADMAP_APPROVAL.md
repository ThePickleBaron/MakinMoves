## Decision Summary

## Strategic Rationale

**Why this roadmap?**
1. **Validated approach:** 3-stream diversification reduces single-point-of-failure risk
2. **Speed-to-market:** Week 1 launch across all 3 streams simultaneously
3. **Stress-tested:** Scenarios include conservative, realistic, and optimistic branches
4. **Decision-gate driven:** 4 explicit gates (Days 30, 45, 60, 90) allow pivot/acceleration without 90-day sunk cost
5. **Sustainable:** 5–6 hours/week matches stated capacity; hiring only if revenue validates

**What it solves:**
- Previous "build in parallel" decision lacked weekly clarity → **Roadmap provides day-by-day execution**
- Financial model lacked execution realism → **Roadmap includes risk mitigation + contingencies**
- No clear pivot points → **Roadmap has 4 decision gates with pre-planned scenarios**
- Board alignment unclear → **Roadmap specifies CEO/COO/CTMO/CFO responsibilities at each phase**

## Four Decision Gates (Go/No-Go)

### Gate 1: Day 30 (April 26, Noon)
**Question:** Launch Sprint — Did products launch successfully? Early revenue signal?

**Metric Thresholds:**
- **GO** (>$200 cumulative) → Continue Phase 1B full acceleration
- **CONDITIONAL** ($100–200) → Rebalance (identify strongest, accelerate)
- **NO-GO** (<$100) → Pause + Pivot (new niche, restart Week 5)

**Risk mitigation:** If P1 <$40, have 2–3 backup niches ready; pivot decision by May 1

### Gate 2: Day 45 (May 15, EOD)
**Question:** Staffing Decision — Revenue validated? Hire freelance writer for P2?

**Metric Thresholds:**
- **HIRE** (>$500 cumulative) → Allocate $200–400/week writer budget
- **CONDITIONAL** ($300–500) → Hire 1 writer, test at 1.5 articles/week
- **HOLD** (<$300) → Continue solo, re-evaluate Day 60

**Payback analysis:** If revenue $500+, writer ROI = 2–4 weeks

### Gate 3: Day 60 (May 24, Noon)
**Question:** Portfolio Validation — Is model fully validated? Accelerate Phase 1B or extend?

**Metric Thresholds:**
- **Scenario 1: Full Acceleration** ($500–1,200) → Unlock Tier 2 budget, hire aggressively, scale all 3 streams
- **Scenario 2: Selective Acceleration** ($300–500) → Identify strongest stream, focus resources, conditional hiring
- **Scenario 3: Pause + Extend** (<$300) → 2-week diagnostic, plan pivot for Week 9

**Timeline impact:**
- Scenario 1: $10K recovery by early August ✅
- Scenario 2: $10K recovery by August–Sept ⚠️
- Scenario 3: $10K recovery by Sept–Oct 🛑

### Gate 4: Day 90 (June 28, Noon)

**Metric Thresholds:**

## Risk Mitigation Summary

### P1 Risks & Mitigations
1. **Niche saturation (low conversion <0.1%)**
   - Mitigation: 2–3 backup niches ready; pivot by Week 5 if <$40
2. **Price wrong ($24.99 too high)**
   - Mitigation: A/B test pricing by Week 6
3. **Traffic insufficient (<50 views/week)**
   - Mitigation: Expand seeding (Reddit, email, Twitter) by Week 4

### P2 Risks & Mitigations
1. **SEO slower than expected (0 impressions by Week 3)**
   - Mitigation: Normal (3–5 day indexing); confirm GSC setup
2. **Email signup rate <1% (low list growth)**
   - Mitigation: Improve CTAs, test lead magnets by Week 3
3. **Affiliate networks reject (no revenue stream)**
   - Mitigation: Apply to 5–6 networks; fallback ready

### P3 Risks & Mitigations
1. **Design quality poor (0–1 clicks)**
   - Mitigation: Use Canva templates, benchmark Etsy by Week 2
2. **Redbubble moderation delays (designs not live)**
   - Mitigation: Test safe designs first; Teespring fallback
3. **Merch by Amazon rejected (no second platform)**
   - Mitigation: Focus Redbubble; plan Printful/Teespring by Week 4

### Macro Risks & Mitigations
1. **User unavailable (personal emergency, travel)**
   - Mitigation: Hire contractor; pause P2/P3, focus P1
2. **Platform rejections (Stripe, MBA, etc.)**
   - Mitigation: Activate fallback (PayPal, Redbubble, CJ) within 24h
3. **Market shift (AI prompts trend drops)**
   - Mitigation: Pivot to new niche (1-week sprint, Week 4–5)
