## DECISION RATIONALE

### Why This Approach Works
1. **Zero upfront cost** — Gumroad free tier, Stripe included, all tools free/self-hosted
2. **Proven demand** — 15K+ monthly searches for "AI prompts for freelancers"
3. **Fast execution** — Products 80% done, 2 weeks to launch is realistic
4. **Validates infrastructure** — Tests n8n, PostgreSQL, payment processing at scale
5. **Establishes revenue flow** — First dollar by Day 13, revenue tracking live Day 1
6. **Low risk** — Products already drafted, refund policy covers customer satisfaction

### Why Now
- CEO strategy approved (Gumroad as Stream 1)
- COO has capacity (all deliverables ready by Day 12)
- CTMO infrastructure ready (n8n + PostgreSQL running)
- Market validation done (research confirms demand)
- Jason capacity available (15–19 hours over 14 days is reasonable)

### Why This Scale (2 products vs. 1)
- **Bundle strategy** drives higher AOV ($49.99 vs. $24.99 single product)
- **Notion template** solves complementary problem (execution, not just ideation)
- **Diversifies revenue** — If prompts flop, template might convert
- **Minimal additional work** — Only 2 hours extra (Jason builds template Days 1–2)

## RISK MITIGATION APPROVED

### Risk 1: Low Sales (<20 units by Day 14)
**Mitigation:** Early Reddit validation (Days 4–12 shows engagement)
**Fallback:** Emergency marketing campaign (Day 15+)
- Paid ads (if Tier 2 budget available)
- Influencer partnerships (micro-influencers 1K–10K followers)
- Product pivot (different niche messaging)
**Approved:** Contingency plan documented, decision gate at Day 14

### Risk 2: Infrastructure Failure
**Mitigation:** Stress testing (Days 1–7), backup recovery drill (Day 3)
**Fallback:** Manual CSV export from Gumroad (until restored)
**Target recovery:** <1 hour downtime
**Approved:** Backup systems in place, recovery playbook documented

### Risk 3: Product Quality Issues
**Mitigation:** Full end-to-end QA (Days 1–10), test purchases (Day 10)
**Fallback:** Immediate file replacement, refund offered
**Approved:** QA process comprehensive

### Risk 4: No Community Engagement
**Mitigation:** Early seeding validation (Days 4–12), channel testing
**Fallback:** Shift to paid ads or partnerships (Day 13+)
**Approved:** Contingency marketing budget available if needed

### Risk 5: Payment Processing Failure
**Mitigation:** Stripe account verified + low-risk category (Day 1)
**Fallback:** Payoneer or manual payout backup
**Approved:** Verification process complete

**Overall Risk Assessment: LOW**
(All major risks have mitigation + fallback plans)
