# Board Decision: Phase 2 Expansion Framework (Months 6–12)
## Strategic Direction Post-Phase-1 Validation

**Date:** 2026-05-31 (pre-decision, pending Phase 1 completion)
**Owner:** CEO
**Approval:** Board (CEO + COO + CTMO + CFO)
**Status:** FRAMEWORK READY — Awaiting Phase 1 completion to activate

---

## DECISION SUMMARY

**Question:** How do we scale from $500–$1,500/month (Phase 1) to $10,000+/month (sustainable business)?

**Answer:** Identify winning stream by Month 6; execute specialized expansion strategy (Months 8–12); hire contractors to accelerate growth; validate SaaS/premium model by Month 12.

**Timeline:** Months 6–12 (July–December 2026)

**Budget:** $4,750–$10,100 (contractors, tools, infrastructure)

**Expected outcome by Month 12:**
- Conservative: $1,500–$2,200/month run rate ($3,000–$4,400 for 2-month period)
- Realistic: $2,000–$3,000/month run rate ($4,000–$6,000 for 2-month period)
- Aggressive: $3,000–$3,500/month run rate ($6,000–$7,000 for 2-month period)

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE: SPECIALIZATION THEN SCALING

We will NOT:
- Divide effort equally among 3 streams
- Launch multiple Phase 2 products simultaneously
- Hire multiple contractors until Phase 1 is automated

We WILL:
- Identify the 1 winning stream by Month 6
- Pour 50%+ of effort into that stream's expansion (Phase 2)
- Let Phase 1 run on autopilot with minimal maintenance
- Hire sequentially (1st contractor Month 9, 2nd Month 11+)

**Rationale:** Concentration of effort = higher revenue per hour invested. Multi-tasking kills execution velocity.

## DECISION POINT 1: MONTH 6 (JUNE 30)
**Decision:** Which stream generated 40%+ of Phase 1 revenue?

**Criteria for "winning":**
- Generated $150–$400/month minimum (Digital)
- OR $250–$600/month minimum (PoD)
- OR 200+ email list + consistent engagement (Affiliate)
- OR 2–3 active retainer clients (Services, if applicable)

**If no clear winner:**
- All three streams <$50/month each: Phase 1 strategy failed; reset
- Two streams equally strong: CEO picks based on scalability + personal preference
- One stream clear winner: Follow specialization path below

**Required documentation:**
- Revenue breakdown by stream (CFO)
- Customer feedback summary (COO)
- Product performance analysis (COO)
- CEO written decision to `/board/decisions/decision_2026-06-30_phase2-expansion.md`

---

## DECISION POINT 2: MONTH 8 (AUGUST 31)
**Decision:** Is Phase 2 product launched and generating revenue/interest?

**Success criteria:**
- Phase 2 product live (launch date: Week 4 of Month 8)
- Phase 2 revenue OR email signups hitting target:
  - Digital SaaS: 20+ waitlist signups
  - PoD new shop: $50+ revenue cumulative
  - Affiliate course: 30+ email signups from landing page
  - Service: 1+ client inquiry
- Phase 1 revenue stable or growing (did not drop >30%)

**If failed:**
- Phase 2 revenue $0 but 10+ signups: Continues; pivot positioning Month 9
- Phase 2 revenue $0 and <5 signups: Wrong target; rapid test (48 hrs) new positioning
- Phase 1 dropped 50%+: Pause Phase 2; investigate Phase 1 drop

**Required documentation:**
- Phase 2 launch summary (COO)
- Feedback from early customers/signups (COO)
- Competitive analysis if repositioning needed (CEO)

---

## DECISION POINT 3: MONTH 10 (OCTOBER 31)
**Decision:** Do we hire contractors? Is Phase 2 revenue-positive enough?

**Success criteria:**
- Phase 2 generating $300+/month
- Phase 1 stable at $1,000–$1,500/month
- Combined revenue $1,300–$2,000/month
- Clear SOP documented for contractor (COO)

**Hiring decision:**
- If YES ($1,300+/month): Hire 1st contractor immediately
  - Cost: $400–$1,000/month
  - Payoff: Phase 2 production +100%; revenue should reach $600–$1,200/month by Month 12
  - Role depends on stream:
    - Digital: Template designer or AI engineer ($500–$800/mo)
    - PoD: Design specialist ($400–$700/mo)
    - Affiliate: Content writer ($600–$1,000/mo)
    - Services: Delivery freelancer ($1,000–$2,000/mo)

- If NO (<$1,300/month): Wait until Month 12; extend Phase 2 4 weeks
  - Rationale: Budget is tight; hiring may not be sustainable
  - Alternative: CEO increases effort 10 hours/week; continue solo production

**Required documentation:**
- Phase 2 revenue analysis (CFO)
- Contractor needs assessment (COO)
- Budget runway analysis (CFO)

## DECISION POINT 4: MONTH 12 (DECEMBER 31)
**Decision:** Did we hit the recovery goal? Do we commit to Year 2 scaling?

**Success criteria (CHOOSE ONE):**
- **A: Total recovery ($10,000 goal hit)** → Victory declared; decision: keep scaling or stop?
- **B: Monthly run rate ($3,000+/month)** → Proof of $10K+/month model; commit Year 2
- **C: Monthly run rate ($1,500–$2,500/month)** → On track but slow; extend Phase 2 6 weeks
- **D: Monthly run rate (<$1,000/month)** → Model not viable; major pivot required

**Year 2 decision (if >$1,500/month):**
- **Path 1: Continue current model**
  - Hire full-time operations manager ($2,000–$3,000/month)
  - Target: $10,000/month by Q4 2027
  - Team: 2–3 contractors + ops manager

- **Path 2: Launch 3rd product line (premium/SaaS)**
  - Hire product/developer focus ($2,000–$3,000/month)
  - Target: $15,000+/month by Q4 2027
  - Team: 3–4 contractors + product person

- **Path 3: Hybrid (current business + advisory/coaching)**
  - Monetize expertise (sell consulting, courses, workshops)
  - Target: $20,000+/month by Q4 2027
  - Team: 2–3 contractors + ops person

**If <$1,000/month:**
- Pivot required; investigate Phase 1 assumptions
- Consider alternate business model or major repositioning
- Board review for strategic reset

**Required documentation:**
- 2026 annual performance review (CEO + CFO)
- 2027 strategic roadmap (CEO)
- Year 2 team + budget plan (COO + CFO)
- Go/no-go decision on continued scaling (CEO)

- CEO effort: 10–15 hours/week
- Contractor: None
- Budget: $500–$1,500 (domains, hosting, tools)
- Goal: Launch 3 streams; achieve $500–$1,500/month revenue

### Phase 2 (Months 6–12): Expansion
- CEO effort: 15–25 hours/week (Months 6–10), 10–15 hours/week (Months 11–12)
- Contractors: 1 (Month 9+), optional 2nd (Month 11+)

- Goal: Build Phase 2 product; achieve $1,500–$3,000/month revenue

### Year 2 (2027): Scale
- CEO effort: 10–20 hours/week (strategy + sales)
- Contractors: 2–3 (production + operations)
- Budget: $6,000–$10,000/month (payroll) + $1,000–$2,000 (tools)
- Goal: Achieve $10,000–$25,000/month revenue; establish team

## IF PHASE 1 FAILS

**Scenario A: Combined Phase 1 revenue <$300/month by Day 90**
- Action: DO NOT proceed to Phase 2
- Investigation: Which stream failed? Why?
- Decision required: Pivot to different business model or extend Phase 1 6 more weeks
- Timeline: Reset by July 2026

**Scenario B: One stream clear winner ($500+/month) but other two dead**
- Action: OK to proceed to Phase 2; specialize
- Investigation: Why didn't other streams work?
- Decision required: Kill underperformers or maintain minimal effort?
- Timeline: Proceed to Phase 2 as planned

**Scenario C: All three streams generating $150–$300/month each**
- Action: OK to proceed to Phase 2; pick most scalable stream
- Investigation: Diversification works but none is dominant
- Decision required: Which stream to accelerate?
- Timeline: Proceed to Phase 2 as planned
