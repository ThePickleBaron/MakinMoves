## BLOCK 1: Profitability Optimization (8:45–8:55 PM)

**Question:** How do we maximize profit margin on each stream?

**Deliverable:** Margin analysis + pricing power
```
P1 GUMROAD:
- What price point maximizes revenue? (Test: $17 vs. $27 vs. $47?)
- At different price points, what's the revenue forecast?
- Where's the elasticity break? (Customer drops off)

P2 FREELANCE WRITING:
- Current: $800–1200/mo per package
- Can we raise prices? By how much without losing clients?
- What's the margin per client? (Revenue - COGS in tools/time)

P3 AFFILIATE:
- Current: Commission varies by program (5–25%)
- Which niches have highest commission rates?
- Bundle multiple affiliate programs per niche to increase revenue per click?
```

**Specific output:**
- Price optimization recommendations (raise/lower/maintain)
- Margin % by stream at current pricing
- Potential revenue uplift if we optimize pricing

**Post to standup in CFO section update at 8:55 PM.**
