```
# Cross-Sell Sequence & Pricing Strategy

## P1 → P2 Cross-Sell
**Trigger:** Customer buys 2+ P1 products
**Sequence:**
1. Day 1 (purchase): Welcome email + product delivery
2. Day 3: Value-add email (extra tips, community link)
3. Day 7: Soft intro to P2 ("If you liked this, you might need...")
4. Day 14: Case study showing P2 ROI
5. Day 21: Direct offer ($200 discount for P2 annual)

**Expected conversion:** 5–10% of P1 buyers → P2 leads
**Revenue impact:** $200–400 additional per P1 customer

---

## P2 → P3 Cross-Sell
**Trigger:** P2 customer gets 2+ deliverables
**Sequence:**
1. Week 2 (onboarding): "Our writers also manage content sites..."
2. Week 4 (first results): Guest post placement on P3 site (thought leadership)
3. Week 8: Offer affiliate partnership (customer gets commission from P3 traffic)

**Expected conversion:** 10–20% of P2 customers → P3 partners
**Revenue impact:** Affiliate kickback + affiliate link clicks

---

## Pricing Rules (CFO-approved guardrails)

### P1 Pricing
- **Standard Price:** $37
- **Test High:** $67 (luxury position, test with influencers)
- **Test Low:** $17 (lead gen, test with ads)
- **Rule:** If conversion drops below 2%, lower price to $27. If >5%, test $47–$57.
- **Decision Point:** First 50 sales tell us what works. Then lock in.

### P2 Pricing
- **Tiers:**
  - **Starter:** $800/mo (2 articles/month, basic editing)
  - **Pro:** $1,200/mo (4 articles/month, strategy included)
  - **Premium:** $1,500/mo (6 articles/month, niche expertise, SEO audit)
- **Launch Price:** Start Premium tier at $1,200. Test $1,500 after first 3 customers.
- **Rule:** If CAC (customer acquisition cost) > $600, raise price. If <$300, lower price.

### P3 Pricing (Affiliate Revenue)
- **Target Commission Rate:** 5–10% per click (varies by partner)
- **Revenue Mix:** 60% affiliate, 30% ads, 10% direct sponsorships
- **Pricing Decision:** No pricing power (affiliate rates set by partners), focus on traffic volume
```
