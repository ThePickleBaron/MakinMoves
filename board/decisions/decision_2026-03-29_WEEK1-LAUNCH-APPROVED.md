## FAILURE CRITERIA (Escalate Immediately)

| Issue | Impact | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Account signup fails (payment rejected) | Blocks entire stream launch | Approve alternate payment method within 2h |
| Product upload fails (format error) | Can't go live | Flag CTMO for format conversion within 2h |
| Email service DNS fails | Can't collect subscribers | Wait 12–24h for DNS propagation (non-blocking) |
| First payment doesn't process | Revenue validation impossible | Investigate with platform within 1h |
| Social media account suspended (spam) | Traffic channel blocked | Review messaging tone, resubmit or pivot channel |

**Escalation rule:** Report immediately to CEO if any of above. CEO responds within 2 hours with decision.
