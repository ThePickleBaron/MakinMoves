## RATIONALE

### The Problem: Founder Capacity Bottleneck

**Current execution load:**
- 27-34 hours/week across P1, P2, P3
- Sustainability threshold: <25 hrs/week for 90+ day marathon
- Burnout timeline: Week 4-6 (Days 22-40) without mitigation

**Risk without hiring:**
- Content quality collapse by Week 5
- Customer acquisition slows (poor email copy)
- Revenue plateaus Month 5-6
- Founder stress exhaustion by end of May

---

### The Solution: Staggered Hiring

**Why three tiers:**
1. **Writer first (Day 25)** — Prevents system failure (P2 burnout)
2. **Social manager (Day 50)** — Unlocks secondary upside (visibility moat)
3. **Email designer (Day 75+)** — Maximizes email synergy (if budget available)

**Why staggered (not all at once):**
- Allows validation of P1/P2/P3 traction before committing full budget
- Onboarding one person at a time reduces training overhead
- If P1/P2/P3 fail by Day 30, can pause hiring and preserve cash
