## STRATEGIC DECISION SUMMARY

**Pivot from parallel launch (Phase 1) to growth through specialization (Phase 2).**

Phase 1 (Days 1–21) establishes three foundational revenue streams with minimal MVP products: Digital Products (Gumroad), Print-on-Demand (Redbubble/Merch), and Affiliate Marketing (content + email).

Phase 2 (Days 22–60) accelerates by identifying early winners and allocating disproportionate effort to maximize ROI. Core bet: **By Day 60, achieve $200–$600 cumulative revenue across all streams**, demonstrating repeatable customer acquisition patterns and establishing the infrastructure (email lists, design systems, content) for Phase 3 passive income.

## PRODUCT MIX STRATEGY

### Priority Allocation (Days 22–60)

**Digital Products: 25% effort (maintenance + strategic expansion)**
- If Phase 1 achieved 1+ sale: Accelerate (create 2–3 new templates, expand proven niche)
- If Phase 1 achieved $0: Execute 1 niche pivot by Day 24; test for 7 days; if still $0, deprioritize to 5%
- Target by Day 60: 4–5 templates, 20–30 sales, $50–$220 revenue
- Why: Template sales are repeatable and non-scalable with single human effort; best for organic discovery

**Print-on-Demand: 30% effort (volume + optimization)**
- If Phase 1 achieved 1–5 sales: Scale (create 20–30 total designs, focus on top-performing themes)
- If Phase 1 achieved $0 sales: Increase design production to 2/day (volume solves discovery problem)
- Target by Day 60: 25–30 designs, 40–80 sales, $240–$640 revenue
- Why: PoD is the highest-confidence revenue stream; volume directly correlates to sales; lowest customer acquisition friction

**Affiliate Marketing: 45% effort (content authority + email list)**
- Publish 2 articles/week (total 14–15 articles by Day 60)
- Build email list to 150–250 subscribers
- Expect zero revenue through Day 60 (SEO ranking takes 8–12 weeks); focus on leading indicators
- Target by Day 60: 0 revenue, but 200+ email subscribers and 2–3 articles ranking
- Why: Affiliate is 6-month play; Phase 2 builds infrastructure for Phase 3 passive income; email list is highest-leverage asset

### Optional SaaS Research (If Digital Products Hits $10/week)
- Allocate 5 hours (research only, no build) if Digital Products revenue >$10/week by Day 35
- Do NOT start SaaS build in Phase 2; reserve for Phase 3 if justified by Phase 2 revenue validation

## PRICING STRATEGY

### Digital Products Pricing
- **Entry-level (basic, <10 sections):** $15–$29
- **Mid-market (comprehensive, 20+ sections):** $39–$79
- **Premium (all-in-one with video):** $99–$199 (test only after 10+ sales)

**Pricing tactic:** Launch at $29, test raise to $39 if 3+ sales/week sustained. Bundle 3 templates at $99 (test premium pricing).

### Print-on-Demand Pricing
- Use platform defaults (Redbubble ~$16.99 base); test $19.99 on best-sellers Week 6+
- Volume > pricing; focus on design quality and marketing, not margin optimization

### Affiliate Pricing
- Commission-based (no direct pricing control)
- Target programs: 20–40% recurring (SaaS), 30% (tools)
- Strategy: Email list monetization (2 promotions/week, 2–5% conversion = recurring revenue)

## RISK MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Phase 1 Lands $0 Revenue (Probability: 20%)
**Mitigation:** By Day 24, auto-execute niche pivot (backup niche pre-selected). Give new niche 7 days (Days 24–31). If still $0, deprioritize to 5% effort.

### Risk 2: PoD Design Production Falls Behind (Probability: 40%)
**Mitigation:** If <10 designs by Day 28, increase production to 2 designs/day. Block 3 hours/day for design batching. Re-evaluate by Day 35.

### Risk 3: Affiliate Stalls (0 articles ranking by Day 45) (Probability: 60%)
**Mitigation:** This is expected. Affiliate takes 8–12 weeks. Expect zero revenue through Day 60. Focus on leading indicators: article impressions, email growth. By Day 90, expect first $50+ in commissions.

### Risk 4: Email List Doesn't Grow (Probability: 30%)
**Mitigation:** Add exit-intent popup Week 6. Create lead magnet. Cross-promote on Twitter/Reddit. Guest post in other newsletters. Target: 200+ by Day 60.

### Risk 5: Team Executes Blind Without Human Course-Correction (Probability: 60%, Days 31–60)
**Mitigation:** Establish decision rules in n8n (auto-executed). CFO dashboard auto-flags revenue drop >20% week-over-week. CEO writes weekly standups (even if autonomous). Weekly auto-escalation if metrics miss by >30%.
