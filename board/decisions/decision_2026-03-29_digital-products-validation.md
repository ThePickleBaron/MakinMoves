## Risk Assessment

### Low Risk Factors
✓ Zero startup costs
✓ No inventory or fulfillment
✓ Organic marketing proven
✓ Part-time compatible
✓ Multiple revenue paths
✓ Reversible (can pivot without sunk costs)

### Potential Challenges
- **Time to significant revenue:** 3-6 months for $5K/month income
- **Audience building:** Requires consistent content (daily social media)
- **Product quality:** Cheap/generic products don't sell well
- **Market saturation:** Some niches (general fitness, productivity) highly competitive
- **Validation:** Need real feedback/sales, not just building

### Mitigation
- Start with niche, underserved market (not general)
- Test pricing and messaging early via pre-sales
- Build personal brand + audience simultaneously with product
- Choose category with demonstrable demand
