### **REJECTED OPPORTUNITIES — 4 Opportunities (Strategic Rejection)**

#### ❌ Template Reselling (Dropshipping)
- **Reason:** CFO's March 29 agent research found white-label shadowban risk (0.5–2% conversion, not 2–5%). CEO just pivoted P1 AWAY from white-label. Re-introducing risk.
- **Economics:** 20–30% margin on $15–30 products = $2–5 per sale. Need 100+ sales/month for $200–500. Competes directly w/ P1 (own products, 100% margin).
- **Verdict:** REJECT

#### ❌ Community Membership (Slack/Discord)
- **Reason:** 3–5 hours/week moderation overhead in Q1 when capacity is constrained. Revenue ramp is slow ($0–200 M1–2, $1.25K–5K M6). Requires 1K+ audience first.
- **Economics:** $950–9.8K Month 6 (50–200 members × $19–49), but time cost is not worth it until Month 4+.
- **Verdict:** REJECT for Q1; revisit Q2 if P1/P2/P3 automated

#### ❌ White-Label Services (Done-for-You Affiliate Sites)
- **Reason:** $100/hour service work vs. $100+/hour product work. Wrong unit economics. 20–40 hour per-project time cost vs. 0 hours for Layer 2. Sales cycle 2–4 months (no Month 1 revenue).
- **Economics:** $2K–5K per project = $100/hour effort. Not defensible at scale (client does it themselves after first site).
- **Verdict:** REJECT

#### ❌ Job Board Marketplace
- **Reason:** Network effect not available (need supply + demand simultaneously). Marketplace revenue competes w/ product sales (10–20% commission vs. 100% on P1/P2/P3). Moderation + legal risk.
- **Economics:** 50–100 jobs/month × 10–20% commission = $200–400/month best case. Not worth the infrastructure.
- **Verdict:** REJECT
