---

## Rationale

### Why This Order?

**Digital Products → Fastest Time-to-First-Dollar**
- Research validated: 5-21 days to first revenue
- ADHD planners: $25-45 price point (proven market)
- Canva bundles: 10-15 min per design (rapid creation)
- Setup: 2-3 hours for 15 templates
- Month 1 potential: $100-300

**Print-on-Demand → Parallel Revenue (Medium Speed)**
- Research validated: 15-60 days to first revenue
- Sticker bundles: Highest velocity (15-30 days)
- Redbubble: Instant activation (no approval wait)
- Setup: 2-4 hours for 15 designs
- Month 1 potential: $50-150

**Affiliate Marketing → Long-Term Passive (SEO Pipeline)**
- Research validated: 4-8 weeks to first clicks
- AI Tools for SMBs: 30-50% lifetime commission
- Content: 10 articles, setup 3-5 hours
- Month 4-6 potential: $400-700+/month
- Parallel development (doesn't block other streams)

## Risk Mitigation

**If Digital Products Sell Slower Than Expected:**
- Pivot: Add more PoD designs, lean on higher-velocity stickers
- Fallback: Expand affiliate content (still builds SEO pipeline)

**If PoD Hits Approval Issues (Merch by Amazon):**
- Mitigated: Launching on Redbubble first (instant, no approval)
- Parallel: MBA application in progress, designs ready to upload

**If Affiliate Content Quality Suffers:**
- Quality gate: Claude generates, COO reviews before publish
- Fallback: Focus on Digital/PoD until affiliate pipeline matures
