## THE DIRECTIVE

**COO/RUNNER Protocol:** If new accounts are needed, RUNNER must have signup pages open and ready for immediate action.

---

## What This Means

### COO's Responsibility:
1. **Identify account needs** during product development
2. **Flag to RUNNER immediately** — "We need Gumroad for Product #2" or "We need Stripe for payment processing"
3. **RUNNER keeps signup pages open** and ready to activate on-demand

### RUNNER's Responsibility:
1. **Monitor COO requests** for new accounts
2. **Have signup pages open and staged** (not actually signing up yet, just ready)
3. **On COO signal:** "Sign up for X now" → RUNNER executes immediately (1-5 minutes)
4. **Report back:** Account created, credentials passed to relevant role (CTMO/CFO)

---

## Timing
- **Zero delay.** If COO says "we need Stripe," RUNNER completes signup in < 5 minutes
- **No waiting on platform approval.** Have pages ready so signup happens instantly when needed
