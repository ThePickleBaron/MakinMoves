## BLOCK 3: Growth Hacking Strategies + Viral Loops (8:55–9:15 PM) [+50% time/depth]

**Question:** How do we accelerate growth beyond organic? What creates viral loops?

**Deliverable:** Growth hacks + viral coefficient analysis

```
P1 GUMROAD — Aggressive Growth:
- Affiliate strategy: Find 20 creators (not 10) in template/design niche, offer 30–40% commission
  - Target: Design influencers, YouTube template channels, Reddit communities
  - Incentive: Tiered commission (higher as they sell more)
  - Tracking: Commission dashboard, monthly payouts

- Bundle strategy: Create themed bundles (3–5 templates) at 30% discount
  - "Startup founder package", "Content creator bundle", "Small business kit"
  - Scarcity: "Limited bundle, expires Sunday"
  - Expected uplift: 2–3x AOV (average order value)

- Social proof: Customer testimonials + case studies
  - First 5 customers get 50% off for video testimonial
  - Feature in email, product page, Twitter/LinkedIn
  - Expected effect: 10–20% conversion uplift from social proof

- Viral mechanics: Referral rewards
  - Buy a template, get $5 credit for each referral
  - Low friction (email to friends, social share)
  - Expected virality: 20–30% of buyers refer

P2 FREELANCE — Niche Domination:
- Niche targeting: Become #1 writer for specific niche (not generic)
  - Pick 1 niche (SaaS, fintech, healthtech, e-commerce, etc.)
  - Target 20 companies in that niche directly
  - Pitch: "I've written for [X companies in your space]. Here's what worked."

- Testimonial farming: First 5 clients get 50% off + revisions
  - In exchange: 5-minute video testimonial
  - Feature testimonials on landing page + LinkedIn
  - Expected effect: 30–50% higher close rate with social proof

- Content marketing: Weekly LinkedIn articles on niche topic
  - Position as thought leader in freelance writing for [niche]
  - Generate inbound leads from content
  - Expected: 1–2 inbound leads per week (Month 2+)

- Affiliate partnership: Partner with writing tools, AI assistants
  - Get featured in their customer success stories
  - Embed affiliate links (Substack, Notion, Grammarly)
  - Expected revenue: Low (5–10% of freelance revenue), but brand lift

P3 AFFILIATE — SEO + Influencer:
- Backlink strategy: Find 100 relevant blogs/forums (not 50)
  - Offer: "I'll write a guest post for your audience if you link to our niche site"
  - Target sites: Similar authority, different audience
  - Expected: 30–50 backlinks in Month 1

- Co-marketing: Partner with 10 micro-influencers (not 5) in niche
  - Free niche site access + affiliate commission (20% of their referrals)
  - They feature site in newsletter, YouTube, TikTok
  - Expected: 500–1000 initial traffic spike + recurring referral traffic

- SEO focus: Target 50 long-tail keywords in Month 1 (not just top keywords)
  - Long-tail: "Best [tool] for [specific use case]" (high intent, low competition)
  - Content: 500-word buyer's guide per keyword
  - Expected: 1000–2000 organic visitors by Month 3

- Product integration: Get featured in relevant tool reviews
  - TrustRadius, G2, Capterra reviews for your recommended products
  - Affiliate commissions + traffic from review pages
  - Expected: 5–10% of affiliate revenue

GENERAL GROWTH:
- Giveaway campaigns: Free niche site content (lead magnet)
  - "Get our free 'best tools' PDF" in exchange for email
  - Expected: 500–1000 emails for your list

- Community building: Subreddit presence, Discord, Slack communities
  - Participate authentically in relevant communities
  - Link to niche site in bio/profile
  - Expected: 10–20% of traffic from communities by Month 2
```
