# Decision: Q1 2026 Revenue Strategy — All Three Streams (Parallel, Time-Weighted)

**Date:** 2026-03-29 15:00
**Proposed by:** CEO
**Status:** APPROVED

---

## Strategic Decision

**Launch all three revenue streams simultaneously in Q1, with time allocation prioritized by time-to-first-dollar:**

1. **Digital Products** (40% focus, Weeks 1–3)
   - Fastest to revenue: 2–4 weeks possible
   - Lowest barrier: Free tools (Canva, Notion)
   - Target: First template live by end of Week 1, first sale by end of Week 2

2. **Print-on-Demand** (35% focus, Weeks 1–3)
   - Medium-speed revenue: 3–4 weeks
   - Proven market: Merch by Amazon, Redbubble
   - Target: 5 designs live by Week 2, first sales by Week 3

3. **Affiliate Marketing** (25% focus, Weeks 1–3, scales to 50% Week 4+)
   - Slower initial revenue: 4–8 weeks for first affiliate click
   - High passive upside: Recurring commissions at scale
   - Target: 5 articles published by Week 2, content SEO pipeline established

---

## Rationale

1. **Diversification:** Three streams reduce risk of single-stream failure. If one stalls, two others generating revenue.
2. **Time-to-revenue:** Digital Products likely to hit revenue target by end of March. PoD follows. Affiliate provides long-term passive base.
3. **Low competition:** Each stream has proven demand but isn't saturated in 2026 market.
4. **Resource fit:** With 30 min/day available, all three are achievable in parallel (1-3 hour chunks during Week 1–2).
5. **Recovery timeline:** Combined target of **$175–$500/month by Day 90** provides clear recovery trajectory.

## Financial Impact

**By End of Q1 (Day 90):**

## Phase Breakdown

### Phase 1: Foundation & Launch (Days 1–21)
- All three streams setup + MVP products live
- Time allocation: 40% Digital, 35% PoD, 25% Affiliate
- Weekly target: 3–5 hours available

### Phase 2: Growth (Days 22–60)
- Double down on momentum
- Time allocation: 20% Digital, 30% PoD, 50% Affiliate
- Scale by publishing volume and design creation

### Phase 3: Maturation (Days 61–90)
- Establish passive baseline
- Maintenance mode: 15–45 min/week per stream
- Analyze what works; prepare Q2 roadmap

## Trade-offs Considered

**Option A:** Focus Digital Products only (fastest revenue)
- ✓ Highest certainty of quick sales
- ✗ Single point of failure; less diversification
- ✗ Doesn't meet "multiple streams" goal

**Option B:** Focus Affiliate only (best long-term passive)
- ✓ Best 12-month upside
- ✗ No revenue for 4–8 weeks (doesn't meet recovery urgency)
- ✗ Creates cash flow gap

**Option C:** All three parallel, weighted by opportunity ← **CHOSEN**
- ✓ Hits recovery timeline (Day 90: $175–$500)
- ✓ Diversifies risk
- ✓ Fits 30 min/day constraint with phase approach
- ✓ Aligns with "multiple streams" mission
