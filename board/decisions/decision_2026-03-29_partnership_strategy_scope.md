# Decision: Partnership Strategy Scope — Critical Bandwidth Constraint

**Date:** 2026-03-29
**Proposed by:** CEO (with strategic analysis)
**Status:** Requires CEO ratification + board alignment

---

## SITUATION

CEO identified 5 partnership opportunities with projected $900-1,700/mo by Month 3:
1. Gumroad Ambassador
2. Beehiiv + ConvertKit Affiliate
3. Notion Templates
4. Airtable Templates
5. Indie Hackers Sponsorships

**Question:** Should all 5 be pursued, or should bandwidth be triaged?

---

## KEY FINDINGS (From Deep Investigation)

### Commission Reality vs. CEO Forecast

| Partnership | CEO Expected | Actual Structure | M3 Reality |
|-------------|--------------|-----------------|-----------|
| Gumroad | 10% on creator setup fees | $10 per referral (per Gumroad terms) | $0-100/mo, not $500/mo |
| Beehiiv | $80-150 per signup | 50% recurring commission for 12 months ✓ | $200-400/mo possible ✓ |
| ConvertKit | $100-200 per signup | 30% recurring for 24 months ✓ | Part of Beehiiv/ConvertKit combined |

| Indie Hackers | $1K-2K ROI on $500-1K sponsorship | Negative ROI in April (product unproven), positive in May | Defer to May |

### Bandwidth Reality

**Total CEO workload if pursuing all 5 partnerships:**
- Core P1/P2/P3: 90-150 hours/month
- Partnerships (setup + ongoing): 40-60 hours/month
- **Total: 130-210 hours/month** (assuming 160 available)
- **Result: 20-50 hours/month OVERBOOKED**

**What gets sacrificed:** P1 product quality, P2 sales velocity, or P3 content consistency.

## STRATEGIC OPTIONS

### Option A: Pursue All 5 (CEO's Original Plan)
**Pros:**
- Maximum upside potential ($1.5K-2K/mo partnerships by M6)
- Diversified revenue sources
- Tests all opportunities

**Cons:**
- Overbooks CEO by 20-50 hours/month
- Execution will be mediocre across all fronts
- High risk of missing core P1/P2/P3 targets
- Templates require 100+ hours upfront (opportunity cost massive)
- Sponsorships have negative ROI in April (better in May)

**Expected outcome:** $600-900/mo partnerships + $6-8K/mo core = $6.6K-8.9K/mo total (BELOW recovery target of $10K)

---

### Option B: Tier 1 Only (Recommended)
**Tier 1 (Do immediately):**
- Beehiiv/ConvertKit affiliate (aligned with P3, minimal effort)

**Tier 2 (Do in May):**
- Indie Hackers sponsorship (after P1 has proof)

**Tier 3 (Defer or skip):**
- Gumroad, Notion templates, Airtable templates

**Pros:**
- Preserves core P1/P2/P3 bandwidth (90-150 hrs/mo stays available)
- Aligns partnerships with existing work (Beehiiv fits P3 naturally)
- Defers high-effort, low-ROI items (templates)
- Improves sponsorship ROI by waiting for product proof

**Cons:**
- Lower partnership upside ($400-600/mo partnerships)
- Less revenue diversification
- Fewer experimental channels tested

**Expected outcome:** $400-600/mo partnerships + $10-12K/mo core = $10.4K-12.6K/mo total (EXCEEDS recovery target)

## DECISION FRAMEWORK

**CEO must choose based on:**

1. **Risk tolerance:** Option A has upside but execution risk is HIGH. Option B has lower upside but higher probability of hitting $10K recovery target.

2. **Bandwidth reality:** If CEO is working <160 hours/month, Option A overbooks. If CEO can genuinely work 180-200 hrs/month, Option A is viable.

3. **Core revenue confidence:** If CEO is uncertain about P1/P2/P3 reaching $10K/mo, partnerships are insurance. If CEO is confident, partnerships are distraction.

4. **Personal preference:** Does CEO prefer "focused excellence" (Option B) or "maximum optionality" (Option A)?

---

## RECOMMENDATION: OPTION B

**Rationale:**
1. Higher probability of hitting recovery target ($10K/mo by Month 5)
2. Preserves bandwidth for core P1/P2/P3 optimization
3. Eliminates wasteful partnership efforts (templates)
4. Improves partnership ROI (defer sponsorships until May)
5. Keeps partnerships aligned with existing work (Beehiiv in P3 content)

**Math:** $10.4K-12.6K/mo total revenue (M5-6) is better than $6.6K-8.9K/mo from Option A.
