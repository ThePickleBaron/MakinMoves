# Decision: P1 Gumroad Revenue Model Revision
**Date:** 2026-03-29 17:00
**Proposed by:** CEO (via stress-testing)
**Status:** RECOMMENDATION FOR BOARD REVIEW

---

## THE QUESTION
Are the P1 Gumroad revenue assumptions realistic? Can we rely on Month 1 = $0-200 revenue?

---

## ANSWER: YES, BUT WITH CRITICAL CAVEAT

The model is viable **IF external traffic strategy is executed in parallel with product build.**

Without external traffic strategy, Month 1 = $0-50 (not $0-200).

---

## 3 KEY FINDINGS

### 1. Gumroad Organic Discovery Is Not the Revenue Driver
- 53% of Gumroad traffic comes from Direct/Email (not internal Discover)
- 34% of Gumroad products make zero sales (lifetime)
- Successful creators build audience OUTSIDE Gumroad, then launch

**Implication:** Product launch must coincide with email list (200+) or Twitter activation.

### 2. Market Saturation Is Niche-Specific
- Writing & Publishing: 226 products, $15.75K avg ✅ OPPORTUNITY
- Photography: 4.2K+ products, $2.8K avg ❌ OVERSATURATED
- Gaming: 779 products, $971 avg ❌ OVERSATURATED

**Implication:** Niche selection matters more than product quality. Must choose Writing, Fitness (niche-specific), or Software.

### 3. Revenue Timeline Is Execution-Dependent
- With warm audience (500+ email): 2-4 weeks to $1K ✅
- Without audience (cold start): 8-12 weeks to meaningful revenue ✅
- **BUT:** Current model doesn't specify which scenario

**Implication:** Need to clarify traffic strategy before launch.

---

## REVISED FINANCIAL ASSUMPTIONS

### Base Case (RECOMMENDED FOR PLANNING)
**If:** Email list 200+ built before launch OR Twitter 500+ activated
```
Month 1: $50-300 (email launch + external traffic)
Month 2: $200-800 (momentum builds)
Month 3: $500-1.5K (multiple channels active)
6-Month Total: $4.3K-11K
```

### Downside Case (If NO external traffic strategy)
**If:** Product launches with zero email list or external traffic
```
Month 1: $0-50 (Gumroad Discover only)
Month 2: $25-150 (slow organic growth)
Month 3: $100-300 (starting to recover)
6-Month Total: $1K-4K
```

### Upside Case (Warm launch + multiple channels)
**If:** Email 500+, Twitter 2K+, Medium articles, multiple sources active
```
Month 1: $400-2K (strong launch)
Month 2: $800-3K (momentum + referrals)
Month 3: $1.5K-5K (flywheel effects)
6-Month Total: $10K-37K
```

## BOARD DECISION REQUIRED

### Option A: MOVE FORWARD (Recommended)
- Approve revised assumptions (Base Case $4.3K-11K, 6-month)
- Require CEO to specify traffic strategy in writing
- Require COO to commit to parallel execution (product + email building)
- CFO to integrate revised model into financial projections

### Option B: HOLD & CLARIFY
- Request traffic strategy document from CEO before launch
- Request proof of 100+ email subscribers OR 500+ Twitter followers before launch
- Delay launch 2-4 weeks to build audience first

### Option C: PIVOT
- If traffic strategy not feasible, reallocate resources to P2/P3
- Treat P1 as secondary opportunity (12-month horizon)
