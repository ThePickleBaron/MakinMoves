## OPERATIONAL RATIONALE

**Burnout risk (if 1 writer only):**
- P2 base case targets 5 active customers by Month 5
- 5 customers = 150% of 1-writer capacity (unsustainable)
- Result: Quality drops, churn accelerates, entire stream collapses

**Impact of writer backup:**
- 1 CEO writer: Handles sales + strategy (10-15 hrs/week)
- 2 freelance writers: Handle execution (combined 25-30 hrs/week)
- Capacity: Sustainable 5-6 customers without burnout, room for growth

**Margin impact:**
- Without writer: 60% gross margin (hypothetical)
- With writer: 45-50% gross margin (realistic)
- Better 50% sustainable margin than 60% unsustainable margin

---

## APPROVAL CONDITIONS

**Backup writer hire approval contingent on:**
1. ✅ P2 achieves 2+ active customers by Apr 30 (proof of concept)
2. ✅ CEO validates writer needed for capacity (not premature hire)
3. ✅ COO has recruitment process ready (by Apr 15)

**If contingencies not met:**
- Defer hire to Jun 1 (reassess if P2 is viable)
- Use freelancer pools: Upwork, Freelancer.com (lower commitment)

---

## WRITER SPECIFICATIONS

**Hiring criteria:**
- 5+ years content/copywriting experience
- Ability to write B2B SaaS + thought leadership content
- Time commitment: 20-30 hrs/week (remote, async)
- Rate: $50-75/hour (industry standard for freelance writers)

**Onboarding process:**
- CEO trains on voice/brand standards (8 hrs)
- Pair on first 4 articles (QA + feedback)
- Independent by Week 4 of hire
