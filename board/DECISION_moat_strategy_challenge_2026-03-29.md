### Option A: Trust CEO's Judgment (Proceed as Planned)
**Recommendation:** Launch Mar 30 with visibility-first strategy

**Decision trigger:** Apr 8 results determine strategy pivot (if needed)
**Risk:** 1-week delay, but eliminates moat risk
**Upside:** Data-driven decision by Apr 8; no guessing

**Decision required:** CEO approval to run validation test; commit to acting on results

---

### Option C: Shift to Email-First Now (Recommended)
**Recommendation:** Deprioritize Twitter; focus on email list + community
**Changes to execution:**
- Reduce Twitter posting from 5x/week to 2-3x/week (utility channel, not moat builder)
- Prioritize email signup mechanism on all products (Week 1 deliverable)
- Build content calendar for SEO + email value (3 articles/week instead of 5 Twitter posts)
- Community building (Discord/Reddit) starts concurrently
- Keep founder visibility, but subordinate to owned assets

**Risk:** Twitter growth slower (but email list compensates 5-20x over)
**Upside:** 50-100% higher revenue by Month 3; more defensible moat

**Decision required:** CEO approval of strategy shift; COO confirmation of execution capacity
