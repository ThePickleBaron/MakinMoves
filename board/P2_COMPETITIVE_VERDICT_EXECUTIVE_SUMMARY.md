## THREE VIABLE NICHE ANGLES

### Option A: SaaS Founder Thought Leadership
- **Price:** $2,500–$3,500/month
- **Deliverables:** 1–2 thought pieces + LinkedIn strategy
- **TAM:** ~50,000 SaaS founders
- **90-day potential:** 2–3 customers = $5,000–$10,500 MRR
- **Moat:** Founder voice (hard to replicate, AI can't interview)
- **Verdict:** ✓ BEST — Low competition, sticky customers, AI-proof

### Option B: FinTech Compliance Content
- **Price:** $3,000–$4,500/month
- **Deliverables:** 4–6 pieces + legal review coordination
- **TAM:** ~15,000 fintech companies
- **90-day potential:** 1–2 customers = $3,000–$9,000 MRR
- **Moat:** Regulatory expertise, high switching cost
- **Verdict:** ✓ STRONG — Premium rates, stable demand, limited competition

### Option C: SaaS Lead-Gen Content (Outcome-Based)
- **Price:** $2,000–$3,000/month (outcome SLA)
- **Deliverables:** 4 articles + SEO + lead tracking
- **TAM:** ~100,000 growth-stage SaaS
- **90-day potential:** 2–3 customers = $4,000–$9,000 MRR
- **Moat:** Performance guarantees, outcome-based positioning
- **Verdict:** ✓ STRONG — Large TAM, high growth potential, differentiated vs. pure writing

## BOARD DECISION REQUIRED

### ❌ DO NOT PROCEED with current $600–$1,200 generalist positioning

**Why:**
- Commodity pricing creates zero moat
- Competes against 10,000+ freelancers + offshore + AI
- No proof/reviews to justify premium over Upwork pros
- Margin insufficient for backup writer hire (Month 4 squeeze)
- Vulnerable to 12-month commoditization cycle

**Expected outcome:** 40–60% revenue miss vs. forecast, churn spike, pivot by Month 5

---

### ✅ APPROVE P2 with mandatory conditions:

**Condition 1: Choose a niche** (before Month 1 launch)
- SaaS thought leadership, OR
- FinTech compliance, OR
- SaaS lead-gen (outcome-based)

**Condition 2: Revise pricing** (to match niche, not commodity)
- $2,500–$4,500/month for niche premium positioning
- NOT $600–$1,200 generalist commodity

**Condition 3: Proof investment** (Months 1–3)
- 1 published case study per niche positioning
- 3–5 customer testimonials
- Author byline on 2–3 published pieces

**Condition 4: Confirm runway** ($10,000 minimum)
- Covers 3-month burn before revenue
- Includes backup writer hire by Month 4
- CFO confirms availability

**Condition 5: Set pivot trigger** (Month 5)
- If <$2,000 MRR by end of Month 5, shut down
- Redeploy resources to P1 or P3
- Clear exit criteria prevents sunk losses
