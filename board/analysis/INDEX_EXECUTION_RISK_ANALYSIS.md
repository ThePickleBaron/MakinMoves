## KEY INSIGHT: THE HIDDEN HOUR PROBLEM

**Plan's design flaw:** Underestimated "hidden hours" in content production

**What was counted:** 4 articles/week writing = 4 hours
**What was NOT counted:**
- Research per article: 20 min × 4 = 80 min (1.3 hrs)
- Keyword research: 15 min × 4 = 60 min (1 hr)
- Editing/polish: 25 min × 4 = 100 min (1.7 hrs)
- Article promotion (Twitter/Reddit/email): 60 min × 4 = 4 hrs (HUGE hidden cost)

**Real time:** 4 + 1.3 + 1 + 1.7 + 4 = **12 hours/week** (not 4 hours)

**Plus Twitter daily:** 30 min × 6 days = 3 hrs
**Plus email + Substack nurture:** 4–5 hrs/week

**Total: 19–22 hours/week of actual task work (not 5–6)**

**Solution:** Hire writer by Day 25 to own articles (removes 12 hrs/week). Founder focuses on promotion + P1 + P3 (remaining 10–12 hrs/week).
