## RISK ASSESSMENT: WHAT COULD GO WRONG?

### Risk #1: P2 Sales Cycle Extends (HIGH IMPACT)
- **If:** Sales cycle becomes 16 weeks instead of 8-12 weeks
- **Then:** M6 customers drop from 4 to 3; revenue -$1,200; profit -$660
- **Impact:** Recovery delays 1 month
- **Mitigation:** Pre-warm 20 prospects NOW; use email sequences to compress cycle

### Risk #2: P1 Traffic Strategy Doesn't Execute (CRITICAL)
- **If:** Email list doesn't reach 200; no Twitter audience
- **Then:** P1 M6 revenue $500 (not $2,500); profit -$3,000
- **Impact:** Recovery delays 2 months
- **Mitigation:** Start email building this week; queue Medium articles; pre-schedule Twitter posts

### Risk #3: P2 Churn Exceeds 10% Monthly (MEDIUM IMPACT)
- **If:** Early-stage service churn hits 10% vs. 3-5% assumed
- **Then:** M6 customers drop to 2-3; revenue -$2,400; profit -$1,320
- **Impact:** Recovery delays 1-2 months
- **Mitigation:** Implement QA checklist; 30-day check-ins; satisfaction guarantee

### Risk #4: P3 Content Bottleneck (MEDIUM IMPACT)
- **If:** Content publishing stalls (life happens, burnout, etc.)
- **Then:** P3 M6 revenue $2,500 (not $7,500); profit -$2,500
- **Impact:** Recovery delays 1 month
- **Mitigation:** Batch-write content in Month 1; hire freelance writer by Month 3

### Risk #5: Infrastructure Costs Higher Than Budgeted (LOW IMPACT)
- **If:** Costs are $300/month instead of $195
- **Then:** Profit impact: -$630 over 6 months (4% of total profit)
- **Impact:** Negligible
- **Mitigation:** Use Docker cost optimization; monitor weekly
