## MOMENTUM RULES

1. **Every day, find 1 new angle.** Not all will launch same day, but identify the next one.

2. **Never go 48 hours without a new launch.** If you do, that's a gap. Fill it.

3. **If metrics flat for 3 days, change something.** Platform not working? Pivot to next one. Content not resonating? Change angle.

4. **Scale what works. Kill what doesn't.** Track which revenue stream gives best ROI per minute. Double down.

5. **No single stream > 40% of focus.** Diversify. 5-7 streams by Week 4.

6. **Always have a queue.** When one launches, next should be 48h behind.

---

## RED FLAGS (Fix Immediately)

- **No new email subscribers for 24h:** Tweak lead magnet or CTA
- **No new Twitter followers for 48h:** Change content angle or posting time
- **No Calendly bookings by Day 3:** Lower price, increase visibility, add to more posts
- **YouTube zero views on new video:** Thumbnail bad, title bad, or not shared enough
- **Discord not growing:** Not promoting invite, CTA weak, or value not clear
- **Affiliate clicks zero for 48h:** Links not visible, content not driving traffic, or wrong audience

**Fix = 15 min pivot, not abandonment.**
