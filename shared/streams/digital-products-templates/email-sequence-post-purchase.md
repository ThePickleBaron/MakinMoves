## EMAIL 3: The Philosophy (Day 5)
**Subject:** Why I built these tools (and what most traders get wrong)

I built these trackers because I kept making the same mistake: entering positions without documenting why.

When the position goes against you, you need something to anchor to. Was your thesis wrong? Or is the market just noisy? Without documentation, you can't tell — and you either panic-sell a good thesis or diamond-hand a broken one.

The Entropy & Divergence approach:
- Every position needs a written thesis
- Every thesis needs a break condition (what would prove you wrong)
- Every exit needs to be decided BEFORE you're emotional

The templates force this discipline. That's the whole point.

If you want more on this framework — the weekly Substack breaks down real trades using this system:
→ entropyanddivergence.substack.com

See you there.

— Jason
