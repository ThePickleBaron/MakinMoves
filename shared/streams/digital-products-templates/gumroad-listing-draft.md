## Full Description

### Stop Losing Track of Your Positions

If you're trading across multiple accounts — individual, IRA, rollover — you know the pain. Brokerage dashboards only show you one account at a time. Spreadsheets get messy fast. Aggregator apps raise privacy concerns.

**This template solves that.**

Built from a real active trader's daily workflow (not a designer guessing what traders need), this Notion template gives you a single source of truth for your entire portfolio.

### What's Inside

**Positions Database** — Track every position with the fields that actually matter: symbol, description, sector, quantity, current price, market value, cost basis, cost per share, gain/loss (% and $), percentage of account, dividend yield, and asset type.

**Account Summary Dashboard** — See your total portfolio at a glance. Individual, IRA, rollover — everything in one view with account-level totals.

**Thesis Notes** — Document why you entered each position, your price targets, stop levels, and exit criteria. Stop relying on memory.

**Realized Gains Log** — Track closed positions with open/close dates, proceeds, cost basis, gain/loss, and short-term vs. long-term classification. Makes tax season painless.

**Watchlist** — Track the tickers you're stalking with target entry prices and your thesis for each.

**Sector Allocation View** — See exactly how concentrated (or diversified) you are across sectors at a glance.

**Macro Dashboard** — Track the macro indicators that inform your trades: oil, gold, DXY, yields, or whatever matters to your strategy.

### Who This Is For

- Active traders managing positions across multiple brokerage accounts
- Investors who want more than what their broker's dashboard shows
- Anyone tired of juggling spreadsheets and multiple browser tabs
- Traders who document their thesis and want it linked to their positions

### Who This Is NOT For

- Passive index fund investors (you don't need this)
- Day traders executing 50+ trades/day (you need a broker-native tool)
- People who've never used Notion (try it free first at notion.so)

### How It Works

1. Duplicate the template to your Notion workspace (one click)
2. Enter your positions (or paste from your broker's CSV export)
3. Customize views to match your workflow
4. Update as you trade — all views auto-refresh

### Why $9?

Because I built this for myself first, and I'm launching it to see if other traders find it useful. Early buyers get the lowest price — it goes up after the first wave of reviews.
