## Full Description

### Stop Losing Track of Your Positions

If you're trading across multiple accounts — individual, IRA, rollover — you know the pain. Brokerage dashboards only show you one account at a time. Spreadsheets get messy fast. Aggregator apps raise privacy concerns.

**This template solves that.**

Built from a real active trader's daily workflow (not a designer guessing what traders need), this Notion template gives you a single source of truth for your entire portfolio.

---

### What's Inside

**Positions Database**
Track every holding with the fields that actually matter: symbol, description, sector, quantity, current price, market value, cost basis, cost per share, gain/loss (% and $), percentage of account, dividend yield, asset type, and position status.

**Thesis Notes**
Document why you entered each position, your price targets, stop levels, and exit criteria — linked directly to each holding. Stop relying on memory. When a position is down 15% and your gut says "sell," read your thesis first.

**Realized Gains Log**
Track every closed position with open/close dates, proceeds, cost basis, gain/loss, and short-term vs. long-term classification. **Wash sale tracking included.** Makes tax season painless.

**Watchlist**
Track the tickers you're stalking with target entry prices, thesis, catalyst, and priority level (Hot/Warm/Cold).

**Trade Journal**
A running decision log separate from thesis notes. Document what you did, why you did it, and what happened. The single best tool for improving as a trader.

**Account Summary Views**
See your total portfolio at a glance. Filter by Individual, IRA, Rollover — everything in one workspace with account-level views.

**Sector Allocation View**
See exactly how concentrated (or diversified) you are across sectors at a glance. Never accidentally build a 30% position in one sector again.

**Dividend Calendar**
Track upcoming dividend pay dates across all holdings.

### Who This Is NOT For

- Passive index fund investors with 3 ETFs (you don't need this)
- Day traders executing 50+ trades/day (you need a broker-native tool)
- People who've never used Notion (try it free first at notion.so)

### How It Works

1. Duplicate the template to your Notion workspace (one click)
2. Delete the sample data
3. Add your current positions (paste from your broker's CSV export)
4. Customize views to match your workflow
5. Update as you trade — all views auto-refresh
