# Active Trader Portfolio Tracker — Notion Template Setup Guide

**Welcome!** You've purchased the Active Trader Portfolio Tracker template. This guide walks you through setup in 5 minutes.

---

## STEP 1: Duplicate the Template (1 min)

1. **Click the template link** you received in your purchase email
2. Click **"Duplicate"** in the top-right corner
3. Choose where to save it (recommend: your main Notion workspace)
4. Notion will create a copy in your account

**That's it!** You now have your own working template.

---

## STEP 2: Customize Your Information (2 min)

### Portfolio Overview Page
- Update "My Portfolio" title (optional, for your reference)
- Change the currency symbol if needed ($ to £, €, etc.)
- Add your total portfolio target/goal

### Positions Database
- Edit the **sample positions** to match YOUR portfolio:
  - **Ticker:** Stock symbol (AAPL, MSFT, etc.)
  - **Shares:** Number of shares you own
  - **Entry Price:** What you paid per share
  - **Current Price:** Today's price (update manually or link to API)
  - **Account:** Where you hold it (Fidelity, Robinhood, etc.)
  - **Sector:** Stock category (Tech, Finance, etc.)
  - **Thesis:** Why you bought it (short description)

- **Delete the sample data** once you've added your real positions

### Realized Trades Database
- Add past trades you want to track (tax/wash sale analysis)
- Or leave empty if you're starting fresh

### Watchlist Database
- Add stocks you're watching (not yet bought)
- Track catalysts and thesis for each

---

## STEP 3: Use the Views (1 min)

Your template includes multiple views for different use cases:

**Positions Database:**
- **All Positions** — Full table of everything you own
- **By Sector** — Board view grouped by sector (see concentration)
- **By Account** — Board view grouped by brokerage (see where your money is)
- **Active Positions** — Filtered to only current holdings
- **Dividend Calendar** — Your dividends by month

**Realized Trades Database:**
- **All Trades** — Complete trading history
- **Wash Sales** — Filtered to trades that might trigger wash sale rules

**Use cases:**
- Need to rebalance? Check "By Sector" to see if tech is overweight
- Tax time? Check "Wash Sales" for trading losses
- Dividend planning? Check "Dividend Calendar" for income projections

---

## STEP 4: Add Formulas & Automations (Optional)

The template includes basic formulas for:
- **Gain/Loss %** — Automatic calculation of % gain/loss per position
- **Total Value** — Sum of all positions
- **Dividend Yield** — Annual dividend as % of portfolio

You can extend these with:
- Price alerts (when a stock hits a target)
- Email notifications (when you add a new position)
- Automated price updates (if you link to an API)

---

## STEP 5: Link Your Brokerages (Optional)

To auto-update prices, consider:
1. **Zapier** (zapier.com) — Connect Notion to TradingView/Yahoo Finance APIs
2. **Make** (make.com) — Similar integration tool
3. **Manual updates** — Just enter prices yourself (quickest for small portfolios)

---

## QUICK TIPS

✅ **Update prices weekly** — Keep data fresh for accurate portfolio snapshot  
✅ **Add notes in Thesis** — When you review, you'll remember why you bought  
✅ **Use Board views** — Better visual representation than tables  
✅ **Backup your data** — Notion auto-saves, but export CSV of Positions quarterly
