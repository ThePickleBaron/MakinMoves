# Twitter Thread Template + Hook Swipes
**For:** @EntropyDivergence account

---

## THREAD FORMULA

### The 10-15 Tweet Pattern

**Tweet 1 (Hook):** Contrarian observation or surprising fact (1 sentence)  
**Tweets 2-3 (Setup):** Why this matters (context + consensus)  
**Tweets 4-7 (Analysis):** Deep-dive on second-order effects  
**Tweets 8-10 (Implication):** What this means for traders  
**Tweets 11-14 (Position):** How to think about this  
**Tweet 15 (CTA):** Subscribe/follow call  

**Format:** "Thread →" or "🧵" at end of hook, then reply-chain
