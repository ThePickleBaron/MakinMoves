## Our Competitive Advantages

1. **Multi-account is unique** — Nobody else lets you view Individual vs IRA vs Rollover in the same system with account-level summaries. This is our #1 differentiator.

2. **Thesis notes linked to positions** — Zero competitors do this. Traders WANT to document why they entered a trade. This is the feature that makes Reddit posts authentic.

3. **Tax features (wash sales, ST/LT classification)** — Nobody addresses tax season. This is a real pain point for active traders. We're the only template that tracks wash sales.

4. **Trade journal** — Separate from thesis notes. Running log of decisions + outcomes. The #1 feature traders wish they had (per Cowork's research).

5. **Price positioning** — $9 for a template that has more features than $25-60 competitors. Impossible to argue against.

6. **Built by an actual active trader** — Not a Notion designer guessing. Real workflow, real columns from real brokerage exports. This shows in the product.

## Recommendations

1. **Launch at $9 confidently** — we have more features than templates priced 2-6x higher
2. **Lead marketing with multi-account + thesis notes** — these are unique
3. **Mention tax features (wash sales) in listing** — nobody else does this, it's a search magnet during Jan-April
4. **Template #2 candidate: Automated version with Python/Zapier** — premium tier at $19-29 for technical users who want live prices
5. **Don't compete on live pricing** — we can't win that in Notion. Win on workflow, organization, and decision-making instead
