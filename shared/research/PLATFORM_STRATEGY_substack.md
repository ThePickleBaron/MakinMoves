## PLATFORM OVERVIEW

**Substack** is the email newsletter platform for paid content. Your Substack is the hub — all other platforms drive traffic here.

## BRIEFING FORMAT (What Premium subscribers get)

**Publication day:** Tuesday 8 AM ET  
**Length:** 700-800 words  
**Structure:**

```
SUBJECT LINE (40 chars max): "The [divergence] That Changes Everything"

---

OPENING HOOK (50-100 words):
What changed, why you noticed it, why it matters

FIRST-ORDER EFFECTS (150-200 words):
What everyone is pricing in already

SECOND-ORDER EFFECTS (250-300 words):
What's being missed, what cascades from primary move
- Direct effect
- Second-order effect
- Third-order effect (where others give up thinking)

TIMELINE (50-100 words):
When does this unfold? Weeks? Months? Years?

POSITIONING (100-150 words):
How to think about this. Specific trade ideas (optional).
Not recommendation, but thesis structure

CLOSING (50 words):
Why this matters to your portfolio
Next week's signal: what to watch
```
