## PLATFORM OVERVIEW

**Medium** is a content distribution platform with 100M+ monthly readers. Publishing on Medium drives SEO backlinks, distributes content to new audiences, and feeds Substack growth via audience crossover.

**Key Stats:**
- Medium Partner Program: $0-1 per 1,000 reads (variable, not guaranteed)
- RSS feed automation: Cross-post articles at scale
- Average article reads: 200-500 (new writer) to 1,000-2,000 (established)
- Traffic to external links: 5-15% of readers
- SEO authority: Medium domain is strong, helps your author profile

---

## STRATEGY: RSS AUTOMATION

**Why RSS automation?**
1. Write once (on your platform)
2. Syndicate everywhere (Medium, Substack cross-post, LinkedIn, etc.)
3. Reach new audiences (Medium readers who'd never find you otherwise)
4. Zero additional effort (scheduled, automated)

**Mechanics:**
1. Write article in your primary platform (e.g., Substack, Ghost, or Google Docs)
2. Create RSS feed from that platform
3. Use Zapier/IFTTT to auto-post to Medium when feed updates
4. Medium canonicalizes back to original (SEO credit stays with you)
5. Readers on Medium get exposed, click through for more

## MEDIUM PUBLICATION MECHANICS

### How to Get Reads (The Algorithm)
1. **Initial push:** First 24 hours determine if Medium algorithmically promotes
   - Target: 50+ claps in first 24 hours (subscribers + email alerts)
   - Target: 5+ comments in first 24 hours (signals engagement)

2. **Medium algorithm:** If article gets traction, promoted to broader audience
   - Claps weighted heavily (reader appreciation signal)
   - Read time weighted (do people finish?)
   - Engagement rate weighted (comments = high signal)

3. **SEO:** Google indexes Medium articles, drives long-tail traffic
   - Articles rank for niche keywords (macro + divergence + specific thesis)
   - Drive traffic to your author profile
   - Author profile links drive to Substack/email

### Publication Timing
- **Tuesday-Thursday:** Best days for publication (higher engagement)
- **10 AM ET:** Optimal publish time
- **Avoid Monday/Friday:** Lower engagement on weekends adjacent days
- **Avoid during major macro events:** Noise too high, articles get buried

## RSS AUTOMATION SETUP

### Step 1: Create RSS Feed
- If using Substack: RSS is built-in (substack.com/api/v1/posts?publication_id=[ID])
- If using Ghost, WordPress, or custom platform: RSS feed is standard

### Step 2: Set Up Zapier Integration
**Trigger:** "New post in RSS feed"  
**Action:** "Create article on Medium"

**Settings:**
- Article title: Use RSS title
- Article body: Use RSS content (strip formatting, add Medium formatting)
- Featured image: Use RSS image if available
- Canonical URL: Point back to original source (SEO credit)
- Tags: Auto-populate with keywords

### Step 3: Schedule Regular Posts
- 2-3 articles per week → 8-12 articles/month on Medium
- Each article reaches 500-1,500 readers on Medium (new audience)
- Drive 5-10% through to Substack link (50-150 new subs/month from Medium)

## WHY MEDIUM OVER SUBSTACK/GHOST

1. **Distribution:** Medium algorithm pushes good content (Substack/Ghost don't)
2. **New audience:** Reach people who'd never find your own platform
3. **SEO:** Medium domain authority helps rankings
4. **Credibility:** Publishing on established platform (vs. personal blog)
5. **Minimal effort:** RSS automation means write once, syndicate everywhere
6. **No cannibalization:** Canonical URLs mean SEO credit goes to you

3. **Series:** Connect articles into narrative (drive readers through your catalog)
4. **Links:** Every article links to Substack (convert readers to email list)
