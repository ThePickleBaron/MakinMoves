## Tier 1: High Priority (Include in Article 1)

### TradingView
- **Commission:** 30% on every subscription purchase
- **Cookie duration:** 90 days (registration) + 90 days (subscription)
- **Recurring:** Yes — 30% on all recurring payments as long as referral stays subscribed
- **Referral incentive:** $15 discount coupon for new users
- **Payout:** Within 30 days after month end
- **Why priority:** Massive brand recognition among traders. High subscription prices ($14.95-$59.95/mo) = strong per-referral revenue. 30% recurring on a $59.95/mo plan = ~$18/mo per referral indefinitely.
- **Revenue potential per referral:** $4.50-$18.00/month recurring
- **URL:** tradingview.com/partner-program/

### Sharesight
- **Commission:** Up to 50% on subscriptions
- **Duration:** Up to 12 months' worth of commissions
- **Recurring:** Yes, up to 12 months
- **Why priority:** Highest commission rate in this niche. Strong product for portfolio tracking + tax reporting. Plans range ~$15-$31/mo.
- **Revenue potential per referral:** $7.50-$15.50/month (for up to 12 months)
- **URL:** sharesight.com/us/affiliates/

### Stock Rover
- **Commission:** 25% on all new and ongoing subscriptions
- **Recurring:** Yes — ongoing as long as referral subscribes
- **Payout:** ~14 days after end of each calendar month
- **Why priority:** Essential tool for fundamental analysis. Plans at $7.99-$27.99/mo. Good fit for our active trader audience.
- **Revenue potential per referral:** $2.00-$7.00/month recurring
- **URL:** stockrover.com/about-us/become-an-affiliate/
