# Twitter Thread: Macro Framework

The market prices headlines. You price the cascade.

---

Most traders react to what's already public. By then, it's priced. Real edge comes from understanding second-order effects before they cascade through markets.

---

This is the Entropy & Divergence thesis: find where institutions diverge from reality, then position before the market corrects that gap.

---

Three layers to this:
1. Spot the divergence (what's mispriced?)
2. Thesis the correction (why will it move?)
3. Position the convergence (scale & conviction)

---

That's the framework. More on this tomorrow.
