## Template Sections to Track

### 1. Shipping Data (Weekly Update)

**KPLER (Vessel Routing):**
- % of oil routing via Cape of Good Hope
- % via normal Suez route
- Baseline (pre-disruption): 15% Cape, 85% Suez
- Target: When Cape >40%, silo problem is acute

**Clarkson Rates (Daily, aggregate weekly avg):**
- VLCC spot: $/day
- Aframax spot: $/day
- Trend: Up/down/stable

**EIA Data (Weekly release Wednesdays):**
- US Strategic Petroleum Reserve level
- Crude stocks (domestic)
- Refinery utilization
- Asian imports (when available)

---

### 2. Production & Storage (Weekly)

**OPEC Production:**
- Official target (bpd)
- Actual production (bpd)
- Gap (voluntary cuts?)

**Well Status (Check industry news):**
- Any announced production cuts?
- Any sanctions impact?
- Any infrastructure damage?

**Storage Status:**
- Saudi strategic reserves capacity %
- Iraqi port activity levels
- Iranian export levels (when available)

---

### 3. Stock Fundamentals (After Earnings or Weekly)

**For DHT, EURN, FRO, TEK:**
- Stock price (Friday close)
- Dividend announced?
- Guidance on rate environment?
- Share buybacks? (=management confidence)

---

### 4. Geopolitical (Daily Scan, Weekly Summary)

**Hormuz Risk Level:**
- Green (low disruption risk)
- Yellow (elevated risk, some rerouting)
- Red (significant disruptions ongoing)
- Black (severe, major impact)

**News to monitor:**
- Houthi statements/attacks
- Iranian military activity
- US Navy movements
- Coalition statements
- Shipping insurance rates (price of risk)

---

### 5. Divergence Signal Scoring

**Every Friday, score the divergence (1-10):**

- 1-3: No divergence, consensus = reality
- 4-6: Emerging divergence, possible mis-pricing
- 7-9: Clear divergence, significant opportunity
- 10: Extreme divergence, high conviction trade

**Scoring rubric:**
- Silos at >85% capacity? +3 points
- Rates declining while disruption ongoing? +2 points
- Market pricing rates as "temporary"? +2 points
- Production cuts announced? +2 points
- Stock trading below book value despite high rates? +1 point
