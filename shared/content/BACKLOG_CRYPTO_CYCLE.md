## Twitter Thread: "The Crypto Pump-Dump Pattern Nobody Talks About"

Bitcoin just hit $[PRICE]. Everyone's bullish. Here's what they're missing about cycles.

---

The pattern is mathematical:
1. Accumulation (institutions quietly buying, 6-12 months)
2. Breakout (retail FOMO, violent spike, 1-3 months)
3. Peak (institutional exit, subtle divergence forms)
4. Capitulation (panic selling, 2-4 weeks)

Most traders trade the spike. Real money trades the divergence before the peak.

---

How to spot it:
- Volume spikes on up days but declines on dips = distribution
- Price hits ATH but fewer new wallets holding = whale exit
- Social sentiment peaks before price peaks = contrarian signal

The crash isn't random. It's synchronized selling into retail FOMO.

---

The edge: **Buy the fear, sell the celebration.**

Position during accumulation (boring phase, takes months).
Exit at peak (looks premature, feels wrong).
Watch it crash, repeat.

This is how macro thesis beats price action.

## Context/Why This Works

Crypto has short cycles (6-18 months) vs stocks (3-5 years). Easier to see divergence. When sentiment is euphoric but whale wallets are static, the peak is near.

**Data points to track:**
- BTC accumulation address growth (on-chain metric)
- Twitter sentiment vs actual holder growth (lagging indicator)
- Exchange inflows (smart money sells into rallies, causing inflows)
- Funding rates on futures (extreme long positions = peak risk)
