# Stream 2 Scope: Freelancer Invoice + Client Tracker

**Objective:** Build a second revenue stream targeting freelancers + solopreneurs (different audience than active traders).

**Status:** Feasibility research + scoping

---

## MARKET OPPORTUNITY

### Problem (for freelancers):
- Juggling 5+ clients
- No single place to track who owes what
- Invoice generation is clunky
- Tax time is a nightmare (need to track all client revenue + expenses)
- No way to forecast income (contract end dates, renewal uncertainty)

### Solution (Stream 2):
Notion template: "Freelancer Client + Invoice Tracker"
- Client database (all your clients, rates, contract terms)
- Invoice tracker (what you've sent, paid status, due dates)
- Income forecast (when money's coming in)
- Tax summary (revenue by client, expense categories)

## PRODUCT STRUCTURE

### Core databases:
1. **Clients** — Name, email, rate/retainer, contract start/end, payment terms
2. **Invoices** — Invoice #, client, date sent, amount, due date, paid status
3. **Projects** (optional) — Project name, client, hours logged, billable amount
4. **Expenses** (optional) — Date, category (software, hardware, etc.), amount, tax deductible

### Key views:
- **All Clients** — Overview of who you work with
- **Active Clients** — Contracts currently active (due date > today)
- **Past Due Invoices** — Red flag view (what's overdue)
- **Monthly Income** — Calendar view of when money arrives
- **Tax Summary** — Revenue by client, expense categories, quarterly estimates
- **Forecasted Income** — Next 3 months based on contracts + retainers

### Formulas:
- **Invoice total** = hours × rate (or fixed amount)
- **Days overdue** = today - due date
- **Client lifetime value** = sum of all invoices from that client
- **Monthly revenue** = sum of invoices paid this month
- **Quarterly tax estimate** = revenue × 25% (default, customizable)
