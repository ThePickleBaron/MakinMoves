# 90-Day Implementation Roadmap

**Goal:** Launch 3 revenue streams in 90 days, focusing on fastest time-to-first-dollar while building scalable, passive income systems.

**Available time:** 30 min/day max (~3.5 hours/week)

**Expected outcome by Day 90:**
- Digital Products: 3+ templates live, 2–5 sales/month
- Affiliate Marketing: 20+ articles published, 100+ email subscribers, first affiliate clicks
- Print-on-Demand: 15+ designs live, 1–3 sales/month
- Combined potential: $50–$300+/month by end of Q1

## DAILY ROUTINE (Sustainable 30 min/day)

Once systems are set up (around Week 4), aim for this rhythm:

**Daily (5–10 min):**
- Check sales across all 3 streams (Gumroad, Redbubble, Merch, affiliate dashboard)
- Respond to any customer messages
- Post 1 promotional message on Twitter/Reddit (rotating streams)

**3x/week (20–30 min):**
- Create 1 new design (PoD, Mon/Wed/Fri)
- OR write/finish 1 article (Affiliate, Tue/Thu/Sat)
- OR refine/optimize existing products (Digital, as needed)

**1x/week (30 min):**
- Email marketing: Write promotional email to list (combine promotions from all streams)
- Analytics review: Check which products are selling, traffic trends
- Promo push: Share top performers again across social

**1x/month (60 min):**
- Revenue analysis: Which stream is performing best? Why?
- Pivot decision: Double down on winners, kill losers
- Plan next month focus

**Total weekly time:** ~3.5 hours (within your 30 min/day constraint)
