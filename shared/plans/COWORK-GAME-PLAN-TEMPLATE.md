## Example: What Good Looks Like

**Bad:** "Your plan looks good. I'll do my part."  
**Good:** "Your timeline assumes Substack approval in 2 days; industry standard is 5–7. Suggest we add buffer or parallel Medium setup. How will you handle if TradingView rejects us?"

**Bad:** "I disagree."  
**Good:** "I disagree because the $9 price point might undervalue the template. Competitors charge $29–49 for Notion portfolio trackers. Have you considered testing higher price first?"
