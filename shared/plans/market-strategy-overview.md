## COMPETITIVE POSITIONING

**Why this market?**
- Underserved: Most portfolio trackers built for passive investors
- High intent: Active traders actively search for solutions
- Recurring revenue: Affiliate programs + email list can generate ongoing income
- Low CAC: Content marketing (Reddit/Twitter) = free distribution

**Differentiator:**
- Built by actual trader (credibility)
- Specific to multi-account active traders (narrow, high-value niche)
- Hands-on templates + walkthroughs (not just theory)
- Honest tool comparisons (not just promoting owned products)
