# Beehiiv vs. Substack — Platform Decision

## Recommendation: Stick with Substack as primary, skip Beehiiv for now.

### Why Substack wins for E&D right now:
1. **Built-in discovery** — Finance category, recommendations, restacks. Beehiiv has zero discovery.
2. **Paid subscriptions built in** — No separate payment integration needed beyond Stripe.
3. **Already set up** — Publication live, article published, categories configured.
4. **Network effects** — Substack readers discover new writers through the platform. Beehiiv readers only come from your own marketing.
5. **Zero cost** — Substack takes 10% of paid revenue (only when you earn). Beehiiv free tier limits to 2,500 subscribers, then $49/mo.

### When Beehiiv makes sense (revisit at 500+ subscribers):
- If you want more email automation (drip sequences, segmentation)
- If you want referral program features (Beehiiv has built-in referral tracking)
- If Substack's 10% cut becomes significant (at $1,000+/mo revenue, that's $100/mo)
- If you want to own the subscriber list fully (Substack export is fine, but Beehiiv gives more control)

### Bottom line:
At 0-100 subscribers, discovery > automation. Substack wins on discovery. Revisit Beehiiv when you hit 500+ subscribers and need advanced segmentation.
