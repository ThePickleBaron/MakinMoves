## Project Principles

1. **Low maintenance first.** Every stream must work with 30 min/day or less.
2. **Low/no upfront cost.** You're recovering from financial loss. No paid tools unless essential.
3. **Digital-first.** No physical inventory, no location-dependent income.
4. **Diversify.** Multiple small streams beat one risky bet.
5. **Realistic timelines.** No "make $10K in 30 days" hype. Honest assessments only.
6. **Legitimate models.** Nothing sketchy or deceptive. All earned through real value delivery.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I really do this in 30 min/day?**
A: Yes, but only after Week 1 (setup takes 15–25 hours). After that, 3.5 hours/week is sustainable. Scaling beyond $500/mo requires more time later, but initial launch is doable.

**Q: Which stream should I focus on first?**
A: All 3 are designed to run in parallel (they complement each other). But if forced to choose: Digital Products first (fastest to first $), then Affiliate (longest ROI), then PoD (medium timeline).

**Q: What if I don't have any "skills"?**
A: These streams don't require advanced skills. Canva does design for you. WordPress has plugins that do SEO. Merch by Amazon handles manufacturing. You just need: problem-solving (choosing niches), consistency (showing up), and marketing (promotion).

**Q: When should I expect real revenue?**
A: First $1–$50 by Week 4. First $100+/month by Week 8–12. This assumes consistent execution. If you miss weeks, timeline extends proportionally.

**Q: What if one stream fails?**
A: You have 2 other streams. This is why diversification matters. Fail fast (2–4 weeks), pivot, try again. By Week 8, you'll know which streams work for you.

**Q: Can I share these plans with someone else?**
A: These plans are specific to your situation. Share the philosophy, not the exact steps. Your niches are your competitive advantage.
