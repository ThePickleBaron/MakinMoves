### Upsides
- ✅ **Consulting upside:** If succeeds, +$25K–$32K additional revenue
- ✅ **Accelerated recovery:** June 15 vs. July 25 (1–2 week speed boost)
- ✅ **High-margin revenue:** Consulting is 60–70% margin (vs. PoD 40% margin)
- ✅ **Phase 2 scaling:** Consulting creates path to $50K+/mo in Phase 2
- ✅ **Zero downside:** If consulting fails, fallback = Trinity baseline timeline

### Downsides
- ⚠️ **Execution risk:** Consulting test requires 20 hours (May 1–15)
- ⚠️ **May timeline:** Consulting won't generate revenue until June (delayed start)
- ⚠️ **Decision complexity:** May 15 gate adds operational decision point
- ⚠️ **Team bandwidth:** CEO/COO need to be available for sales calls

### Net Assessment
**Risk-Adjusted Expected Value:** +$15K–$20K additional revenue by August 31 (even after accounting for 60% failure rate). **Hybrid is clear winner.**

## Fallback Protections

### What If P2 Consulting Fails?
- **Pre-positioned freelance writers:** Already identified and contract-ready
- **Fallback revenue:** $1.5K–$2.5K/mo (proven baseline from existing model)
- **Setup cost:** Zero (no new infrastructure needed)
- **Timeline impact:** None (P1+P3 continue uninterrupted)
- **May revenue loss:** $0 (fallback launches May 16, sustains $1.5K–$2.5K baseline)

**Verdict:** Consulting failure is NOT a risk. It's a pivot point with zero downside.

### What If P1 Underperforms?
- **P2 + P3 compensate:** If consulting succeeds, covers P1 shortfall
- **Or fallback:** If consulting fails, freelance + P3 still hit baseline timeline
- **Worst case:** All three streams hit conservative estimates = still $8.6K–$12K by Aug 31

### What If P3 Stalls?
- **P1 + P2 compensate:** If consulting succeeds, covers P3 shortfall
- **Contingency:** Activate paid traffic (Google Ads) or add P3 niche #4
- **Worst case:** Without P3, still hit $15K–$25K if P1+P2 succeed

**Conclusion:** Portfolio is highly resilient. No single stream failure derails recovery.
