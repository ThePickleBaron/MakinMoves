**Competitors:**
- Animalz: $7,500+/mo (thought leadership)

- Contently: $5,000+/mo (enterprise platform)
- Beam: $2,500+/mo (flexible collaborative)
- Uplift: $2,000+/mo (SaaS-focused)

**ICP:**
- Size: 2–5 person teams or solo founders
- Revenue: $0–$2M ARR
- Budget: $600–$1,500/mo
- Pain: Upwork vetting, quality inconsistency, time burden
- Needs: Consistency, relationship, strategic input, reliability

**CAC:**
- Warm outreach: $100–$200
- Cold outreach: $150–$300
- Paid ads (optional): $300–$500
- Blended organic: $100–$200

**LTV:**
- 12-month retention: 60%
- Monthly revenue: $850 average
- Customer lifetime: $3,000–$3,500
- Payback period: 0.4–0.6 months

## Key Insights & Differentiators

### Market Gap
- No agency owns the $600–$1,500/mo segment with clear positioning
- Upwork dominates low-end but has consistency and vetting problems
- Agencies dominate high-end but pricing excludes bootstrap SaaS
- **Our position:** Goldilocks option—agency quality, freelancer price, retainer relationship

### Competitive Advantages
1. **Price:** 50–70% cheaper than agencies; 20–30% cheaper than Upwork all-in
2. **Consistency:** Same writer every month; coherent brand voice
3. **Relationship:** Accountability through service (we replace writer if needed)
4. **Vetting:** Pre-vetted pool, not marketplace lottery
5. **Strategic input:** Topic suggestions, content calendaring, market insights
6. **Speed:** 2 days to setup vs. 4–8 weeks for agencies

### Positioning Statement
*"Skip the Upwork chaos and mid-market agency costs. Get a dedicated SaaS writer who learns your market and delivers consistent quality at $600–$1,500/month."*

### Go/No-Go Signals
**Green lights:**
- Market gap confirmed
- Low CAC ($100–$300)
- High LTV ($3,000+)
- Fast breakeven (month 2–3)
- Founder fit (plays to content strength)

**Red lights (none at this stage):**
- Will test during week 1–3 pilot phase
