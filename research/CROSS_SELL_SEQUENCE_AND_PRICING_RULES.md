## PRICING GUARDRAILS

### P1 (Digital Templates) — Pricing Rules

**Starting Price:** $37 per template

**Testing Window:** First 50 sales (approximately Week 1-2)

**Guardrail Rules:**

| Metric | Current | Healthy | Action (If Breached) |
|--------|---------|---------|----------------------|
| **Conversion Rate** | TBD | >2% | If <2%, lower to $27 (reduce friction) |
| **Revenue per Template** | $37 | >$30 | If <$30 (poor conversion), test lower price |
| **Repeat Purchase Rate** | TBD | >5% (2nd template) | If <5%, offer bundle discount ($79 for 3 templates) |

**Price Testing Logic:**

1. **Weeks 1-2:** Launch at $37
   - If conversion >5%, you have pricing power
   - If conversion 2-5%, price is right
   - If conversion <2%, lower to $27 immediately

2. **After Week 2:** Optimize based on data
   - High conversion (>5%) → Test $47–$57 with influencers
   - Healthy (2-5%) → Maintain $37, increase marketing
   - Low (<2%) → Lower to $27, test different traffic source

3. **Decision Point (Day 30):**
   - Lock in winning price for next 30 days
   - Only re-test if conversion drifts >1% point

**Premium Pricing:** $67–$97 for bundles (3–5 templates as "packages")
- Bundle discount: 25% off ($79 for 3 templates vs. $111 retail)
- Scarcity: "Limited time bundles, expire Sunday"
- Expected uplift: 2–3x AOV (average order value)

### P2 (Freelance Writing Services) — Pricing Rules

**Tier Structure:**

| Tier | Price | Deliverable | Target Customer | Priority |
|------|-------|-------------|-----------------|----------|
| **Starter** | $800/mo | 2 articles/month | Consultants, solopreneurs | Launch with this |
| **Pro** | $1,200/mo | 4 articles/month + strategy | SaaS founders, small teams | Primary target |
| **Premium** | $1,500/mo | 6 articles/month + SEO audit + monthly strategy call | Scale-stage SaaS companies | Upsell after Month 2 |

**Pricing Rules:**

| Metric | Healthy | Alert | Action |
|--------|---------|-------|--------|
| **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)** | <$500 | >$700 | Lower price to $800 (Starter tier) or stop paid ads |
| **Close Rate** | >30% | <20% | Revise pitch, add case study, lower price |
| **Customer Tenure** | >3 months | <2 months (churn) | Improve onboarding, increase deliverable quality |
| **Upgrade Rate** | >20% (to Premium) | <10% | Sweeten Premium tier, add extra service |

**Pricing Decision Logic:**

1. **First Customer:** Offer Pro tier ($1,200/mo)
   - Track if they upgrade to Premium or churn
   - If upgrade, maintain Premium upsell
   - If churn, lower to Starter ($800) next pitch

2. **After 3 Customers:** Evaluate mix
   - If 2+ at Pro or Premium: Maintain pricing
   - If 2+ at Starter: Price too high, lower anchor to $1,000 Pro
   - If mixed: Keep tiers, emphasize Pro as standard

3. **By Day 60:** Lock pricing strategy
   - Avoid re-pricing mid-contract (confuses customers)
   - Only new customers get pricing changes

**Guardrail:** Never go below $600/month (need to cover writer cost + buffer)

### P3 (Affiliate Sites) — Pricing Rules

**No Direct Pricing (Affiliate Commission Structure)**

**Revenue Model:**

| Commission Type | Rate | Volume | Expected Revenue |
|-----------------|------|--------|-------------------|
| **Affiliate Commissions** | 5–10% | 1,000–5,000 clicks/month | $500–2,500/month |
| **Display Ads (Google AdSense)** | $0.50–$5 CPM | 50,000–100,000 pageviews | $50–500/month |
| **Sponsorships (Direct)** | $1,000–5,000 | 1–2 per month | $1,000–10,000/month |

**Guardrail Rules:**

| Metric | Healthy | Alert | Action |
|--------|---------|-------|--------|
| **Site Traffic** | >10K visitors/month | <5K at Month 2 | Increase publishing frequency, SEO push, or pause site |
| **Affiliate Revenue Per Site** | >$500/month | <$200 at Month 3 | Renegotiate commission rates, add more affiliate partners |
| **Click-to-Commission Rate** | 5–10% conversion | <3% | Improve product alignment (recommend better products) |
| **Average Session Duration** | >3 min | <1 min | Improve content quality, add more detailed reviews |

**Site Economics (Breakeven Analysis):**
- **P3 hosting cost:** $15–20/month per site
- **Breakeven traffic:** 300–400 affiliate clicks/month (at 5% commission rate on $30–50 tools)
- **Timeline to profitability:** 4–6 months

**Decision Logic:**

1. **Month 1:** Launch, no profitability expected
2. **Month 2:**
   - If >5K visitors and 100+ clicks: On track, continue
   - If <5K visitors and <50 clicks: SEO strategy failing, consider pivot
3. **Month 3:**
   - If >$300 revenue: Keep and scale
   - If <$200 revenue: Pause site, redeploy effort to higher-performing site

## GUARDRAILS SUMMARY (CFO Controls)

**Price Changes Require CFO Approval If:**
- P1 changes >$10 from current price
- P2 changes >$200/month from current price
- Any price change happens more than 2x per quarter

**Auto-Kill Trigger (CEO Decides):**
- P1 template: 0 sales in 14 days → test lower price or discontinue
- P2 client: CAC >$1,000 → pause cold outreach, retarget warm leads
- P3 site: <100 traffic at Month 3 → pause content, redirect effort

**Auto-Upsell Trigger (COO Executes):**
- P1 customer buys 2+ templates → P2 discovery email day 3
- P2 customer week 2 delivery → P3 co-publish offer
- P3 subscriber (opt-in) → P1 discount offer day 1
