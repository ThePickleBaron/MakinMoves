## 3. ARTICLE TEMPLATES: <30 min per article

### Template 1: Product Review (Highest Intent)
**Time:** 25 minutes | **Search Intent:** Commercial-transactional | **CTR:** 8-15%

**Outline:**
- H1: "[Tool Name] Review 2026: Pros, Cons & Honest Pricing"
- H2: Quick Summary (2-3 sentences)
- H2: Who It's For (small businesses, freelancers, etc.)
- H2: Key Features Breakdown (3-4 features with screenshots)
- H2: Pricing (honest, detailed)
- H2: Pros & Cons (bulleted)
- H2: How It Compares to [Main Competitor]
- H2: Alternative Tools (2-3 quick mentions)
- H2: Is It Worth It? (honest conclusion)
- H2: FAQ (3-5 questions)
- CTA: Affiliate link to start free trial

**AI Speedup:** Use ChatGPT to generate feature list and FAQs; you write unique pros/cons from experience.

## 4. CONTENT FRAMEWORK: Write 10 Articles in <6 Hours

### Batch Content Strategy

**Hour 1-2: Keyword Research & Outlining**
- Use SEMrush/Ahrefs free trial: Find 20 keywords with difficulty <20, volume >100/month
- Create outline for each article (reusable across similar templates)
- Map keywords to article types (Product Review, Best Of, Comparison)

**Hour 2-3: AI Generation Phase**
- Paste outlines into Claude/ChatGPT with template prompt
- Generate 80% of article in bulk (3-4 articles at once)
- Export markdown versions

**Hour 3-4: Customization & Voice**
- Read through AI-generated drafts
- Add personal insights, examples, experience
- Rewrite intro/conclusion for brand voice
- Add unique angles (e.g., "I tested 12 tools, here's what worked")

**Hour 4-5: Link Building & Affiliate Links**
- Add affiliate links naturally within text (not just CTAs)
- Add 2-3 internal links to boost SEO authority
- Create FAQ section (use Google "People Also Ask" for 5 questions)

**Hour 5-6: Technical SEO & Publishing**
- Optimize meta descriptions, title tags
- Add alt text to images
- Set primary keyword in Rank Math/AIOSEO
- Publish with internal linking

### Example: 10-Article Plan (Realistic Timeline)

**Day 1 (Wednesday):**
- 5 Product Reviews (Answrr, Writesonic, GetResponse, Jasper, Copy.ai)
- Time per article: 25 min = 2 hours 5 min

**Day 2 (Thursday):**
- 3 Best Of lists ("Best AI Customer Service," "Best AI Content Tools," "Best AI Email Automation")
- Time per article: 20 min = 1 hour

**Day 3 (Friday):**
- 2 Industry-Specific ("Best AI Tools for Freelance Copywriters," "AI Automation for Small Law Firms")
- Time per article: 24 min = 48 min

**Total Time:** ~4 hours of actual work (plus 2 hours AI generation running in parallel)

## 10. KEY SUCCESS FACTORS

**What Wins in This Niche (2026):**
1. **Personal Experience** — "I tested 15 tools, here's what actually works" beats generic lists
2. **Industry Specificity** — "Best AI tools for yoga studios" converts 3x better than "Best AI tools"
3. **Honest Reviews** — Mention cons; affiliate readers trust transparency
4. **Quick ROI Calculation** — Show $$ savings; SMBs only care about ROI
5. **Speed of Ranking** — Long-tail keywords rank in 8-12 weeks (vs. 6+ months in other niches)

**What Fails:**
- Generic "Top 10 AI Tools" without specific use cases
- Writing 1 article/month (needs batch momentum)
- No internal linking (kills topical authority)
- Targeting high-difficulty keywords (>50 difficulty)
- Waiting for perfect before publishing (publish, iterate)
