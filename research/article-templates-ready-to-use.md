**Use these templates directly in Claude/ChatGPT with a prompt like:**

## HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

### For the COO (Content Creator):

1. **Pick a keyword** from your keyword research (e.g., "best AI customer service tools")
2. **Choose the matching template** based on keyword intent
3. **Copy the outline** into a new document
4. **Replace [PLACEHOLDERS]** with your actual data
5. **Paste into Claude/ChatGPT** with prompt:
   > "Write a detailed, conversion-focused affiliate blog article using this outline. Be honest about pros/cons but position the recommended tool as the best choice. Target small business owners. Make it compelling and helpful."
6. **Review AI output** for accuracy
7. **Add personal touches** (examples, experience, unique insights)
8. **Publish to WordPress** with internal links

### Time Breakdown (Per Article):
- Setup outline: 3 min
- Generate with AI: 8 min (parallel while you work on next outline)
- Review + customize: 10 min
- Add links + publish: 4 min
- **Total: ~25 min per article**

### Batch Workflow (10 Articles in 4 Hours):
- Hour 1: Create 10 outlines (all templates mixed)
- Hour 2: Feed all 10 outlines to AI generator (in parallel)
- Hour 3-4: Review, customize, and publish 10 articles

**Pro Tips:**
- Use different templates for each article (variation = better SEO ranking)
- Write 2 product reviews, 2 best-ofs, 2 industry-specific, 2 comparisons, 2 problem-solution
- Always include honest cons (builds trust = higher conversion)
- Link related articles (e.g., link "AI customer service comparison" to "best tools" list)
- Add affiliate links in multiple places (intro CTA, feature section, conclusion)
