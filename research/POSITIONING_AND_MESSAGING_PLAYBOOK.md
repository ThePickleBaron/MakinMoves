## COO Usage Guide

**When to use Track #1 (Cold Email):**
- Prospecting P2 clients
- Selling P1 to email lists
- Recruiting P3 partners

**When to use Track #2 (Social):**
- Twitter/LinkedIn posting
- Community engagement (Reddit, IH, HN)
- Retweet & respond

**When to use Track #3 (Announcement):**
- Product launch week
- Milestone announcements
- Building hype before sales

**Adapt these scripts:**
- Change founder name / company name / specific details
- Keep the structure (problem → solution → proof → CTA)
- Test different angles, track what converts

**DO NOT:**
- Promise guarantees you can't deliver
- Lie about features or timelines
- Oversell features (undersell, overdeliver)

**DO:**
- Personalize with their company name / use case
- Acknowledge real problems (they'll trust you)
- Keep CTAs specific (link, calendar, email response)
